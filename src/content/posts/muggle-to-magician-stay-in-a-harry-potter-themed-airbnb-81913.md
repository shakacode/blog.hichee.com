<p>Nestled just a few miles from Disney Parks, this beautiful property will excite the Potterhead in you with its Harry Potter-themed rooms and world-class amenities. </p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Make sure you bring the Sorting hat because this vacation home has rooms for all 4 houses (though everybody wants to be in Gryffindor, am I right?, and rooms dedicated to Hagrid, Dumbledore, and He-Who-Must-Not-be-Named Lord Voldemort. </p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>This property also features a pool with a Quidditch court to practice your skills after having fun at the parks. Among the other cool offerings are a fully equipped kitchen, access to Windsor Hills’ amenities, and the best one—a custom-built Nimbus 2001 above the bed in the Slytherin room.</p>
