<p>This is a small South Pacific island northwest of Tahiti in French Polynesia. Surrounded by sand-fringed motus (islets) and a turquoise lagoon protected by a coral reef, it’s known for its scuba diving. It&#8217;s also a popular luxury resort destination where guest bungalows are perched over the water on stilts. At the island&#8217;s center rises Mt. Otemanu, a 727m dormant volcano.</p>
