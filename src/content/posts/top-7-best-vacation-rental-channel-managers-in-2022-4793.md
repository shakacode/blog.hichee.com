<h2 class="wp-block-heading">What to Look For in a Vacation Rental Channel Manager</h2>



<p>Channel managers were originally built for hotels to be able to sell rooms on multiple booking sites at the same time. But as the vacation rental industry has exploded, there’s now an increasing number of companies offering channel managers for vacation rentals to meet the demand.</p>



<p>A vacation rental channel manager is software that will automatically distribute your property listings across multiple booking channels. Depending on the software, this means you can sync calendar availability, rates, and other property details.</p>



<p>There are dozens (hundreds, maybe?) of vacation rental channel managers you could choose from, and more popping up all the time. It can be a bit overwhelming, really.</p>



<p>https://tenor.com/embed/12708224?canonicalurl=https://www.getfloorspace.com/best-vacation-rental-channel-managers/</p>



<p>But not to worry. We’ve done the research for you, as part of our ongoing effort to be able to make the best, most informed software recommendations to our clients. Before we get into our reviews of the best channel managers for vacation rentals, here are a few things to keep in mind while deciding.</p>



<ul>
<li><strong>Supported channels</strong>&nbsp;– Do they offer native/API integrations (not just&nbsp;<a href="https://www.getfloorspace.com/vacation-rental-calendar-sync/">iCal calendar sync</a>) with Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com, and any other channels you want to list your properties on?</li>



<li><strong>What gets synced</strong>&nbsp;– Not all channel integrations are created equal. Some only sync calendar availability, while others can also sync booking details, rates, and property details.</li>



<li><strong>Value for the price</strong>&nbsp;– Everyone loves a good deal, but it’s important to keep in mind the time (read money) you can save if you choose the best platform for your needs, rather than the cheapest.</li>



<li><strong>Additional features</strong>&nbsp;– Most of these platforms offer channel management as just one feature in a suite of vacation rental management features—like a&nbsp;<a href="https://getfloorspace.com/vacation-rental-website-builders/">direct booking website builder</a>, owner commission statements and payouts, Quickbooks accounting integration, etc. All of our top picks offer automated messaging (excluding Syncbnb) and direct bookings.</li>



<li><strong>Number of properties</strong>&nbsp;– Some of these platforms can be overkill if you just have 1 or 2 properties. Others are perfectly suited to that.</li>
</ul>



<p>With these in mind, let’s get into our reviews and comparisons of the best vacation rental channel managers on the market today.</p>
