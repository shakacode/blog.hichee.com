<p>A Romantic Airbnb In California , Seamist Beach Cottage, nestled beside Bodega Bay, offers the ideal Valentine&#8217;s Day retreat for millennial couples. Situated atop a cliff with its own staircase leading to a secluded beach, this destination combines the thrill of adventure with the tranquility of a romantic escape.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Inside, it&#8217;s all cozy and quaint with a master bedroom and a loft with twin beds – perfect whether you&#8217;re planning a couple&#8217;s retreat or a small family adventure. The private beach is just the spot for those #RelationshipGoals moments, from dreamy picnics to exploring tide pools.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Highlighted in the &#8220;Staycation&#8221; TV series, this location transcends the typical Airbnb to offer an immersive experience. Embodying quintessential cottage ambiance, it boasts a captivating spiral staircase leading to a cozy loft, all adorned in nautical decor that epitomizes relaxation.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>The deck&#8217;s hot tub is where it&#8217;s at, offering insane ocean views – think perfect backdrop for sipping that Sonoma wine. Nights by the fire pit? Total win for cuddles and deep convos under the stars.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>For adventurous couples, fishing gear awaits your touch. Nestled in a renowned fishing haven, imagine grilling your fresh catch for a memorable meal. And for those seeking more thrills, horseback riding and surfing opportunities abound in the vicinity.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-what-guests-have-to-say-about-this-romantic-airbnb-in-california">What Guests have to say about this romantic Airbnb in California</h3>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>This vacation rental for lovebirds has 292 reviews on Airbnb, with an average rating of 4.67 out of 5 stars.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Here&#8217;s what Jennifer had to say about her stay</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>There&#8217;s so much to love about the Seamist cottage. The views are absolutely stunning. We spent many happy hours sitting in the hot tub, watching the surf and mist roll in. We also really appreciated the complimentary snacks and wine and how clean the cottage was on arrival. </p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Another nice surprise was the record player with a bunch of old records&#8211;our teen was fascinated. Blanca, who is the main point of contact during your stay, was lovely and a joy to work with. A few things to note as you&#8217;re deciding whether this is the right place for you: &#8211; As others have mentioned, there is a large construction project across the street which is due to end some time in 2023. </p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>The noise often woke us up at 6 AM, but I always travel with earplugs and everyone was able to fall back asleep so it wasn&#8217;t much of an issue for us. &#8211; The staircase to the loft is very narrow. It wasn&#8217;t easy getting getting up or down, esp for my tall teen. That area is probably best suited for a kid between the ages of 8 &#8211; 14, and preferably on the shorter side. Make sure you don&#8217;t need to take a lot of luggage up there. &#8211; </p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>The queen bed is very soft (memory foam I think?) and we actually ended up leaving a day early because it was hurting my back. However, this is highly personal. My husband found it okay. </p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Overall, the views and beach access are hard to match (especially with the location of the hot tub, I mean, wow!). If the above aren&#8217;t issues for you, then I predict you&#8217;ll very much enjoy your stay here. I think it would be especially nice for <mark>romantic</mark> getaway for a couple.</p>
