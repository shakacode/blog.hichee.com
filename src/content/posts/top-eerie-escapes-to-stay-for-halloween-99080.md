<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Looking for the perfect Halloween getaway that’s both spooky and luxurious? This historic studio apartment at Montauk Manor is the ultimate spot for your next ghostly retreat! Built in 1929, this registered landmark oozes old-world charm and eerie vibes, perfect for fans of classic horror films like The Shining or those looking to channel some serious Edgar Allan Poe energy.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Nestled inside this Tudor-style manor, your deluxe studio comes with all the modern amenities—cable TV, HBO, Wi-Fi, and a fully equipped kitchen. But the real draw? The moody atmosphere of the grand lobby, complete with a fireplace and plush lounge furniture, making it easy to imagine you’re in a haunted mansion straight out of Crimson Peak. After a day exploring nearby beaches, unwind in the indoor pool, jacuzzi, or sauna, perfect for soothing those post-spookfest chills.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Montauk Manor also offers seasonal access to an outdoor pool, tennis courts, and BBQ areas, ideal for gathering your ghoulish crew for some Halloween fun. Whether you’re into eerie elegance or just looking to experience Halloween in a place rich with history, this spot will give you all the thrills—no tricks necessary!</p>
