<p>If you haven’t received your Hogwarts letter yet, you can stop wondering if Uncle Vernon had taken them, and instead, Wingardium Leviosa (It&#8217;s leviOsa, not levioSA!) your way to this magical vacation home in Florida! Muggle Manor, where every Potterhead&#8217;s dream comes to life, is not just some cookie-cutter rental property—it’s an entire experience of immersing yourself into the magical world of Harry Potter, the boy who lived.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Be prepared for a jaw-dropping surprise as you enter the Great Room. It&#8217;s the ultimate hangout spot, decked out with magical artifacts behind charm-proof glass. Plush couches make it perfect for a Potter movie marathon or a wizard&#8217;s duel of board games. The bedrooms are straight out of a Potter fan&#8217;s dream, while The Hut gives off major Hagrid’s homey vibes.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Even He-Who-Shall-Not-Be-Named would be stoked about the pool. Redone in 2023, it&#8217;s a saltwater paradise, complete with a control module for that magical touch. It&#8217;s like having your own Prefects&#8217; Bathroom, but without the Moaning Myrtle drama.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>From the owner:<br /></strong>We&#8217;ve waited to receive our acceptance letter since reading the first book and seeing the first movie. Now we can say that dream has come true with Muggle Manor.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Muggle Manor is a complete themed home from the ceilings, with custom beams, to the custom concrete floor. No space was left untouched! Enjoy this 4 bedroom, 3 bath home with a private pool and spa. Centrally located just 26 miles from Universal Studios Hogsmeade and Diagon Alley, 12 miles to Disney, and 33 miles to MCO.</p>
