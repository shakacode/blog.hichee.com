<p>Check out this wonderful Airbnb Wedding Venue In Florida &#8211; How about tying the knot in style, just a skip away from the happiest place on earth? The Formosa Grand, a fancy 9-bedroom lakeside villa, is the perfect spot for a dreamy and intimate wedding bash. Tucked away in the gated Formosa Gardens community, this place boasts classy marble floors, a majestic staircase, and high-reaching ceilings.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Inside, you&#8217;ve got two chill-out zones, a top-notch open kitchen, a killer pool with panoramic views, and bedrooms like luxury havens with fancy sheets and private baths. And if that&#8217;s not enough, there&#8217;s a movie room, a full-body workout contraption, and a game room for added fun. Picture this: saying your vows on a 120ft balcony, with an infinity pool and an outdoor kitchen overlooking the lake—now that&#8217;s a backdrop that&#8217;ll make your special day even more special.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<h4 class="wp-block-heading">From the owner:</h4>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote is-layout-flow wp-block-quote-is-layout-flow"><p></p>
<p>Imagine a vacation in a 9 bedroom, 10 bath custom luxury lakefront pool villa. The Formosa Grand is located in the exclusive gated community of Formosa Gardens Estates and one of the closest communities to Disney (less than 10 minutes!!) No expense was spared in the gorgeous estate, making it an ideal setting for intimate weddings.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>You will be wowed as soon as you enter and take note of the polished marble floors, grand staircase, marbled pillars, 20ft high ceilings &amp; baby grand piano! The main living area has 2 separate seating areas with a large wall-mounted flat-screen TV for viewing when you aren&#8217;t taking in the views of the pool &amp; lake. As you enter the open-concept kitchen &amp; dining area, you will immediately notice the custom details and picturesque view overlooking the pool area &amp; lake. </p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>The kitchen is fully stocked if you decide to prepare your own meals &amp; a Keurig is provided for coffee lovers. Granite countertops &amp; large bar area with plenty of seating &amp; prep space help to make this space a chef&#8217;s dream &amp; perfect for entertaining. The dining table has seating for 10. There is also a wine cellar with a bar &amp; cooler adjacent to the kitchen. The laundry room is on the main floor with a large washing machine &amp; twin dryers for guest use.</p>
