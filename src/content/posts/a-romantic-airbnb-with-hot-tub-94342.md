<p>Aim for a Valentine&#8217;s Day unlike any other at Sol to Soul in the Mojave Desert. Condé Nast Traveler hails it as California&#8217;s coolest Airbnb. More than a place to stay, it&#8217;s an immersive journey.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Set in the creative heart of Gamma Gulch, this desert retreat spans 10 acres. It&#8217;s where raw wilderness meets modern elegance. A perfect blend.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>This gem, perfect for romantics, stands out with its white, modern design amid the desert&#8217;s grand boulders. It&#8217;s your slice of the Mojave, ideal for those sunrise and sunset selfies with your loved one. Nights here? They&#8217;re magical. Imagine unwinding in a hot tub under the stars, feeling like the only two people in the world. After all, that&#8217;s the essence of love, isn&#8217;t it?</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>It also features king-sized beds with all-natural mattresses and organic bedding that has bougee written all over it. Floor-to-ceiling sliders in the bedrooms mean you wake up to unreal desert views. It&#8217;s the kind of place where you can unplug, chill, and just be together.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>And if you want to add a bit of adventure, you&#8217;re in the perfect spot. You&#8217;re close to all the cool desert spots but in a setting that feels like your own secret world.</p>
<p></p>

<p>We invite you to experience the otherworldly at Sol to Soul, a private 10-acre sanctuary in the starkly, beautiful Mojave Desert. Here, you will reclaim your sense of awe and magic and leave more inspired + spellbound by life than when you arrived. Distinguished by Condé Nast Traveler December 2021 Best Airbnb in California</p>
<p></p>

<p>We have the honor of being distinguished by Condé Nast Traveler as the coolest Airbnb in the entire state of California (The Coolest Airbnb in Every State).</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>The enchantment begins the moment you turn off the pavement into the exclusive area of Gamma Gulch. A private, maintained dirt road takes you through fields of Joshua Trees and past massive outcroppings of granite boulders that the Joshua Tree National Park is known for.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Turning into the driveway there is an otherworldly sense of arrival with Sol&#8217;s iconic, all white, modern structure set in the wild terrain of epic boulders. The crowning feature is the massive whale-shaped rock that towers above Sol, as if wanting to see and greet each guest. A celebrity in its own right &#8211; and one of the most photographed boulders in the high-desert &#8211; we have unoriginally but affectionately named it Whale Rock.</p>
<p></p>

There’s bbq and live music 15-minutes away at world-famous Pappy &amp; Harriet’s Pioneertown Palace; a sound bath at The Integratron – built by a UFO advocate; antiquing in Yucca; and custom guided hikes, rock-climbing lessons and even a night with your own personal astronomer in JTNP.</p>
