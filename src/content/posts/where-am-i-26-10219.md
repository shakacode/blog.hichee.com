<p>This place is a major economic center and home to mosques, palaces, and gardens. The medina is a densely packed, walled medieval city dating to the Berber Empire, with mazelike alleys where thriving souks (marketplaces) sell traditional textiles, pottery, and jewelry. A symbol of the city, visible for miles, is the Moorish minaret of the 12th-century Koutoubia Mosque.</p>
