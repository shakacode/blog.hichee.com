<p>It&#8217;s a city at the famous waterfalls of the same name, linked with the U.S. by the Rainbow Bridge. Its site on the Niagara River&#8217;s western shore overlooks the Horseshoe Falls, the cascades&#8217; most expansive section. Elevators take visitors to a lower, wetter vantage point behind the falls. A cliffside park features a promenade alongside 520-ft.-high Skylon Tower with an observation deck.</p>
