<p>As you probably know, many listing sites charge a commission, or as they call it, a service fee.<br />These fees can range from 8 to a whopping 25 percent.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>So, if you&#8217;re paying a base rate of $100 a night you could be paying $125 a night, just because you booked the property through one of the big listing companies.</p>
<p>Owners or hosts don&#8217;t charge commission so if you can find the owners or hosts own website and you book directly with them, you could be saving a lot of money.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>In the super short video below, I save $527 in just one minute, just by checking if the listing on Hichee has a verified owner.</p>
