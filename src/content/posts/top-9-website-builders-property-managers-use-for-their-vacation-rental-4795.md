<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="so-is-lodgify-worth-it">So, is Lodgify worth it?</h3>



<p>Lodgify is worth it if you (or any homeowner or property managers who) just want a straightforward, all-in-one platform for managing their property.</p>



<p>They cost $17-$70 per property per month. Still, if you can afford it, you essentially get a tightly integrated system for making a website, managing your property, and customers, and listing (and syncing) on various short-term vacation rental marketplaces.</p>



<p>Lodgify is not so great, however, if you care deeply about customizing the website and user experience for your customer. They don&#8217;t (and probably can&#8217;t afford to) allow customization on your website.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="guesty-pricing">Guesty Pricing</h3>



<p>Guesty takes a cut, an agreed-upon percentage (2-5%) of all bookings. Steep, I know. The pricing structure is based on the number of listings managed, and the different features selected.</p>



<p>There&#8217;s also a&nbsp;<a href="https://www.getapp.com/hospitality-travel-software/a/guesty/reviews/73fe962bb5/">$1000 onboarding fee</a>, where you get concierge assistance from Guesty account managers in the initial setup. What&#8217;s more, is that they&#8217;ll charge a fee for any canceled bookings to your rental.</p>



<p>As you can see all these costs do add up. But at least there are some cost savings during the offseason — they won&#8217;t charge you if there isn&#8217;t any booking.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="is-guesty-worth-it-despite-the-high-cost">Is Guesty worth it despite the high cost?</h3>



<p>The ROI for using Guesty is only compelling if you use at least several of the features (i.e. Not ideal for single-feature usage).</p>



<p>The fact that Guesty requires at least 5 properties to get on board means they&#8217;re catered for property management companies.</p>



<p>It&#8217;s a powerful software no less, and Guesty can save property management companies a lot of time and cost, particularly if you have listings on multiple online travel agencies (OTAs) and want to be able to capture direct bookings.</p>



<p>In the end, Guesty makes sense&nbsp;<strong>if you intend to scale your rental operation.&nbsp;</strong>Despite the high cost, it will probably pay off in the long run.</p>
