<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Introduction to smart home devices for vacation rentals</strong></h2>



<p>Are you a smart host looking to upgrade your vacation rental property with cutting-edge technology? Look no further than smart home devices! These innovative devices have revolutionized how we live, work, and play, and now they can transform your vacation rental into a smart, connected space that will delight guests and boost your bottom line.</p>



<p>Smart home devices are a broad category of gadgets that use internet connectivity and artificial intelligence to automate and optimize various aspects of your home. From smart thermostats that learn your temperature preferences to smart locks that let you remotely control access, these devices offer a wide range of benefits for hosts and guests. </p>



<p>Some other popular examples of smart devices include smart locks smart lighting, smart speakers, smart cameras, and smart appliances.</p>



<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img decoding="async" width="1024" height="456" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Screenshot-2023-03-21-at-11.00.35-1024x456.png" alt="Some other popular examples of smart devices include smart locks smart lighting, smart speakers, smart cameras, and smart appliances." class="wp-image-17681" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Screenshot-2023-03-21-at-11.00.35-1024x456.png 1024w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Screenshot-2023-03-21-at-11.00.35-300x134.png 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Screenshot-2023-03-21-at-11.00.35-768x342.png 768w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Screenshot-2023-03-21-at-11.00.35-1536x684.png 1536w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Screenshot-2023-03-21-at-11.00.35.png 1734w" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" /></figure>



<p>By installing smart home devices in your vacation rental, you can offer guests a seamless, high-tech experience that will make their stay more enjoyable and memorable. For example, smart thermostats can adjust the temperature based on guest occupancy and weather patterns, while smart lighting can create a cozy, welcoming atmosphere that enhances relaxation and sleep. Additionally, smart locks can streamline check-in and check-out processes, while smart cameras can provide added security and peace of mind for hosts and guests.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Benefits of incorporating smart home devices</strong></h2>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Choosing the best ecosystem for your smart choices</strong></h2>



<p>Even though you may be planning to add smart devices to your home, you should be aware that not all devices are smart enough to connect to each other. The whole idea of introducing smart devices to your home is to make your life, and in this case, running your short-term rental business, more efficient.</p>



<p>You don’t want to end up with 10 different apps on your phone, and you certainly don’t want to find yourself being frustrated when it comes to devices not wanting to play together,<br><br>Time spent looking into connectivity and ease of setup before spending your hard-earned cash is time well spent.</p>



<figure class="wp-block-image size-full is-resized"><img decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Screenshot-2023-03-21-at-12.01.30.png" alt="Matter" class="wp-image-17683" width="100" height="100"/></figure>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Matter</strong></h2>



<p>Saying that&#8230; late last year, we saw the introduction of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matter_(standard)" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Matter</a>. <br>It&#8217;s a connectivity standard for smart home and Internet of Things devices, which aims to improve their compatibility and security. The standard is royalty-free, though developers and manufacturers incur certification costs.<br><br>If you are just starting your journey into turning your short-term rental into a smart home, make sure the devices you purchase have the Matter logo on the box. This will save you a lot of pain as you set up your network of devices.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Smart locks</h2>



<p>A smart lock is possibly one of the most important parts of a smart vacation rental home. Especially if it&#8217;s paired with a smart doorbell.</p>



<p>You can assign special access privileges to guests, cleaners, or maintenance staff. Some will even let you know if the door has been left unlocked or ajar.<br><br>It doesn&#8217;t stop there either; you can link your smart lock to other smart devices. <h2 class="wp-block-heading has-text-color" style="color:#f60b0b">Important note</h2>



<p>The big listing sites require that you indicate the presence of all security cameras or other recording devices in or around a property &#8211; even if they&#8217;re not turned on or hooked up.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><br><strong>Airbnb</strong> (<a href="https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/2914#:~:text=Airbnb%20prohibits%20security%20cameras%20or,turned%20on%20or%20hooked%20up" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Full details and rules here</a>)</h3>



<ol>
<li>Go to&nbsp;<a href="https://www.airbnb.com/hosting/listings" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Listings</a>&nbsp;and click or tap on the listing you want to edit</li>



<li>In&nbsp;<strong>Listing details</strong>, scroll down to&nbsp;<strong>Things guests should know</strong>&nbsp;and click or tap&nbsp;<strong>Edit</strong></li>



<li>Under&nbsp;<strong>Safety Devices</strong>, check the box for&nbsp;<strong>Security cameras/audio recording devices</strong></li>



<li>Click or tap&nbsp;<strong>Edit</strong>&nbsp;and describe each device, its location, and if it will be off or on (you’ll also be asked to confirm some details about your listing)</li>
</ol>

<p>Surveillance devices, including security cameras and smart doorbells (which may record audio), are permitted if they follow these rules:</p>



<ul>
<li>Surveillance devices should only be used for security purposes.</li>



<li>Location and coverage of devices must be disclosed on the property description page (see&nbsp;<a href="https://help.vrbo.com/articles/How-do-I-add-or-edit-a-changeover-date" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Edit your property listing</a>&nbsp;for step-by-step instructions). <li>Areas where guests have a reasonable expectation of privacy, such as the bathroom or bedroom, should not be under surveillance.</li>



<li>Reasonable measures should be taken to limit access to surveillance data.</li>



<li>Surveillance data should be deleted when no longer needed.</li>
