<p>Known for its world-famous surf breaks, incredible wildlife, and ancient rainforests. Built by returned soldiers between 1919 and 1932 and dedicated to soldiers killed during World War I, the road is the world&#8217;s largest war memorial. Winding through varying terrain along the coast and providing access to several prominent landmarks, including the Twelve Apostles limestone stack formations, the road is an important tourist attraction in the region.</p>
