<p>What’s better than a Harry Potter-themed vacation home? A bunch of Potterheads to enjoy it with! This Hogwarts-style team space in Downtown St. Louis is the perfect place for groups to immerse themselves in the wizarding world of Harry Potter (we hope Lord Voldemort doesn’t show up!). This rental property can accommodate up to 24 people in its 6 spacious rooms representing the Gryffindor, Slytherin, Hufflepuff, and Ravenclaw houses, the Professors’ room, and an entertainment room. </p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>It also has a fully-equipped kitchen and 4 ensuite bathrooms, which hopefully aren’t haunted by Moaning Myrtle. Either way, your group will surely have a spectacular time creating magical memories!</p>

<p></p>
<p><strong>From the owner:</strong><br />Our 2,000 sq ft Hogwarts-style team space is excellent for wedding parties and groups attending conventions, especially a group of bridesmaids or groomsmen!</p>
