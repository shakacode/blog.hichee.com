<p>Backpacking <a href="https://www.nomadicmatt.com/travel-guides/europe-travel-tips/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Europe</a> is a rite of passage. With a well-worn travel trail, a huge variety of amazing destinations, and tons of hostels, Europe is a fun, safe, and easy place to travel. It’s where I first started backpacking over 15 years ago, and I still make sure to visit the continent every year.</p>
<p>Since budget travel is a huge part of Europe’s culture (and since many countries in Europe are expensive), hostels are pretty much everywhere. They’re the go-to accommodation choice for younger travelers (though many older travelers use them too).</p>
<p>But even affordable hostels add up if you’re traveling for weeks or months at a time.</p>
<p>Fortunately, there’s a new way to save on hostels around Europe: <a href="https://www.hostelpass.co/hostel-card/?ref=mattkepnes4" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">HostelPass</a>.</p>
<p>HostelPass is a membership program that provides discounts on select hostels and activities in Europe.</p>
<p>But is it worth it? Will you actually save money using it?</p>
<p>In this post, I’ll explain everything you need to know about HostelPass to help you decide if it’s right for your next trip to Europe.</p>

<h3><span id="how-does-hostelpass-work">How Does HostelPass Work?</span></h3>
<p>To sign up for a HostelPass membership, simply visit <a href="https://www.hostelpass.co/hostel-card/?ref=mattkepnes4" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">hostelpass.co</a> and purchase the annual membership. Your pass is valid for a year from the date of purchase, so be sure to time it with when you’ll actually start booking hostels (not months and months beforehand). You’ll want it to cover booking your hostels as well as when you’re actually on your trip.</p>
<p><img decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/hostelpassreview4b.png" width="675" height="395" alt="Purchase page for HostelPass" /></p>

<p>Once you do that, you can then start booking accommodation and activities via the HostelPass website. </p>
<p>When you see an activity or hostel that you want to book, just select it and you’ll be presented with the booking link. Follow that link and place your booking. That’s it!<br /> </p>
<h3><span id="what-is-included-in-hostelpass">What is Included in HostelPass?</span></h3>
<p>There are currently over 100 discounts available on <a href="https://www.hostelpass.co/hostel-card/?ref=mattkepnes4" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">HostelPass</a>. Most of these are hostels, with discounts usually ranging from 10-20% off. Some of these hostels also include other perks, like free breakfast or a free welcome drink.</p>
<p>There are also discounts on experiences, activities, and museums too, such as 15% off a Harry Potter walking tour in Edinburgh, 10% off canal cruises in Amsterdam, and 12% off a food tour in Paris.</p>

<h3><span id="is-hostelpass-actually-worth-it">Is HostelPass ACTUALLY Worth It?</span></h3>
<p>If you’re heading to Europe and have the flexibility to plan your trip around the hostels and activities available on HostelPass, then you can easily make your money back (and then some). </p>
<p>You will roughly need to stay at 5-7 discounted hostels to get your money back (depending on the price per night). Once you throw some discounted tours and activities in there though, it becomes much more attainable and the savings much greater.</p>
<p>If you don’t have your full travel itinerary planned but will be in Europe for a few months, the pass is likely worth getting as you can probably make your money back (while also enjoying some nice perks like free breakfast or free drinks).</p>
<p>If you’re only heading to Europe for a week or two, then chances are you won’t recoup the cost of a pass. But you’ll want to do the math to be sure.<br /> </p>
<h3><span id="hostelpass-frequently-asked-questions">HostelPass Frequently Asked Questions</span></h3>
<p><strong>Can I share the pass with a friend?</strong><br />Multiple travelers can’t share a single HostelPass, however, at checkout you can add users to your purchase. For example, if you’re traveling with three friends you can put all four of you on a single pass. You’ll need to pay for four passes, but all four passes will be on a single account (instead of having four different accounts) so everyone will get the perks and discounts as you travel.</p>
<p>You can also gift a pass in case you know a traveler who would appreciate it or if a friend needs an extra incentive to join you on a trip. Their pass will become valid from the day they first use it, not the day you purchased it. </p>
<p><strong>Do I need to book on HostelPass’ site?</strong><br />Once you have your pass and are ready to book, you can get the booking links for each discount on HostelPass’ site. They’ll send you a specific link to use that will allow you to enjoy your discount.</p>
<p>If you don’t book via HostelPass, you won’t get the discount.</p>
<p><strong>Is there an age limit to use HostelPass?</strong><br />Travelers need to be at least 18 years of age to get a HostelPass membership.</p>
<p><strong>How much does HostelPass cost?</strong><br />An annual HostelPass membership is 29.99 EUR per person. </p>
