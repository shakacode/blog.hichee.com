<p>How to get – 💺 The Best Airline Seats in Economy Class is really not that difficult, with a little planning.</p>
<p>Yes, you can select your 💺 airline seat, well before your departure date. Here, are just a few tips regarding economy seat selection and finding that best possible seat, well before you leave.</p>
<p>Keep in mind though, most major airlines do charge an additional fee for this service, as with most everything else these days.</p>
<p>My personal opinion and especially for those taking dreaded ✈ long-haul flights in economy class, the extra cost for a good seat, may be well worth it! 😀</p>
<p><strong>Sitting where you want and being comfortable in 💺 airline seats, can make a huge difference in your overall flight experience.</strong></p>

<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Having_To_Fly_In_Economy_Class"></span>Having To Fly In Economy Class<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Inside-of-Airplane.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-0" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Inside-of-Airplane.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11886" alt="Inside of Airplane" width="450" height="338" data-sizes="(max-width: 450px) 100vw, 450px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Inside-of-Airplane.jpg 450w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Inside-of-Airplane-300x225-1.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p>If I’m flying economy class which is the case the majority of the time and my flight is over 3 hours, I want to be sure I am sitting where I would most like to be. I want to feel and be as comfortable as I possibly can.</p>
<p>I think most people feel the same way when flying, whatever class they have chosen.</p>
<p>If you can select your airline seats without charge prior to your flight, that’s great!</p>
<p>But did you know, that it’s still no guarantee that you will end up with those seats, when you arrive at the airport and check in either.</p>
<p>As well, most airlines are now charge for advanced seat selection, along with everything else for economy passengers these days.:(</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Flight-Departures-Display.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-1" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Flight-Departures-Display-600x301-1.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-29346" alt="Flight Departures Display" width="600" height="301" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Flight-Departures-Display-600x301-1.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Flight-Departures-Display-300x151-1.jpg 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Flight-Departures-Display.jpg 757w" /></a></p>
<p>Flights times do regularly change prior to departure and when this happens, your seats could go as well.  You may be stuck having to be re-assigned with new seats at the airport, on the day of departure.</p>
<p>That could be very bad news, especially if you’re taking a long haul flight!</p>
<p>Getting bumped to a new flight could result in choosing what’s left for that flight. On a positive note and as an example, the 🍁 Canadian Government for example, has buckled down on carriers bumping and overbooking flights.</p>
<p>📖 Read more about this on my latest post on 🍁 <a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/new-canada-air-passenger-regulations/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><em>New Canada Air Passenger Regulations</em></a>.</p>
<p>So, be sure to check online or with the airline prior to leaving, especially if you are fussy with seat assignments like I am.</p>
<p>Unless you purchased those – Premium Economy, Business Class or First Class seats up front that we all drool over, here are a few tips in helping you select better 💺 airline seats for your next flight.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Choosing_The_Best_Airline_Seats_For_Those_Longer_Flights"></span>Choosing The Best Airline Seats For Those Longer Flights<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Best-Economy-Airline-Seats.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-2" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Best-Economy-Airline-Seats-600x338-1.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-29347" alt="Best Economy Airline Seats" width="600" height="338" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Best-Economy-Airline-Seats-600x338-1.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Best-Economy-Airline-Seats-300x169-1.jpg 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Best-Economy-Airline-Seats.jpg 620w" /></a></p>
<p>Everyone has their own personal preference as to whether they prefer a seat located in the …</p>
<p><center><i><b>Window … Middle … Isle</b></i></center> </p>
<p>As well, some prefer their seat to be more <em><strong>forward</strong></em>, which I definitely prefer, in the<em><strong> middle</strong></em> or towards the<em><strong> rear</strong></em> of the aircraft.</p>
<p>It really is based upon personal preference.</p>
<p>Once you have made these choices, let’s have a quick look at the {pros and cons,} of each option.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Getting_The_Best_Airline_Seating_In_Economy_Class"></span>Getting The Best Airline Seating In Economy Class<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><em>Get The Best Airline Seats That Are Right For You</em></p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/P1011148-640x350-1.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-3" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/P1011148-640x350-1.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-8106 " title alt="Best Airline Seats" width="600" height="328" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/P1011148-640x350-1.jpg 640w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/P1011148-640x350-300x164-1.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p>Without a doubt, the most sought after seats in {Economy} are the <em><strong>Exit Row</strong></em> seats.</p>
<p>These seats do come with restrictions and with a higher extra surcharge with most major airlines. There are age restrictions and you must be prepared to assist with crew in the event of emergency, opening of the exit doors.</p>
<p>As there are no seats directly in front of you, tray tables are usually mounted in the armrests and some TV screens as well. This makes the armrests permanently fixed in their position.</p>
<p>Just a small inconvenience to put up with for that extra space and leg room in front of you.</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/No-Recline-Airplane-Seat.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-4" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/No-Recline-Airplane-Seat-600x387-1.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-29348" alt="No Recline Airplane Seat" width="600" height="387" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/No-Recline-Airplane-Seat-600x387-1.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/No-Recline-Airplane-Seat-300x193-1.jpg 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/No-Recline-Airplane-Seat-768x495-1.jpg 768w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/No-Recline-Airplane-Seat.jpg 980w" /></a></p>
<p>You should also note and something we discovered on a past flight, that your seat {may not recline} and is fixed in the upright position.</p>
<p>Bummer for a long flight! 🙁</p>
<p><em><strong>Window</strong></em> seats are also preferred by many people. You have easy viewing access outside and if you are planning on 😴 sleeping a fair bit, the window seat is offers more privacy.</p>
<p>You can rest your pillow against the wall next to you and you get the least amount of – disruption.</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/1.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-5" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/1-768x1024-1.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-8108" title alt="Best Economy Airline Seats" width="301" height="402" data-sizes="(max-width: 301px) 100vw, 301px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/1-768x1024-1.jpg 768w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/1-225x300-1.jpg 225w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/1-900x1200-1.jpg 900w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/1.jpg 1536w" /></a></p>
<p>Certainly these are nice features, especially on longer and more scenic flights. The downside is, unless you’re sitting next to friends or family, this seat can be difficult at times.</p>
<p>Every time you wish to get up from your seat, you always have to interrupt passengers who are seated next to you.</p>
<p>Oh well, you win some and you lose some! 🙂</p>
<p>I always try to select an isle seat for all my flights, both short and long.</p>
<p> I like to get up and out of my seat to stretch often, particularly on long haul flights. Be sure to check out my past article on 🚶 <a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/stretching-during-air-travel/"><em>Stretching During Air Travel</em></a>.</p>
<p>It’s so important to get up, 🚶 move around and stretch during those long flights!</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Each_Aircraft_Have_Different_Seat_Configurations"></span>Each Aircraft Have Different Seat Configurations<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><em>Selecting Your Seats Before Departure</em></p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Insurance.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-6" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Insurance.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-8107 " title alt=" Best Economy Airline Seats" width="599" height="449" data-sizes="(max-width: 599px) 100vw, 599px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Insurance.jpg 640w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Insurance-300x225-1.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p>Another often overlooked inconvenience and worthy of sharing, is that your leg room may be somewhat restricted on certain types of ✈ aircraft. Towards the front of the plane, the walls often curve inward, reducing your feet space for window seating.</p>
<p>As well and more often then not, there seems to be a small box on the floor in front of you, that restricts your foot space.</p>
<p>I believe it may be for the electronics and televisions mounted in the seat backs? ❓</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airplane-Isle-Seat.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-7" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airplane-Isle-Seat-600x400-1.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-29349" alt="Airplane Isle Seat" width="600" height="400" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airplane-Isle-Seat-600x400-1.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airplane-Isle-Seat-300x200-1.jpg 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airplane-Isle-Seat.jpg 634w" /></a></p>
<p><em><strong>Isle</strong></em> seats are my favorite on any flight unlike my daughter pictured here, who prefers a window view seat.</p>
<p>I like the open space next to me and the ease of getting in and out of my seat, any time I wish. But and like other seats, they do come with inconveniences as well.</p>
<p>Such as; people bumping you as they walk up and down the aisle. This happens particularly when you’re trying to have that quick nap, and are just about to nod off! 😕</p>
<p><strong>Also because of passengers and flight crew continually walking past you, expect disruptions throughout the flight.</strong></p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="The_Middle_Seat_Is_The_Least_Preferred"></span>The Middle Seat Is The Least Preferred<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/best-airline-seats/middle-airplane-seats/" rel="attachment wp-att-16658"><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Middle-Airplane-Seats.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-16658" title="Middle Airplane Seats" alt="Middle Airplane Seats" width="602" height="246" data-sizes="(max-width: 602px) 100vw, 602px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Middle-Airplane-Seats.jpg 1011w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Middle-Airplane-Seats-300x123-1.jpg 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Middle-Airplane-Seats-768x314-1.jpg 768w" /></a></p>
<p>You are basically the <em><strong>🐖 Pig in the Middle</strong></em> and there is no way out!</p>
<p>Who gets those armrests on either side of you? ❓</p>

<p>Some things in that post may come as quite a surprise for many! 🙂</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Final-Thoughts-Banner.png" data-rel="lightbox-image-8" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Final-Thoughts-Banner.png" decoding="async" loading="lazy" alt="Final Thoughts Banner" width="500" height="155" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-33185" data-sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Final-Thoughts-Banner.png 500w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Final-Thoughts-Banner-300x93-1.png 300w" /></a></p>
<p>As you can see, there are many factors to consider when choosing a seat in economy class. From legroom space, toilet and galley locations, to seat pitch.</p>
<p>All are things to consider when reserving your seats if you choose to do so.</p>
<p>Especially, if you are 🕵 picky about where you sit, like me!</p>
