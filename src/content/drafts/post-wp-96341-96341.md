<p>I think <a href="https://www.nomadicmatt.com/travel-guides/austria-travel-guide/vienna/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Vienna</a> is one of the most beautiful cities in <a href="https://www.nomadicmatt.com/travel-guides/europe-travel-tips/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Europe</a>. The historic core of the city is a massive UNESCO Heritage Site brimming with incredible museums, beautiful galleries, and sprawling Baroque palaces.</p>
<p>The city is a history buff’s dream and <a href="https://www.nomadicmatt.com/travel-blogs/3-day-vienna-itinerary/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">there’s a lot more to see and do here</a>. There’s an incredible café culture, a nearby wine region, tons of amazing eateries, awesome live music (including world-class opera), and much more.</p>
<p>I’ve been coming here for years and I never get tired of visiting. (I love the city so much I used to run tours here!)</p>
<p>Naturally, I’ve stayed at countless hotels during my visits. Here’s my list of the best hotels in Vienna:</p>
<h3>1. Hotel-Pension Wild</h3>
<p><img decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/viennahotel1.jpeg" width="675" height="395" alt="A hotel room with blue carpet at the Hotel-Pension Wild in Vienna, Austria" /></p>
<p><noscript><img decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/viennahotel1.jpeg" width="675" height="395" alt="A hotel room with blue carpet at the Hotel-Pension Wild in Vienna, Austria" /></noscript><br />This two-star hotel is one of the few affordable lodgings in the city center. It has simple but bright rooms that feature comfortable beds, plenty of space, and large windows that let in a lot of natural light. The design is a bit dated (the carpeted rooms don’t look particularly stylish) but everything is clean and functional. Rooms include basic amenities like a flatscreen TV, desk, and free Wi-Fi. The bathrooms are pretty small, but they’re clean and the showers have good water pressure. The hotel offers a hearty continental breakfast and the owner is friendly and welcoming. If you’re on a tight budget, there are small, no-frills single rooms with a shared bathroom available as well.<br />

<h3>2. Hotel Domizil</h3>
<p><img decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/viennahotel2.jpeg" width="675" height="395" alt="A charming hotel room with antique furniture at Hotel Domizil in Vienna, Austria" /></p>
<p><noscript><img decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/viennahotel2.jpeg" width="675" height="395" alt="A charming hotel room with antique furniture at Hotel Domizil in Vienna, Austria" /></noscript><br />This boutique four-star hotel has small, clean rooms with décor that makes you feel like you’re back in Vienna’s imperial past. The rooms have plenty of light and lots of antique touches, such as wooden desks and tables, and upholstered armchairs. Rooms also have free Wi-Fi, a flatscreen TV, and a coffee/tea maker. The bathrooms, while not particularly fashionable (the tiles are kind of ugly), are very spacious and the showers have excellent water pressure. The breakfast, which can be included in the price, has tons of variety, including lots of different fresh breads and cheeses.</p>
<p>Located in the city center, I think this is one of the best value places to stay if you want to be in the center of it all.</p>
<p><center><a class="bookhostel" href="https://www.booking.com/hotel/at/domizil.en.html?aid=1383362&amp;no_rooms=1&amp;group_adults=2" rel="nofollow">Book here!</a></center><br /> </p>
<h3>3. Hotel Mozart</h3>
<p><img decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/viennahotel3.jpeg" width="675" height="395" alt="A spacious and airy hotel room at Hotel Mozart in Vienna, Austria" /></p>
<p><noscript><img decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/viennahotel3.jpeg" width="675" height="395" alt="A spacious and airy hotel room at Hotel Mozart in Vienna, Austria" /></noscript><br />One of the few hotels in the Rossau neighborhood (just northwest of the city center), this budget-friendly three-star hotel boasts large rooms with lots of natural light. Rooms are spacious and decorated in light colors and feature hardwood or parquet floors. There are lots of other wooden touches too, such as desks/tables, and large wooden headboards. Free Wi-Fi is included, as is a flatscreen TV and AC (a must if you’re visiting in the summer). Some rooms include coffee machines. The hotel boasts a filling breakfast spread each morning with lots of fresh fruit and pastries. There’s also a bar on-site and the staff are always happy to share their tips and advice too.<br />
<center><a class="bookhostel" href="https://www.booking.com/hotel/at/mozart-wien.en.html?aid=1383362&amp;no_rooms=1&amp;group_adults=2" rel="nofollow">Book here!</a></center><br /> </p>
<h3>4. Hotel Johann Strauss</h3>
<p><img decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/viennahotel4.jpeg" width="675" height="395" alt="A large, comfy bed with artwork above it at Hotel Johann Strauss in Vienna, Austria" /></p>
<p><noscript><img decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/viennahotel4.jpeg" width="675" height="395" alt="A large, comfy bed with artwork above it at Hotel Johann Strauss in Vienna, Austria" /></noscript><br />Located in the Wieden neighborhood, this stylish four-star hotel is named after the eponymous 19th-century Austrian composer. The hotel is set in a historic Art Nouveau building that’s been entirely renovated, with rooms that have lots of natural light and a soft, welcoming color palette. Rooms include a flatscreen TV, minibar, coffee/tea maker, desk, and sofa. I really like that there’s a lot of art around the property, including in the rooms (naturally, much of the art is music centered). The tiled bathrooms are huge, with lots of light and great water pressure. I especially like that the breakfast buffet is enormous and features a lot of variety. The staff is exceptionally friendly too and happy to help you make the most of your stay.<br />
<center><a class="bookhostel" href="https://www.booking.com/hotel/at/hotjohannstrausswien.en.html?aid=1383362&amp;no_rooms=1&amp;group_adults=2" rel="nofollow">Book here!</a></center><br /> </p>
<h3>5. Hotel Am Konzerthaus Vienna</h3>
<p><img decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/viennahotel5.jpeg" width="675" height="395" alt="A sleek, modern hotel room at Hotel Am Konserthaus in Vienna, Austria" /></p>
<p><noscript><img decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/viennahotel5.jpeg" width="675" height="395" alt="A sleek, modern hotel room at Hotel Am Konserthaus in Vienna, Austria" /></noscript><br />Located in Landstrasse near the iconic Belvedere Palace, this luxe four-star hotel feels more like a five-star property. It has a chic lobby and large, bright rooms with super comfy beds. Everything is fashionable and trendy, with lots of rich colors from a darker palette. The large bathrooms are bedecked with dark tiles and are well lit, featuring plush bathrobes and relaxing rain showers. Rooms also include a flatscreen TV, desk, and coffee/tea maker. The breakfast buffet is huge and has a lot of options, but I especially love that the on-site restaurant has a Michelin star (it does amazing modern takes on traditional Austrian dishes). </p>
<p>The hotel is a perfect choice for travelers who want some luxury without breaking the bank. </p>
<p><center><a class="bookhostel" href="https://www.booking.com/hotel/at/hotel-am-konzerthaus.en.html?aid=1383362&amp;no_rooms=1&amp;group_adults=2" rel="nofollow">Book here!</a></center><br /> </p>
<h3>6. The Ritz-Carlton Vienna</h3>
<p><img decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/viennahotel6.jpeg" width="675" height="395" alt="A huge, spacious hotel room at the Ritz in Vienna, Austria" /></p>
<p><noscript><img decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/viennahotel6.jpeg" width="675" height="395" alt="A huge, spacious hotel room at the Ritz in Vienna, Austria" /></noscript><br />This is arguably the fanciest hotel in town. A five-star property right in the heart of the city, this hotel is spread across four historic palaces (yes, actual palaces). While the hotel feels palatial, it has understated décor with a chic minimalist design (think lots of white space with touches of color or artwork). The marble bathrooms are huge and the showers have perfect water pressure. The rooms are also massive and have big, comfy beds (as well as desks, AC, sofas, and electric kettles). </p>
<p>There’s also a gigantic breakfast buffet offered each morning and a free glass of champagne when you check in. The hotel also has a pool that plays music underwater, a fitness center, sauna, steam room, and three different spas on-site. In short, it’s the pinnacle of luxury in the city and the best choice for travelers looking to splurge on an elegant stay.</p>
<p><center><a class="bookhostel" href="https://www.booking.com/hotel/at/the-ritz-carlton-vienna.en.html?aid=1383362&amp;no_rooms=1&amp;group_adults=2" rel="nofollow">Book here!</a></center><br />
<center>***</center></p>
<p><a href="https://www.nomadicmatt.com/travel-guides/austria-travel-guide/vienna/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Vienna</a> is a beautiful city. I always love coming here. While it can be expensive, by staying in one of the hotels above, you’ll be sure to get great value, whether you’re looking to keep to a budget or splash out on a luxury stay.</p>
