<div id="ez-toc-container" class="ez-toc-v2_0_45_1 counter-hierarchy ez-toc-counter ez-toc-light-blue ez-toc-container-direction">

<p><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/writer.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" alt="Writer at Desk" width="150" height="143" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9443" />When traveling to new destinations around the world, many of us will end up visiting the most popular attractions in that region. My guest author has provided an interesting post on; 🏯 Tourist Attractions – What They Don’t Tell You, that I thought would be fun to share with you.<br />
Robert 🙂</p>
<p>When creating a 📝 travel itinerary to a new destination, most of us usually want to add some of the areas popular tourist attractions to our list. After all, they are popular and iconic for a reason – right? ❓</p>
<p>Well, not always!</p>
<p>Places like these are usually called <em>⛲ “Tourist Traps”</em> for a reason. Oftentimes, your expectations will exceed your reality when it comes to experiencing these attractions.</p>
<p>
They are often heavily crowded and oftentimes, less impressive than you had expected.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Go_Early_To_Beat_The_Crowds"></span>Go Early To Beat The Crowds<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><strong>Tourist Attractions Tip # 1</strong></p>
<div class="wp-block-image">
<figure class="aligncenter size-large"><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Great-Wall-of-China-Tourist-Attraction-600x371-1.png" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="600" height="371" class="wp-image-27748" alt="Great Wall of China Tourist Attraction" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Great-Wall-of-China-Tourist-Attraction-600x371-1.png 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Great-Wall-of-China-Tourist-Attraction-300x186-1.png 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Great-Wall-of-China-Tourist-Attraction-768x476-1.png 768w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Great-Wall-of-China-Tourist-Attraction.png 982w" /></figure>
</div>
<p>These places are popular, which means that there will be hundreds, or even thousands of other tourists there. Like you, they are all trying to get the same 📸 pictures, and have the same experience as you at the same time.</p>
<p>Therefore, it is going to be crowded and you might spend the majority of your time just waiting in line, and/or pushing your way through the crowds.</p>
<p>Probably not the way you want to spend your vacation, right? 🥱</p>
<p>If you want to have the most space available and least crowds, it is best to get there as soon as you can in the morning.</p>
<p>
Do some research to see what time places open, in order to be there the minute they unlock the doors. This will definitely help in skipping the long lines and heavier crowds of people.</p>
<div class="wp-block-image">
<figure class="aligncenter size-large"><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Tourist-Attractions-600x233-1.png" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="600" height="233" class="wp-image-27750" alt="Tourist Attractions" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Tourist-Attractions-600x233-1.png 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Tourist-Attractions-300x117-1.png 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Tourist-Attractions-768x299-1.png 768w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Tourist-Attractions.png 792w" /></figure>
</div>
<p>If you look on Google, you can sometimes see the peak times for locations like; museums or amusement parks. This way you can plan accordingly to be there, during the times when things are a bit slower.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Many_Attractions_May_Not_Be_Worth_Going_To"></span>Many Attractions May Not Be Worth Going To<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><strong>Tourist Attractions Tip # 2</strong></p>
<div class="wp-block-image">
<figure class="aligncenter size-large"><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/The-Colosseum-Rome-Italy.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="600" height="313" class="wp-image-13822" alt="The Colosseum Rome Italy" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/The-Colosseum-Rome-Italy.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/The-Colosseum-Rome-Italy-300x157-1.jpg 300w" /></figure>
</div>
<p>Just because a spot is popular, doesn’t necessarily mean that you need to see it while you’re visiting. There are plenty of popular attractions around the world, that are definitely overrated.</p>
<p>
They are simply not as fun or interesting as they may appear to be in brochures, photos and/or on the internet.</p>
<p>For instance, check out this post from – 🧳 The Travel. A very popular travel website located in the United States, and their article on; 🌄 20 Of The Most Overrated USA Tourist Attractions.</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/The-Travel-Logo.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-0" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/The-Travel-Logo.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-29987" alt="The Travel Logo" width="216" height="98" /></a></p>
<p><em>🏰 <a href="https://www.thetravel.com/20-of-the-most-overrated-usa-tourist-attractions-that-are-a-waste-of-time/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">The Travel – 20 Most Overrated USA Tourist Attractions</a></em></p>
<p>Some of the most overrated tourist destination spots they mention in their article and which you may or may not agree with include;</p>
<p><em>The Empire State Building and Times Square in New York City;<br />The Hollywood Sign And Walk of Fame in Los Angeles;<br />The Space Needle in Seattle;<br />The Palace of Versailles in France; <br />The Mona Lisa at The Louvre;<br />The Taj Mahal in India;<br />The Colosseum in Rome.</em></p>
<div class="wp-block-image">
<figure class="aligncenter size-large"><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Space-Needle-Seattle-600x478-1.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="600" height="478" class="wp-image-27751" alt="Space Needle Seattle" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Space-Needle-Seattle-600x478-1.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Space-Needle-Seattle-300x239-1.jpg 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Space-Needle-Seattle.jpg 640w" /></figure>
</div>
<p>All of these places tend to be extremely overcrowded and really offer little to do, besides take 📸 pictures or listen to a history lesson.</p>
<p>
While you may want to pop in just to say that you’ve been there, many seasoned travelers suggest spending your time – elsewhere.</p>
<div class="wp-block-image">
<figure class="aligncenter size-large"><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Colloseum-Rome-600x450-1.png" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="600" height="450" class="wp-image-27753" alt="Colloseum Rome" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Colloseum-Rome-600x450-1.png 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Colloseum-Rome-300x225-1.png 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Colloseum-Rome-768x576-1.png 768w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Colloseum-Rome.png 800w" /></figure>
</div>
<p>Instead, take some time to research other areas by checking out some reputable travel blogs for example. See what recommendations they offer in the area, as a better alternative.</p>
<p>Also, be sure to talk to the locals and ask what they may personally recommend checking out.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Attractions_That_Fit_Into_Your_Budget"></span>Attractions That Fit Into Your Budget<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><strong>Tourist Attractions Tip #3</strong></p>
<div class="wp-block-image">
<figure class="aligncenter size-large"><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Budget-600x400-1.png" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="600" height="400" class="wp-image-27754" alt="Travel Budget" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Budget-600x400-1.png 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Budget-300x200-1.png 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Budget-768x512-1.png 768w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Budget.png 936w" /></figure>
</div>
<p>Tourism makes cities a lot of money, so businesses will often up charge everything in the area to maximize profits.</p>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/SelectUSA-Logo.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-1" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://gr8traveltips.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/SelectUSA-Logo.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-29988" alt="SelectUSA Logo" width="355" height="74" data-sizes="(max-width: 355px) 100vw, 355px" srcset="https://gr8traveltips.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/SelectUSA-Logo.jpg 355w, https://gr8traveltips.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/SelectUSA-Logo-300x63.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p>According to <em>🏛 <a href="https://www.selectusa.gov/travel-tourism-and-hospitality-industry-united-states">SelectUSA</a></em> -The United States alone generates over $1.6 trillion in travel and tourism alone every year!</p>
<p>Typically, everything is going to be far more expensive within the general vicinity of popular tourist spots. Things such as; transportation and 🚕 taxi rates, food &#038; drinks, and parking prices.</p>
<p>This is of course in addition to the cost of admittance at these popular tourist attractions, so the dollars can add up pretty fast!</p>
<p>If you are traveling on a tight budget, you will want to factor these costs in before you commit to any plans.</p>
<p>
While there are some ways to save yourself some money (such as renting bikes or packing your own lunch), it may end up not being worth going, based on the cost of admission alone.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Consider_Travel_In_The_Off_Season"></span>Consider Travel In The Off Season<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><strong>Tourist Attractions Tip # 4</strong></p>
<div class="wp-block-image">
<figure class="aligncenter size-large"><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Winter-Travel-600x399-1.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="600" height="399" class="wp-image-27755" alt="Winter Travel" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Winter-Travel-600x399-1.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Winter-Travel-300x200-1.jpg 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Winter-Travel.jpg 640w" /></figure>
</div>
<p><em>Photo by Just Name from Pexels</em></p>
<p>If you want to save money and get away from the crowds, consider going during the off-season.</p>
<p>
Travelling during the less popular times of the year, can also save you a pretty penny on ✈ airplane tickets and 🏨 hotel reservations, as an example.</p>
<p>Many of the popular tourist attractions will certainly be less busy in the off season, and entrance fees may be reduced as well.</p>
<blockquote class="instagram-media" data-instgrm-permalink="https://www.instagram.com/p/BjVV3fOHw4E/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;utm_campaign=loading" data-instgrm-version="12" readability="-11.980519480519"></blockquote>
<p>The best practices to save yourself money and steer away from the busy crowds is to;</p>
<p><em>✔ Go to “summer” destinations in the fall or winter (or vice versa);<br />✔ Go the week or month before major travel events, like spring break or big festivals;<br />✔ Don’t be afraid of rainy season travel.</em></p>
<p>
Oftentimes, it will just rain for a few hours of the day and be sunny and comfortable for the rest of the time;<br />✔ Consider visiting cities in the winter time and plan more indoor activities.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Travel_In_Groups_Save"></span>Travel In Groups &#038; Save<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><strong>Tourist Attractions Tip # 5</strong></p>
<div class="wp-block-image">
<figure class="aligncenter size-large"><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Group-Travel-600x399-1.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="600" height="399" class="wp-image-27756" alt="Group Travel" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Group-Travel-600x399-1.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Group-Travel-300x200-1.jpg 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Group-Travel.jpg 640w" /></figure>
</div>
<p><i>Photo by bruce mars from Pexels</i></p>
<p>Rather than trying to plan out and book a travel itinerary on your own, you may want to consider using a professional travel agent.</p>
<p>
These people of a wealth of experience and knowledge and in many cases, can get you discount deals on major attractions and so forth.</p>
<p>Check Robert’s post here on the  👩 <a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/benefits-of-booking-with-a-travel-agent/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><em>Benefits Of</em> <em>Booking With A Professional Travel Agent</em>.</a> </p>
<p>
Many hotels also offer tours, that can be added on to your booking for a discount.</p>
<p>For example, world renowned <em>🛌 <a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/how-to-book-a-room-with-airbnb/">🛌 Airbnb, </a></em>also offers unique experiences that are hosted by knowledgeable local guides.</p>
<div class="wp-block-image">
<figure class="aligncenter size-large"><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airbnb-Adventures-600x248-1.png" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="600" height="248" class="wp-image-27757" alt="Airbnb Adventures" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airbnb-Adventures-600x248-1.png 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airbnb-Adventures-300x124-1.png 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airbnb-Adventures-768x318-1.png 768w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airbnb-Adventures.png 1060w" /></figure>
</div>
<p>Traveling with a group of tourists is a fun way to meet other travelers during your trip, and also save money booking a group local tour.</p>
<p>
Plus, the guides will often take you to multiple attractions on the same trip, which allows you to see the sights with a knowledgeable local.</p>

<p>Traveling is always an incredible experience, but you want to be sure that you make the most of your trip.</p>
<p>
Sometimes heading to a popular destination attraction is definitely worth the time and expense. Sometimes, it just isn’t.</p>
<p>Always do your research to see what other travelers have to say and be open to changing your plans, to create a better experience – no matter where the road takes you!</p>
