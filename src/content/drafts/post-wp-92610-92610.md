<p>Rose Lane is all about that upscale comfort with a spicy twist, just a short stroll from a serene private beach in Rothesay. This isn&#8217;t just any house; it&#8217;s where luxury lounges with a dash of &#8220;Moulin Rouge&#8221; flair.</p>



<p>This three-bed, two-bath spot is more than just a place to crash. The master suite, hidden behind a bookcase (yeah, you read that right), is your chill zone with a jet tub. Downstairs, the vibe&#8217;s all about maxing and relaxing – think spacious kitchen and cozy dining for those feast and chill moments.</p>



<p>Upstairs takes the chill to the next level. Two more bedrooms, a family room with a 60&#8243; TV, and a throwback vintage massage chair mix comfort with a bit of quirk.</p>



<p>But here&#8217;s the real tea: the master turns into a secret &#8220;Adult theme room.&#8221; New-age Victorian meets burlesque vibes, mirrored ceiling, leather bench – low-key risqué. Feeling adventurous? Dive into the hidden tickle trunk packed with playful surprises. Craving more? There’s a light BDSM option, but why not?</p>
