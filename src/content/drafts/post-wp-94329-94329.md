<p>The Cliff House, perched near Sleeping Ute Mountain in McElmo Canyon, is the ultimate Valentine&#8217;s Day getaway for couples who vibe with both nature and modern comforts. It&#8217;s not just a stay; it&#8217;s an experience, 40 minutes from Mesa Verde and a quick drive from Cortez.</p>
<p>This romantic Airbnb isn&#8217;t your typical retreat. Built into a red rock cliff, it&#8217;s a real-life dream for those who love unique, Instagram-worthy places. The native landscaping blooms all summer, setting a perfect, natural scene. Picture you and your other half chilling on the porch, the canyon views making every moment feel like a scene from Fools Rush In.</p>
<p>While the Cliff House skips the TV to amp up the romance and connection with nature, it&#8217;s still got the tech to keep you hooked up. Fast broadband, Apple HomePod, and solid Verizon LTE mean you can stream your fave love songs or post those perfect couple selfies with no hassle.</p>
<p>For Valentine’s Day, this spot is a no-brainer. It&#8217;s the perfect mix of adventure, tranquility, and modern tech, ideal for creating those unforgettable moments.</p>
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote is-layout-flow wp-block-quote-is-layout-flow">
<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-from-the-owner">From the owner:</h2>
</p>
<p>Stay on the flank of Sleeping Ute Mountain in historic McElmo Canyon just 40 minutes from Mesa Verde and 20 minutes from the town of Cortez. The Cliff House is built right into the red rock cliff wall of a private red rock canyon alcove with comfortable amenities, internet, nearby petroglyphs and sweeping views down canyon. A perfect place to base yourself for your next creative endeavor or for exploring in the wilds of the four corners.</p>
<p>In addition to being built into sandstone rock, the Cliff House is landscaped with native species that bloom all summer. Around the corner from the house, in another rock alcove, is a little lawn. Enjoy comfortable porch seating with a stunning view.</p>
<p>We purposefully do not provide a TV and hope our space is a nature-inspired respite. That said, we also know you might want to get away and retreat to the Cliff House in order to do some work in-between exploring the Southwest. We have fast, broadband wireless internet with Eero WiFi. Stream music to our Apple HomePod with your iPhone or AirPlay-enabled device. We have a network extender for strong Verizon LTE service.</p>
