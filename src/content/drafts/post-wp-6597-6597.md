<span lang="EN-GB" style="mso-ansi-language: EN-GB;">The global<br />
holiday rentals industry is growing annually with more and more guests now<br />
choosing individual <a href="http://www.needmorerentals.com/rentals-tips/what-rental-guests-want/holiday-homes-v-hotels/">homes<br />
over a hotel</a> to suit their individual requirements. Whether you are new to<br />
renting or a long-term manager, it is always important to continually ask<br />
yourself what are you doing to get ahead of your competition and receiving a<br />
slice of that pie. </span></p>
<p></p>
<div class="MsoNormal">
</div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span lang="EN-GB" style="mso-ansi-language: EN-GB;">Damian<br />
Sheridan of <a href="http://www.needmorerentals.com/">NeedMoreRentals.com</a><br />
provides rental home owners and property managers a few ways to stay ahead of<br />
the field.</span></div>
<h4>
Your Listings on Booking Portals</h4>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span lang="EN-GB" style="mso-ansi-language: EN-GB;">How will<br />
your property stand out from the many others in your area? You realistically<br />
have around 2 seconds to impress your prospective guest &#8211; it’s all about making<br />
sure you get that initial click through to your listing. Think about the lead<br />
image thumbnail, title, then description. <a href="http://www.needmorerentals.com/marketing/on-line-marketing/advertising-on-booking-portals/your-property-photos/">Images</a><br />
tell a thousand stories and with vacation rentals you can’t underestimate their<br />
importance. Don’t think that your fancy I-phone images will suffice – get<br />
professional photos done. When expanded on a computer screen they can appear<br />
pixelated and fuzzy. It’s well worth the investment. Your lead image should<br />
ideally be your stand-out feature, however if, for example, all of your<br />
neighbours are parading their pool, try a nice shot of a sun terrace or balcony<br />
view. Furthermore consider carefully your property title and description. Which<br />
of the following property titles would be more grabbing &#8211; “Apartment to rent in<br />
Barcelona” or “Spacious 2 Bedroom Beach Apartment, Free Wi-Fi &#038; Welcome<br />
Pack”?</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
</div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span lang="EN-GB" style="mso-ansi-language: EN-GB;">When adding<br />
your complete and detailed <a href="http://www.needmorerentals.com/marketing/on-line-marketing/advertising-on-booking-portals/your-property-description/">property<br />
description</a> put yourself in your visitors’ shoes. Try to walk them around<br />
your home. Think comfort, think relaxation and think convenience. You have to<br />
be passionate about the place. What extras do you provide? What’s within<br />
walking distance? Have you included as many great photos as possible? Are there<br />
any spelling or grammar mistakes? Is your unique selling point clear?</span></div>
<h4>
Your Unique Selling Point</h4>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span lang="EN-GB" style="mso-ansi-language: EN-GB;">What is the<br />
unique selling point of your rental home for your guests? Your wonderful pool?<br />
The stunning view? A quirky theme? You <i>have</i> to know what will set your<br />
place apart from your local competition. One of our clients in Ireland offers<br />
free payment of a motorway toll upon checking in &#8211; a great idea! Check out your<br />
competitors and see if any of them are offering things like <a href="http://www.needmorerentals.com/rentals-tips/wifi/">Wi-Fi</a> internet,<br />
satellite TV or <a href="http://www.needmorerentals.com/welcome-pack/">welcome<br />
packs</a>. You could even consider <a href="http://www.needmorerentals.com/rentals-tips/pets/">accepting pets.</a><br />
Anything that will differentiate <i>your</i> rental property is imperative to<br />
know.</span></div>
<h4>
Your Own Website</h4>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span lang="EN-GB" style="mso-ansi-language: EN-GB;">Do you have<br />
<a href="http://www.needmorerentals.com/marketing/on-line-marketing/your-own-website/">your<br />
own website</a>? If not, get one! It’s your shop window. Guests will now often <i>expect</i><br />
to see one and a few days hard work will save you considerable time in the long<br />
run when answering the same standard questions again and again. Your rental<br />
home website is a great place to add property images, <a href="http://www.needmorerentals.com/marketing/on-line-marketing/a-video-for-your-rental-home/">videos</a>,<br />
<a href="http://www.needmorerentals.com/marketing/on-line-marketing/advertising-on-booking-portals/guest-reviews/">guest<br />
reviews</a>, your bio or other information. If that’s not enough persuasion,<br />
you can even make money off the back of your guests’ travel plans by joining<br />
affiliate linking programmes and placing banners on your site.</span></div>
