<h4 class="fusion-responsive-typography-calculated" data-fontsize="18" data-lineheight="30.06px"><b>3. Recurring Expenses</b></h4>
<p>Among your recurring expenses are anything that has a consistent monthly or annual cost. These expense include utilities, subscriptions, insurance, licensing, banking fees, financing, and strata fees.</p>
<blockquote readability="34.757575757576">
<p><span><b>7. Utilities</b></span><span><b> – </b><span>unlike with long term rentals, as the homeowner, you will be responsible for providing heat, water, electricity, and internet.</span></span></p>
<p><span><b>8. Subscriptions </b><span data-fusion-font="true"><b>– </b>this category includes subscriptions to streaming services (Netflix, Disney+), <a href="https://www.liftylife.ca/airbnb-vacation-rental-software/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">vacation rental software</a> (channel manager, dynamic pricing), and business tools (G-suite, accounting software).</span></span></p>
<p><span><b>9. Insurance – </b>STR insurance is different</span><span> than your home or rental insurance; </span><span>get a quote on <a href="https://www.squareone.ca/?offer_code=LIFTYLIFE" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">STR insurance here</a>. Not all providers insure short term rentals.</span></p>
<p><span><b>10. Licensing &#038; Property Taxes – </b>STR licensing can range from $100 to $2500 annually, depending on the city. Property taxes are disclosed on MLS listings.</span></p>
<p><strong>11. Financing &#038; Banking – </strong><span>use a mortgage calculator to get an estimate on your financing costs; <a href="https://www.rbcroyalbank.com/mortgages/mortgage-calculators.html">here is the RBC calculator</a>. Banking expenses include a credit card, chequing account, and e-transfer fees (used to pay cleaners).</span></p>
<p><strong>12. Strata Fees – </strong><span>in stratified complexes you will also be responsible for strata fees. Check what is included in your strata fees in some cases utilities will be covered. You can find the monthly strata fee on the MLS.</span></p>
</blockquote>
