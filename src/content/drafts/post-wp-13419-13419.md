<p>The area meets four of UNESCO&#8217;s ten required natural criteria and remains one of only a handful of places in the world to achieve this high criteria status level. The colorful and diverse landscapes, rare flora and fauna, and world-class examples of Earth’s ecological processes all contribute to the region&#8217;s World Heritage listing. The area contains plant species that are unique and considered new to science, five of Australia&#8217;s 26 species of endangered Australian mammals, as well as 35% of all of Australia&#8217;s bird species.</p>
