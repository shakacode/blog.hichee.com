<p>I don&#8217;t know about you, but I do love a rope bridge.<br />It adds to the adventure and takes a treehouse to another level.<br />This treehouse has a fun feel with its Swiss Family Robinson theme.<br />There are many little details like the cleat handles, the ship&#8217;s wheel, and the toy parrot that add to the shipwrecked feel.</p>
<p><strong>Here&#8217;s what the owners have to say about this rental</strong></p>
<p>Welcome to Treasure Hunt Tree House, located in Kennesaw GA (about 20 min drive from Atlanta)</p>
<p>This is a truly unique three-floor tree house complete with many fun features as well as this cool activity: The tree house has an adventurous twist in the form of a treasure hunt for any guest who stays in this fun little bungalow.</p>
<p>This unique two-structure tree house connected by a rope bridge sits high among the bamboo leaves. Down below you’ll hear the sounds of a trickling stream that runs underneath the treehouse, a bridge, and a hammock. This simple intimate setting provides quiet spaces to rest and relax, with an added bonus of an escape room-style treasure hunt hidden amongst the tree houses.</p>
