<p>While traveling can be exciting, it can also be at certain times – stressful. </p>
<p>Due to all the increased 🛂 security demands in place today, we all need to travel with and insure our <em>📰 Sensitive Documents</em>, such as; passports, tourist visas and so on are valid.</p>
<p>We as travelers, always need to carry these documents with us in a safe and secure manner.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Two_Important_Sensitive_Documents_To_Carry_With_You"></span>Two Important Sensitive Documents To Carry With You<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><strong>Traveling With Sensitive Documents #1</strong></p>
<p><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-9710 size-full" title="Tips For Traveling With Sensitive Documents" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/US-Customs.jpg" alt="Tips For Traveling With Sensitive Documents" width="500" height="333" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/US-Customs.jpg 500w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/US-Customs-300x200-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" /></p>
<p>Two important documents to have on hand when traveling include; Passport, along with secondary Government Issued Identification {Drivers License.}</p>
<p>It’s always a good measure, to have at least 2 official forms of photo identification along with you when traveling. </p>
<p>We are also in possession of 💳 NEXUS cards, which are great to have for those traveling on a regular basis and offer additional official photo identification.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Sensitive_Documents_-_For_American_Citizens"></span>Sensitive Documents – For American Citizens<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><strong>Traveling With Sensitive Documents #2</strong></p>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/travel-documents/102810_1844_whattraveld1-jpg/" rel="attachment wp-att-261"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-261 size-full" title="travel documents" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/102810_1844_Whattraveld1.jpg" alt="travel documents" width="280" height="297" /></a></p>
<p>For those of you who tend to misplace things, or have lost important documents for various reasons, you can contact a company like that of; <em>✔ VitalChek.</em> </p>
<p>This company has been an – Official U.S. Government Authorized Service for over 25 years. They are a trusted source for acquiring a replacement – certified birth record.</p>
<p>Once you have your replacement birth certificate, you can complete the process to get your passport. U.S. passports are administered by the – U.S. Department of State.</p>
<p>If you or someone in your family is applying for a U.S. passport for the first time, it is important to begin the process well far enough in advance of your trip, to complete the entire process without issues.</p>
<p>A routine service passport timeline can average between four and six weeks to complete minimum. While an expedited service is quicker than routine service, it can still average between two and three weeks.</p>
<p> <br /><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/U.S.-Department-of-State-Logo.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-1" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-29744" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/U.S.-Department-of-State-Logo.jpg" alt="U.S. Department of State Logo" width="510" height="123" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/U.S.-Department-of-State-Logo.jpg 510w, https://gr8traveltips.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/U.S.-Department-of-State-Logo-300x72.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 510px) 100vw, 510px" /></a></p>
<p>For the most up-to-date information on <em>🛂 <a title="Passport Processing" href="http://travel.state.gov/passport/processing/processing_1740.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">passport processing times</a></em>, you can visit the U.S. State Department website. </p>
<p>If you are traveling out of the country, it’s always good to research where the American Embassy is located, in proximity to your destination. Should you lose your passport, you will need to contact them for an emergency replacement.</p>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/Of-Special-Note-e1626881250268.png" data-rel="lightbox-image-2" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Of-Special-Note-250x250-1.png" alt="Of Special Note" width="150" height="150" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-33912" /></a></p>
<p>I strongly suggest taking a photo of your documents with your 📱 cellphone and also 📧 emailing them to you as well. </p>
<p>This will make the process of providing proof of identity go much more smoothly and quickly, should you ever need a replacement. </p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Smart_Traveler_Enrollment_Program_-_STEP"></span>Smart Traveler Enrollment Program – STEP<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><strong>Traveling With Sensitive Documents #3</strong></p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/STEP-Enrollment-Program.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-3" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-12520" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/STEP-Enrollment-Program.jpg" alt="STEP Enrollment Program" width="502" height="229" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/STEP-Enrollment-Program.jpg 640w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/STEP-Enrollment-Program-300x137-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 502px) 100vw, 502px" /></a></p>
<p><strong>STEP</strong> is a free service provided by the – 🏢 Bureau of Consular Affairs, from the U.S. State Department. This service is for U.S. citizens, who are traveling or living outside the country.</p>
<p>This program allows you to enter information about your upcoming trip, so the State Department can stay in contact with you in case of an emergency. </p>
<p>It also allows Americans residing abroad, to get routine information from the nearest U.S. embassy or consulate.</p>
<p>Regardless of the country you are traveling to, the U.S. Embassy and Consulate are both lifelines for American citizens traveling abroad. </p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/STEP-Program.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-4" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-29980" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/STEP-Program-600x99-1.jpg" alt="STEP Program" width="600" height="99" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/STEP-Program-600x99-1.jpg 600w, https://gr8traveltips.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/STEP-Program-300x50.jpg 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/STEP-Program.jpg 713w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a> Consider registering prior to traveling abroad. I’ve included the link below to the Official website for your convenience.</p>
<p><em>➡   <a title="STEP" href="https://step.state.gov/step/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Smart Traveler Enrollment Program</a></em> (STEP)</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Keeping_Your_Documents_Safe_Secure"></span>Keeping Your Documents Safe &#038; Secure<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><strong>Traveling With Sensitive Documents #4</strong></p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Turkish-Airlines-Airplane.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-5" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter  wp-image-13324" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Turkish-Airlines-Airplane.jpg" alt="Turkish Airlines Airplane" width="595" height="257" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Turkish-Airlines-Airplane.jpg 717w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Turkish-Airlines-Airplane-300x130-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 595px) 100vw, 595px" /></a></p>
<p>Once your trip is underway, you need to be very sure about how you keep your documents secure. </p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Helpful-Tips-Banner.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-6" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Helpful-Tips-Banner.jpg" alt="Helpful Tips Banner" width="220" height="105" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-22258" /></a></p>
<p><center><i><b> Never carry important documents in your checked 🧳 luggage!</b></i></center> </p>
<p>Keep them safe and secure in your 👜 carry-on at all times, when in transit.</p>
<p>If possible and once you arrive at your destination, place your travel documents, tickets and other valuables in a 🔒 safe in your room, to help keep them secure at all times.</p>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/executive-suites-squamish/executive-suites-hotel-lobby-squamish-bc/" rel="attachment wp-att-13867"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-13867 size-full" title="Executive Suites Hotel Lobby Squamish BC" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Executive-Suites-Hotel-Lobby-Squamish-BC.jpg" alt="Executive Suites Hotel Lobby Squamish BC" width="600" height="322" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Executive-Suites-Hotel-Lobby-Squamish-BC.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Executive-Suites-Hotel-Lobby-Squamish-BC-300x161-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a></p>
<p>If your room doesn’t have a safe, ask at the front desk if there is usually a 🔒 safety deposit box available for their guests, for secure storage of sensitive items or valuables. </p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Final-Thoughts-Banner.png" data-rel="lightbox-image-7" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Final-Thoughts-Banner.png" alt="Final Thoughts Banner" width="500" height="155" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-33185" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Final-Thoughts-Banner.png 500w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Final-Thoughts-Banner-300x93-1.png 300w" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" /></a></p>
<p><strong>Final Note:</strong></p>
<p>I’ve always said that the most important things to have and to hang on to when traveling are your;</p>
<p><center><i><b>Travel Medical Insurance … Passport … Cellphone </b></i></center></p>
<p>Everything else can be easily replaced!</p>
<p>As mentioned above, I always make a point of 📸 photographing our sensitive documents and then also emailing them to me for safe keeping. </p>
<p>
Should you happen to lose your passport, this will make replacement at the Consulate much easier for you.</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/lost-luggage.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-8" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9929" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/lost-luggage.jpg" alt="Lost Travel Luggage" width="434" height="244" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/lost-luggage.jpg 434w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/lost-luggage-300x169-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 434px) 100vw, 434px" /></a></p>
<p>As well, I also take a 📸 photo of my checked 🧳 luggage, should it go missing in transit. </p>
<p>
Just a few things to consider about {safe guarding your documents} when traveling.</p>
