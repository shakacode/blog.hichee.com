<p>These are two of the most photographed mountains in North America — and for a good reason. Rising more than 14,000 feet above sea level just south of Aspen, the twin peaks are striped with snow all summer. Aspen trees drape the hillsides, while Maroon Lake provides a handy mirror that grounds the whole scene in a postcard-perfect reflection.</p>
