<p>The pet-friendly Shenandoah Yurt is a furbaby-friendly yurt that combines the rustic touch of a cabin with modern amenities, creating a cozy getaway as it is Instagrammable for you and your furry friends. Imagine your pup frolicking on the expansive deck or cozying up by the wood-burning stove after a day of adventure. This is a place where pet tales come to life.</p>



<p>The yurt, designed with attention to detail, is a haven for those seeking a stylish, nature-immersed experience. Start your day with fresh organic eggs in the fully equipped kitchen, practice yoga with your four-legged companion on the deck, or unwind in the outdoor hot tub under the stars. For eco-conscious travelers, we offer an EV charging station.</p>



<p>Ideal for four but accommodating up to eight, the yurt features two queen-sized bedrooms, a king-size sleeper sofa in the loft, and a comfy sleeper couch in the living area. Pet-friendly amenities like dog beds and bowls are provided, ensuring your pets enjoy the comforts of home.</p>



<p>The kitchen is ready for your culinary adventures, equipped with everything from a Nespresso machine for your morning brew to wine glasses for evening relaxation. The open floor plan, panoramic windows, and loft with a pool table ensure your stay is as enjoyable indoors as it is outdoors.</p>
