<p>This lovely 100-year-old rental has a raised deck for lounging and spying on the wildlife, not exactly a treehouse, but more a house in the trees.<br />This rental has been renovated with a huge dollop of style. The mostly wooden interior is warm and cozy, and the furniture and fittings are 100% on point.<br />Everywhere you look could be on the front cover of an interior design magazine.</p>
<p><strong>Here&#8217;s what the owners have to say about this historic property&#8230;<br /></strong></p>
<p>Wiley Mountain Lodge is a century-old Inn that welcomed North Georgia&#8217;s 1st visitors off the Tallulah Falls Locomotive Trains at Wiley Station. Horse and buggy brought guests to the front steps of, what was then, Mountain Laurel Inn. Guests were greeted by the Inn&#8217;s owner, Mrs Blanche Court, and the luggage boy took the leather suitcases up the winding stone steps to the broad front porch.</p>
<p>100 years later we have transformed Wiley Mountain Lodge into a cozy mountain retreat where you can quickly escape from the city to be surrounded by nature. The Lakemont style batten-board lodge is totally private, perfectly located, and completely outfitted with all the details. We imagine that our pioneer ancestors would get a kick out of how fashionable Wiley looks today. When you walk through the screen doors you immediately have a sense of connection to the past that&#8217;s hard to resist! Wiley feels honest and unpretentious. It evokes a simpler time with natural warmth, organic textures, tree top views, and clean mountain air.</p>
