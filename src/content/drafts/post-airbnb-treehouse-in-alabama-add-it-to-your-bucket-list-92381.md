<p>Our very unique treehouse is nestled into the treetops on 40 acres of forest. Great for a couples retreat, honeymoon, or spiritual reconnecting. Get away from it all and enjoy the nature trails and 2 acre lake(seasonal at times)to pass the time and be able to really unwind. Sit and enjoy your morning coffee outside on the deck as you may be able to catch a peak at the deer.</p>



<p>Only 20 minutes from I65. One hour from either Birmingham or Huntsville, Al. 10 minutes from nearby boat launch for Smith Lake. Plenty of room to park boat. Please check out Guidebook for all local attractions that I have put together to help you with questions and to navigate better. Lots of waterfalls, hiking, horseback riding etc. to check out that is within 30-60 minutes from property.</p>
