<p>Smart Pricing is is a pricing <a href="https://thedexterapp.com/blog/smart-pricing-strategy/">strategy</a> that helps you make data-based pricing decisions for your business. It allows you to take advantage of market fluctuations on an almost real-time basis. Also known as dynamic pricing, it  works by monitoring changes in the market and adjusting your rates accordingly. For example, if demand for accommodation in your area increases during peak season, you can use dynamic pricing to increase your rates accordingly. Alternatively, if there’s less demand during down times, you can use dynamic pricing to reduce your rates so that you don’t miss out on potential bookings. The idea is that no matter what the current market conditions are like, you’ll always be able to charge fair prices for your rental property.</p>
