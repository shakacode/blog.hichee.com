<p>A charming tiny home with essential amenities, farm-fresh eggs and sausages, and a creek running just outside your door? Where do I sign up? Pachamama Farmhouse is not the biggest farmhouse you’ll see on this list, but what it lacks in size, it more than makes up for in charm and appeal. This hippie rental property features an organic and free-range farm, high-speed Wi-Fi, and various farm animals that your children would love to see!</p>
</p>
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote is-layout-flow wp-block-quote-is-layout-flow">
<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-from-the-owner">From the owner:</h3>
</p>
<p>24&#8242; Yurt &amp; attached Tiny house is opulent! Indoor bathroom &amp; kitchen, Glamping at its finest! This One of a kind Gem is an Off Grid Solar &amp; Propane powered fully functional Home from Wood harvested on site We provide all of the essentials , with a running creek outside your door. Including farm-fresh organic eggs &amp; sausages (from our Farm!) muffin, yogurt, fruit, juice, hot cereal,coffee &amp; more.</p>
</p>
<p>We have WIFI</p>
</p>
<p>Private fire pit creekside ,We are dog friendly but Do Not allow CATS</p>
</p>
<p>The space</p>
</p>
<p>Nestled in the Douglas County forest, Pachamama Farm is surrounded by untouched mountain springs and soaring fir trees. This fully equipped 24&#8242; yurt is a unique experience. Indoor bathroom, and kitchen are in an attached tiny house, so your glamping experience can have all of the comforts of home. With a Queen bed, and full size futon, this yurt can easily accommodate 4 people. The Hippe Shack will be your sanctuary for peace and quiet with breathtaking panoramic views, miles of hiking trails, a private beach on woods creek, and a fire pit for cozy nights.</p>
</p>
</blockquote>

<p>The property &#8220;Potomac Overlook Farms&#8221; boasts an impressive average rating of 4.96 stars from a total of 107 guest reviews. Guests have consistently praised the estate for its quality and amenities, reflected in high ratings across various categories. Here&#8217;s a breakdown of the ratings:</p>
</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Cleanliness: </strong>4.9/5 &#8211; Most guests found the property very clean, contributing to a pleasant and comfortable stay.</li>
</p>
<li><strong>Accuracy: </strong>5.0/5 &#8211; The property&#8217;s description matches guest expectations exactly, ensuring no unpleasant surprises upon arrival.</li>
</p>
<li><strong>Communication: </strong>5.0/5 &#8211; The hosts, Charles &amp; Amber, are noted for their prompt and clear communication, greatly enhancing guest experiences.</li>
</p>
<li><strong>Location: </strong>4.9/5 &#8211; Its serene and picturesque setting near the Potomac River is highly appreciated, although some noted the remote nature of the property.</li>
</p>
<li><strong>Value: </strong>4.8/5 &#8211; Guests feel they receive great value for the cost, thanks to the extensive amenities and large space, though a few mentioned it as pricey.</li>
</ul>
</p>
<p>Visitors love the property for its spacious environment, natural beauty, and range of activities available, including a private pool and various river activities. However, a recurring note of concern involves the presence of insects due to the rural setting. Guests with insect allergies or phobias are advised to consider this before booking.</p>
</p>
<p>The overall sentiment is overwhelmingly positive, with the estate frequently recommended for family gatherings and group retreats. The few criticisms are generally minor and do not significantly detract from the overall excellent reviews.</p>
