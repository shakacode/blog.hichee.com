<p>This place is known for its towering pali, or sea cliffs, punctuated by narrow valleys, streams, and cascading waterfalls. The Kalalau Trail is a steep footpath running through the park, between nearby Ke’e Beach and sandy Kalalau Beach. It cuts through 5 valleys, including the Hanakoa Valley, with its native plants and old agricultural terraces.</p>
