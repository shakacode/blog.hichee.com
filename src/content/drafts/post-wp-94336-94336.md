<p>Smiths Point on the Hudson River is the ultimate getaway for couples seeking a Valentine&#8217;s Day escape straight out of a rom-com. This spot is not just about the views (though trust us, they will take your breath away); it&#8217;s about creating those &#8220;just us&#8221; moments.</p>
<p>Wake up to the kind of sunrise that has you both reaching for your phones to capture the moment, then spend the day chilling on the deck. Here, you can watch eagles soar and yachts cruise by, maybe while sipping your fave brew. And for some social media wows? Pose with your feet dangling off the dock, capturing those lazy, love-filled afternoons.</p>
<p>The Carriage House, exclusively yours, blends privacy with quaint charm. Picture unwinding in the hot sauna or steam shower – it&#8217;s like a spa date but better because it&#8217;s just the two of you. And when night falls, the gazebo is your go-to for those deep convos and maybe even a little stargazing.</p>
<p>This property isn&#8217;t just about the stunning river views; it&#8217;s about making Valentine&#8217;s Day (or any day) extra special. With towns like Saugerties and Woodstock nearby, you&#8217;ve got the perfect mix of scenic tranquility and quaint town vibes.</p>
