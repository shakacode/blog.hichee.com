<p><strong>Stay ahead of the competition by checking out these 6 short-term rental courses</strong> that will help boost your knowledge of the vacation rental language, technology and operations and keep you up to date with industry best practices. </p>
<p>Disclaimer: This is by no means an exhaustive list. Many consultants, bloggers and podcasters also provide valuable learning resources that may not come in a course format.</p>
<h2>What are the benefits of short-term rental courses?</h2>
<p>Guest behaviour, travel demands and <a href="https://superhog.com/short-term-rental-predictions-2023/">industry trends</a> change year on year, which is why it’s so important to <strong>stay up to date</strong> with what’s happening, so you can continue to increase your revenue, streamline your operations, target niche markets and <strong>grow your business effectively and efficiently. </strong></p>
<p>Equip yourself with the knowledge and skills required to be successful in such a multi-faceted role by choosing the right short-term rental courses for you.</p>
<h2>Choosing the right short-term rental course for you</h2>
<p>As you know, learning from others is an excellent resource for all property managers and hosts. </p>
<p>If you want to venture into the world of events, check out <a href="https://superhog.com/vacation-rental-events-2023/">the top 10 vacation rental events</a> and conferences to attend in 2023.  </p>
<p>But, if you’re searching for the best online property management courses to help boost your vacation rental education, then you need to consider what exactly it is you’re hoping to learn so that you can choose the right course for you. </p>
<p>Here are two points to help you identify whether the course will be a good fit for you:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>How do you learn best? </strong>Do you learn best from visuals, auditory, reading/writing or kinesthetic learning?</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><strong>What do you need help with?</strong> Are you just getting started in the industry or is there a particular business strategy you’re looking to learn more about and implement into your operations? Do you have any knowledge gaps?</li>
</ul>

Whether you’re just starting out or looking to refine your strategy and employee education, VRU trains and supports those in the vacation rental industry. </p>
<p><strong><em>What is the cost of VRU?</em></strong></p>

<li><strong><em>Basic</em>: </strong>Free</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><strong><em>Masters</em>: </strong>Lifetime access for $495 or a monthly subscription for $49pm</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><strong><em>Enterprise</em>: </strong>Custom plan &#038; pricing upon request</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><strong><em>1-1</em> <em>Consulting</em>: </strong>Custom plan &#038; pricing upon request</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><em>Does the course provide short-term rental certification?</em></strong></p>
<p><strong>Yes</strong>, you can sign up for their <a href="https://www.thevru.com/why-realtors-need-vru/">VRU Realtor Certification</a> which will help <em>‘teach you all you need to know about the vacation rental industry, attract more investor clients, close more sales and even manage the properties.’</em></p>

<p><strong><em>Why should you join VRMA?</em></strong></p>
<p>The Vacation Rental Management Association is one of the most well-known and prestigious professional bodies in the short-term rental space. Alongside their annual <a href="https://superhog.com/vacation-rental-events-2023/">industry events</a>, their <a href="https://www.vrma.org/page/vrma-certificate-program-1321">vacation rental management certification course</a> is for those looking to<em> ‘strengthen their industry knowledge, sharpen their skills, advance their career, improve their business and gain a competitive edge in the professionally managed vacation rental business’. </em></p>
<p><strong><em>What is the cost of VRMA?</em></strong></p>
<p>If you are a member of VRMA, the certificate programme will cost you $450.</p>
<p>For non-members, the cost is $750.</p>
<p><strong><em>Does the course provide short-term rental certification?</em></strong></p>
<p><strong>Yes, </strong>you must complete the 5-module online programme and achieve a ‘pass’ on the assessment to earn your certificate.</p>
<p>Once the certification has been gained, you can use it to help distinguish your standard of excellence to the industry, including guests and other property managers.</p>

<p><strong><em>Why should you complete the STR Certification?</em></strong></p>
<p><a href="https://www.strcertification.com/">STR Certification</a> course is all about short-term rental education. This course was designed by Julie Davies to educate on best practices for the emerging vacation rental industry after public officials in Las Vegas noticed a lack of valuable accredited courses on such topics. </p>
<p>With excellent academic reviews, the STR Certification is a college-level course that can be taken as a 2-day live course or a 2-week self-study online course, with an available textbook ‘vacation rental management 411’.</p>
<p>You can register your interest for STR Certification on their website and check out the upcoming course dates available.</p>
<p><strong><em>What is the cost of the STR Certification?</em></strong></p>
<p>Price available upon registration.<strong> </strong></p>
<p><strong><em>Does the course provide short-term rental certification?</em></strong></p>
<p><strong>Yes</strong>, upon completion of the course, property managers are awarded the STR Certification Seal to represent that they are now an accredited short-term rental professional who abides by industry best practices.</p>

<p><strong><em>Why should you complete the Cleaning Certification?</em></strong></p>
<p><a href="https://cleaningcertification.com/">Cleaning Certification</a> is a great short-term rental course to have your vacation rental cleaners complete so that you know their following best practices at all times. Since Covid, this has become a primary concern to both hosts and guests. </p>
<p><strong>What is the cost of the Cleaning Certification</strong>?</p>
<p>Courses start at $24 but there is the option to buy in bulk if you would like multiple employees to complete the certification.</p>
<p><strong>Does the course provide short-term rental certification?</strong> </p>

<p>This badge can then be displayed on your listing on OTA sights like Airbnb and Vrbo, or even your own direct booking website and other marketing materials you use.</p>
