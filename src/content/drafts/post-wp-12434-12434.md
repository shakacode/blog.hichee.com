<p>We are continually surprised (and discouraged) by how many VR operators are NOT doing the basics. Be different, Be bold!  This episode digs into some big – and small – things you can do to rise above your competition.  </p>
<p>Topics discussed in depth include: </p>
<p><strong>1) Creating a “I would never leave” relationship with your owners</strong></p>
<p><strong>2) Being Bold</strong> </p>
<p><strong>3)  Being meticulous about the properties you take on and those you KEEP </strong></p>
<p><strong>4) Personalized welcome notes and gift</strong></p>
<p><strong>5)  Direct Marketing – at the very least to your return guest list! </strong></p>
<p><strong>6) Creating a tone with owners and guests of Connection vs. Standard Customer Service.</strong> </p>
