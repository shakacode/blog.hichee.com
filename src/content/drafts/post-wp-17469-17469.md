<p>The Brighton Fringe Festival is one of the UK’s largest and most exciting arts festivals, showcasing all kinds of inspiring performances across the city. From theatre to music and comedy, this month-long celebration brings together a wealth of local and international talent for an unmissable event.</p>
<p>In May 2023, the Brighton Fringe Festival will return for its 24th edition, running from 6th May to 28th May. Visitors can expect a diverse line-up of events at venues across the city, with something for everyone. From theatre shows to art exhibitions and comedy nights, it promises to be an incredible month of culture and entertainment.</p>
