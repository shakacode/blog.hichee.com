<p>Here is a short list of just a few things to consider when selecting a car to rent;</p>
<p><em>🚗 What type and size of car are you paying for?</em></p>
<p>🚗 Just what’s included in that rental car anyway?</p>
<p>🚗 Does the car have air conditioning?</p>
<p>🚗 Is unlimited mileage included in the contract?</p>
<p>🚗 Does the rental have an automatic transmission?</p>
<p>💺 Do you have to pay extra for child car seats?</p>
<p>As you can see, all these things add up quickly if they are not included in the rental policy. </p>
<p>So, it is very important to ask a lot of questions and <span> <em><strong>📰 Read the Fine Print,</strong></em></span> before signing that contract!</p>
<p>Don’t get me wrong, I hate paying higher prices for most anything I purchase. You can save a great deal of 💵 money, knowing just what is included when renting a car, especially from a major national rental car company.</p>
<p><strong>I do recommend shopping around and check the various rates available, especially when it’s so easy to do these days on the 💻 Internet.</strong></p>

<p>Here are a couple of more things to consider before renting that car;</p>
<p><em><br />👩 Who is going to be driving the car, is anyone under age?</em></p>
<p>😎 Are you by yourself on a business trip or are you traveling with the whole family?</p>
<p>🚥 If there is going to be more than one person driving, be sure you have the appropriate insurance coverage.</p>
<p>🚌  size of car will I need that will suit the number of people and baggage?</p>
<p>🛺 Is the vehicle going to be big enough?</p>
<p>🥜 Do I need rental car insurance?</p>
<p>💺 Do you need an Infant or a child booster seat?</p>
<p>Never take chances and drive without the proper rental car insurance in place!</p>
<p>My wife and I were involved in a <em>serious rear end collision</em> a few years back, when attending the <em>🏁 <a title="Nascar Races" href="https://gr8traveltips.com/nascar-races-for-family-fun/">NASCAR Races</a> </em>in Phoenix, Arizona.</p>
<p>Luckily, we had the proper collision insurance in place at the time. Because of this, everything went smoothly with the rental car company and the insurance company as well.</p>
<p><strong>This saved us a great deal of time, stress and of course, money!</strong></p>

<p>One other very important consideration to think about when renting a car is, what happens in the event of a break down? ❓ </p>
<p>❓ Is there <em>road side assistance</em> included in the event of a break down or collision? ❓</p>
<p>❓ Is it easy to contact the rental car company, arrange for transportation and receive a replacement car immediately? ❓</p>
<p><strong>If not, find a reputable car rental company that does, especially if you are traveling on business!</strong></p>

<p><b>Things you should check and do before leaving the airport, or rental lot with your car.</b>  </p>
<p>After that long tiring ✈ flight and waiting in line for your 🧳 luggage at the baggage claim carousel, it’s understandable that you just want to get into your rental car and drive away to your final destination as quickly as possible.</p>
<p>I know, we’ve been there and done that many times over the years. 😴</p>
<p><strong><span>But wait!</span></strong></p>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/national-rental-car-tips" rel="attachment wp-att-1263"><img decoding="async" title="National Rental Car Tips" alt="National Rental Car Tips" width="303" height="217" sizes="(max-width: 303px) 100vw, 303px" nitro-lazy-srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/010411_0232_Somehelpful2.jpg 303w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/010411_0232_Somehelpful2-300x215-1.jpg 300w" nitro-lazy-src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/010411_0232_Somehelpful2.jpg" class="aligncenter wp-image-1263 size-full nitro-lazy" nitro-lazy-empty id="MTA1Njo1ODU=-1" src="data:image/svg+xml;nitro-empty-id=MTA1Njo1ODU=-1;base64,PHN2ZyB2aWV3Qm94PSIwIDAgMzAzIDIxNyIgd2lkdGg9IjMwMyIgaGVpZ2h0PSIyMTciIHhtbG5zPSJodHRwOi8vd3d3LnczLm9yZy8yMDAwL3N2ZyI+PC9zdmc+" /></a></p>
<p>Here’s a list of things you <span><em>Must Check,</em></span> before exiting the car rental lot:</p>
<p>Start by making a careful;</p>
<ul readability="-0.5">
<li readability="2"><strong><em><span>Check Around The Car</span></em></strong> <br />Look for any small dents or scratches.  In the event you find some, don’t be lazy, go back to the office and ask a representative of the rental company to write down all of the previous imperfections, and ask for a copy of that report! 
<p>If there’s no one available, take 📸 pictures with the time and date on them if possible. </p>
<p>Otherwise you might be responsible for damages you didn’t perform.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><strong><em><span>Open The Trunk</span></em></strong><br />Make sure you have a spare tire, a jack and set of tools, should you be doing any remote driving. There is nothing more irritating than to find out in the middle of nowhere, that you don’t have a jack when you need to replace a flat tire.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><strong><em><span>Get An Extra Key</span></em></strong><br />If you think you need one. Just ask the customer service representative for one. This can come in handy for some of us who are absent minded.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><strong><em><span>Grab A Map</span></em> </strong><br />Spend few more minutes to plan your driving route.  Most reputable national rental car companies will provide you with local maps <em>free of charge</em>.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Just ask!</strong><br /> <br />Even with GPS these days on our 📱 cellphones and in the car, it’s always good to have a standby 🗺 map just in case and easy viewing. </p>
