<p></p>
<p>This treehouse is one of 15 beautiful treehouses on the same estate. They are all finished to a high standard, and you are bound to find one that suits your needs.<br />This one is called The Elm Treehouse, and it has 2 bedrooms and sleeps up to 4 guests.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Here&#8217;s what the owners have to say about this place&#8230;</strong></p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Embrace your time among the trees in the Elm Treehouse. This fabulous canopy lodging offers you a slightly different design than the rest of the Grove, with a wonderful view of the tranquil stream and an enclosed viewing area of the main treehouse.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Lean against the trees that come through the deck as you gaze out over the forest below. Gentle birdcalls and the sound of wind through the branches will fill your ears, as the unmistakable clean air of the Great Smoky Mountains cycles through your lungs, replacing the toxins of the city with the purity of fresh air.</p>
