<h2>Why attend vacation rental industry events?</h2>
<h3>Learning opportunities</h3>
<p>Our industry is full of opportunities to learn from others, and vacation rental events and conferences are the best way to hear other industry perspectives – even helping you solve that obstacle you’ve been facing for what feels like forever! </p>
<p>Whether you’re fresh through the industry doors or a flourished hospitality leader, there is something to gain for everyone.</p>
<h3>The power of networking</h3>
<p>Social learning is a great way to learn from others’ mistakes. These events bring together industry experts, giving you face-to-face networking opportunities. </p>
<p>So, what are the benefits of networking?</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Strengthen your business connections</strong></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><strong>Fresh ideas and new perspectives</strong></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><strong>Advice and support</strong></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><strong>Different perspectives</strong></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><strong>Build your professional profile</strong></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><strong>Share your knowledge, get your voice heard</strong></li>
</ul>
<h3>Get inspired as a short-term rental operator</h3>
<p><em>What is your short-term rental missing? Can you improve the safety of your Airbnb property? How do you attract more direct bookings? What technologies do you need to streamline your business? </em></p>
<p>If any of these questions ring true for you (or even if you’re a complete novice) then attending these events will be the inspiration you need to take your business to the next level.  </p>
<p><strong>Take a look at what vacation rental events are coming up in 2023 we think will help get you inspired.</strong></p>

<h3>1. STAMA &#038; ShortStay Greece</h3>
<p><strong>Organiser: STAMA and B&#038;B News </strong><br /><strong>Date: </strong>18th – 19th February 2023<br /><strong>Where: </strong><em>Athens, Greece<br /></em><strong>Price: </strong>Starting at €13</p>
<p>Previously known as BnB Conference Greece, <a href="https://shortstayconference.gr/en/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">ShortStay Greece</a> is an annual short-term rental conference for the Greek market of property managers, short-term rentals, villas and serviced apartments, with the aim to introduce them with solutions, services and industry experts to help improve their business processes. In sponsorship with STAMA (the institutional body of professional Greek STR’s).</p>
<p>Tickets are available <a href="https://shortstayconference.gr/en/etn/shortstay-conference/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">here</a></p>
<p>Last year, Know Your Guest was the first announced partner for the STAMA association. We again look forward to attending ShortStay Greece and inspiring and educating the Greek market.</p>
<p>Our very own Head of Partnerships will be speaking at the conference, so if you are there, make sure to come say hello.</p>
