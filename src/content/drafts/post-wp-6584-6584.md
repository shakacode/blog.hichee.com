Lets talk about how the Seasonal Rental generally works, (keep in mind rules, laws, pricing may differ state to state or country to country).</p>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<o:p></o:p></div>
</p>
<div class="MsoNormal">
A seasonal renter has the property from Nov-April.  They either pay for the five months prior to occupancy<br />
or monthly (majority pays in full prior). Utilities are the responsibility of<br />
the tenant, some owners will incorporate into the rental rate, capping<br />
heating/gas etc.  for obvious reasons.<o:p></o:p></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
</div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
</div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
Many Seasonal owners will allow extra months on either end (Sept/Oct<br />
or May/June) at discounted rates (these are usually the months where the<br />
property does not rent well in general. Properties located in your cold weather<br />
regions may offer greatly reduced rates for the winter season as they prefer<br />
not to close it up for the winter. Same goes for your warm weather regions only<br />
reversed. </div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
</div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
Whether you prefer snowcapped mountains, captivating canyons or ocean waves<br />
caressing the warm sand, A seasonal rental is becoming more popular for those<br />
who want to have their own property for the entire season.<o:p></o:p></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
</div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
If Skiing, Boarding or Snowmobiling is your passion, a seasonal<br />
is great for full time or weekend warriors. Being able to leave all your gear<br />
and equipment is a plus. Enjoy the areas winter wonderland all season with festivals &#038; local events. After a long day on the slopes, enjoy your favorite beverage in front of a crackling fire with family &#038; friends. </div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<o:p></o:p></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
</div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
Maybe your passion is walking the beach, golfing or fishing.<br />
Finding that perfect property in the warmer climates accommodates those preferring<br />
to be closer to the sun. What’s not to love when you can buy fresh fruits &#038;<br />
vegetables from local markets. The Seafood truck is always around the corner selling their catch of the day. <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;"> </span>Whether you enjoy a game of golf,  relaxing on the beach with your favorite book, or hiking trails,  a seasonal is something you can call home for the months rented. <o:p></o:p></div>

</div>
