With a tropically landscaped backyard that&#8217;s more oasis than yard, you&#8217;ve got the ultimate setup for making those Insta stories pop.</p>
<p>It boasts of a pool with its own swim-up bar (yes, you heard that right), so you can sip something chilled from the wine fridge while gathering around the firepit or melting stress away in the hot tub. And when the island breeze calls, your covered deck is the spot to answer.</p>
<p>Indoors, you&#8217;re greeted with chic rustic nautical decor and an open space that’s all about vibing together. From the living room&#8217;s dry bar setup to a kitchen begging for a cook-off (Wolf oven included), it’s dialed in for chill nights and social spice.</p>
<p>Bedroom scenes range from a primary suite with spa feels to a loft that’s a kids&#8217; hideaway dream, ensuring everyone&#8217;s got their chill zone. And when you’re ready to hit the sand, Holmes Beach is just a flip-flop away.</p>
