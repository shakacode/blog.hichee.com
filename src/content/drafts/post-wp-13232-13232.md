<p>This is a volcanic archipelago about 350 kilometers off Brazil&#8217;s northeast coast. It’s named after its largest island, a protected national marine park and ecological sanctuary with a jagged coastline and diverse ecosystems. It&#8217;s renowned for its undeveloped beaches, and for scuba diving and snorkeling. Sea turtles, rays, dolphins, and reef sharks swim in its warm, clear waters.</p>
