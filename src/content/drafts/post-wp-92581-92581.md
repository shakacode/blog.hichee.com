<p>Get ready to elevate your getaway game with this pet-friendly, cedar-built treehouse, nestled 20 feet above the trees. Perfect for a couple and their furry sidekick, this pine-tastic retreat is all about blending rustic charm with an airy, modern vibe.</p>



<p>This treehouse is a double deal, offering two units with soundproof walls for peace and quiet. It&#8217;s a private escape in the sky, where you and your pet can chill out, enjoying the views without any disturbance from the outside world.</p>



<p>Cooking here is an outdoor affair. Whip a meal on the grill or outdoor counter with a convenient sink for easy clean-up. The treehouse’s interior keeps the pet-friendly theme going strong. It&#8217;s filled with all the essentials – a hot plate, microwave, Keurig, and fridge. You won’t miss a beat in this nature-inspired culinary setup.</p>



<p>The adventure continues downstairs in your private bathroom, fully equipped for all your needs. The interior&#8217;s warm cedar and wormwood walls and rustic accents back upstairs make it the perfect spot to unwind.</p>
