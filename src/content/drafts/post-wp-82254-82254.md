<p class="has-larken-font-family has-text-xxl-font-size">There’s never a bad time to book a holiday but Black Friday may be one of the best, especially when you can save up to 20% on flights with Etihad Airways. Starting on Friday 24 November 2023, you can save a fifth of your airfare when you <a href="https://www.skyscanner.net/airline/airline-etihad-airways-ey.html">fly with Etihad</a>. </p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity" />
<p>You can enjoy savings of <strong>up to 20% on flights</strong> to a selection of stunning worldwide destinations. Ready for a new, unforgettable adventure? Grab an unbeatable Black Friday deal when you book before 27 November for outbound travel from <strong>15th January 2024 to 12th June 2024</strong>. Where will you go next?</p>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Where does Etihad fly from in the UK?</h2>
<p>Etihad offers flights from two airports in England: <a href="https://www.skyscanner.net/transport/flights/lhr/auh/">London Heathrow</a> and <a href="https://www.skyscanner.net/transport/flights/man/auh/">Manchester</a>. For those across the Irish Sea, there are also flights available from <a href="https://www.skyscanner.net/transport/flights/dub/auh/">Dublin</a>.</p>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Where can you fly to with Etihad?</h2>
<p>From those three airports in the British Isles, the only direct flight with Etihad Airways goes to their base in Abu Dhabi. Once in the UAE, you can catch a connecting flight to many countries, including many destinations in Africa, Asia and Australia. Here are a few of our favourites:</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity" />
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Melbourne, Australia</h3>
<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/shutterstock_1426038686.jpg" alt="Melbourne Flinders Street Train Station with moving tram in Australia at sunset" class="wp-image-2D772DFA-26B4-420B-8F6BB82C1F73A6CF" /></figure>
<p>Melbourne is one of two Australian cities you can fly to with Etihad (the other being Sydney). And while Sydney has many of the famous sites — like the Opera House — Melbourne oozes charm and creativity. Plus, it’s home to some of the country’s biggest sporting arenas, a colony of wild penguins (yes, really!) and close to the starting point for the Great Ocean Road, one of Australia’s most iconic road trips.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity" />
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Mahé, Seychelles</h3>
<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/seychelles-spiaggia.jpg" alt="A beach in the Seychelles" class="wp-image-6ACD86E1-0D2A-4BA1-9E4DF2B7644A4593" /></figure>
<p>Just a short flight from <a href="https://www.skyscanner.net/airline/airline-etihad-airways-ey.html">Abu Dhabi</a> is Seychelles, an idyllic island chain in the Indian Ocean. There’s not much to do here, but that’s kind of the point. Book a coastal resort and enjoy the best rest of your life. Lie on one of the many picture-perfect beaches, rest under palm trees and marvel at the wildlife — the Seychelles is the only place outside the Galapagos Islands where you can see ancient giant tortoises.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity" />
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Seoul, South Korea</h3>
<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/shutterstock_487257514.jpg" alt="Autumn at Gyeongbokgung Palace in seoul,Korea." class="wp-image-0F6E1FB3-7035-4D43-B8719A179DEED561" /></figure>
<p>Etihad flies to dozens of Asian destinations so it’s hard to pick just one. South Korea’s capital is a great choice as it’s a city that has it all. Marvel at the technological side of one of the world’s most advanced cities — but one that hasn’t forgotten its past. In between towering skyscrapers, you’ll find temples that are thousands of years old. You also have nature hikes within the city’s bounds and plenty of oddities, like puppy cafes, for a unique flavour.</p>
