<p>Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you plan the perfect holiday with Layla, the new AI trip planner from Beautiful Destinations. </p>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-left has-text-h-3-font-size" id="voxblock"><strong>Start with inspiration</strong></h2>
<p>Feeling stuck in a travel rut? Use AI to sprinkle a bit of inspiration into your wanderlust-filled soul.</p>
<p>Start by telling Layla what you’re looking for, or simply ask for inspiration. As an AI travel planner, Layla scans through a wide range of <a href="https://www.skyscanner.net/news">travel content</a>, asking you to answer a few prompts so she can find you the coolest and most underrated spots.</p>
<p>AIs don’t just guess; they’re powered by data wizardry. Crunching through vast amounts of travel data, from user reviews to real-time local information, ensuring that your trip suggestions are as fresh as that croissant you’re about to devour in a Parisian café. Layla isn’t just a normal AI, she has her own unique travel data to harness so you can trust her to guide you to the hottest spots and hidden gems alike.</p>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading has-text-h-3-font-size"><strong>Feel the destination’s vibe</strong></h2>
<p>For every destination Layla recommends for your holiday, she’ll show you videos so you can quickly see if it’s your sort of place.
