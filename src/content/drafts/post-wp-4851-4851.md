<p>Since 2007 (the birth of Airbnb), the vacation rental industry has been rapidly expanding in size and popularity not just within the US, but globally. With that, the need for trust and protection is now more important than ever. That’s where SUPERHOG comes in.</p>
<p>You want to know you’re safe, protected, and will have a good booking – that goes for both hosts and guests. After all, we’re all strangers.</p>
<p>Who is that guest you’re hosting in your home?</p>
<p>Who is the host of the home you have booked?</p>
<p>We ask these questions but what do we really find out? We place our trust in the major booking sites, yet what they consider guest verification and protection is vastly different from company to company. <h3>What is a SaaS platform?</h3>
<p>A SaaS platform stands for ‘software as a solution’. This is key to what SUPERHOG is doing; however, do you understand what software as a solution really means?</p>
<p>It may just sound like further jargon that doesn’t fully explain the ins and outs of what it is and how it works. It may be tricky to wrap your head around, but it doesn’t have to be.</p>
<p>Let’s break down how SUPERHOG works as a SaaS platform. </p>
<h4><strong>The software</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>This is the process that the guest will go through to become verified. This includes biometric face recognition and ID cross-referencing along with a liveness test which allows SUPERHOG to ascertain whether the ID is a) valid and b) whether it belongs to the individual. Our system then matches the information we’ve been given to that of the guest, including their name and email to check for any anomalies. With the findings, SUPERHOG can raise flags to the property manager or host and tell them to not take the booking. </li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>The solution </strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>The solution for SUPERHOG is in the form of financial protection. This is the $5,000,000 guarantee (embedded insurance) in case something does go wrong with a verified booking. Our in-house resolutions team will be on hand to offer you the support to resolve the issue on your behalf. </li>
</ul>
