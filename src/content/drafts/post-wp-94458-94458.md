<p>I&#8217;m in many Facebook and LinkedIn groups for Airbnb hosts and vacation rental managers, and I&#8217;m seeing many people complaining about a lack of bookings. </p>
<p>To be honest, I&#8217;m not surprised. </p>
<p>Supply is outstripping demand. The number of listings just keeps on growing. Here at HiChee, we collate all of Airbnb&#8217;s, Booking.com&#8217;s, and Vrbo&#8217;s listings. As of today, those 3 companies have 14,695,254 short-term rental listings between them. </p>
<p><strong>This number is increasing by around 4,000 listings a day! </strong></p>
<p>With such fierce competition, it&#8217;s no surprise that getting bookings is becoming more and more of a lottery.</p>
<p>In this article, I will show you some ways that you can increase your odds of snagging more bookings. These methods really work, so read on&#8230;</p>

<p>The pet-friendly niche is the easiest niche to enter. There’s little to no start up cost and better still, pet friendly vacation home owners can charge a premium of 20% to 30% above the average rent in a specific area.</p>
<p><strong>Pet-friendly:</strong> Fenced yards, pet beds, welcome gifts, walking trails, pet-sitting services.</p>
<p>The specific percentage increase in bookings you could expect by becoming pet-friendly depends on various factors, including your location, the demand for pet-friendly accommodations in your area, the type of property you have, and the overall competition. However, becoming pet-friendly can significantly broaden your market appeal, as a notable portion of travelers prefer or need to travel with their pets.</p>
<p>A few points to consider:</p>
<p>1. **Market Demand**: The demand for pet-friendly accommodations has been on the rise. According to various travel and hospitality industry reports, a considerable number of travelers consider their pets as part of the family and prefer accommodations that welcome pets.</p>
<p>2. **Limited Supply**: Pet-friendly accommodations are often in shorter supply compared to the overall market. By catering to pet owners, you tap into a niche market with less competition and higher demand.</p>
<p>3. **Potential for Premium Pricing**: Properties that are pet-friendly can sometimes charge a premium, including pet fees or higher cleaning fees, which can increase your revenue per booking.</p>
<p>4. **Longer Stays**: Pet owners might also tend to book longer stays, as arranging for pet-friendly travel can be more cumbersome and thus less frequent.</p>
<p>5. **Repeat Business**: If pet owners find a comfortable and welcoming place to stay with their pets, they are likely to return or recommend it to others, enhancing your bookings through word-of-mouth.</p>
<p>While there isn&#8217;t a universal percentage increase applicable to all Airbnb listings, studies and anecdotal evidence suggest that making your property pet-friendly could potentially increase your bookings by a significant margin. Some property owners report an increase in occupancy rates by 10% to 20% or more after allowing pets, but this varies widely by location and property type.</p>
<p>To maximize the potential increase in bookings:</p>
<p>&#8211; **Market Your Property**: Clearly state in your listing that you’re pet-friendly and highlight any pet-specific amenities you offer, such as bowls, beds, or a fenced yard.</p>
<p>&#8211; **Set Clear Policies**: Have a clear pet policy regarding the size, number, and type of pets allowed, and communicate any extra fees upfront.</p>
<p>&#8211; **Prepare Your Property**: Ensure your property is equipped to handle pets, considering durable furnishings and easy-to-clean surfaces.</p>
<p>Ultimately, becoming pet-friendly is a strategic decision that should align with your business goals, target market, and personal preferences regarding pet accommodation.</p>
