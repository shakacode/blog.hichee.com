<p>This location is one of those unusual places that are more bizarre than normal, making it one of the best vacation spots for nature lovers. It is an archipelago that hosts countless towering dragon blood trees, which fan upwards like an umbrella or a giant, branch-filled mushroom. There is a grand total of 825 rare species of flora on this island, and a good third of them can only be seen in this small locale.</p>
