<p>Located on southern Thailand’s west coast, this province is characterized by craggy, sheer limestone cliffs, dense mangrove forests, and over a hundred offshore islands. Its most popular beach destinations include the Phi Phi Islands, which jut from the sea like giant rainforested boulders, and Railay Beach, accessible only by boat and a prominent rock-climbing spot.</p>
