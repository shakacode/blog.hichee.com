<p>This place is also known as &#8220;sheep islands&#8221; are 18 islands in the North East Atlantic. The beautiful green islands are a self-governing part of the Danish Realm. These are an archipelago of 18 islands that were shaped by volcanic activity and the glaciers of the ice age. Seventeen of the islands are inhabited, and they are well-connected with paved roads, tunnels, bridges, and ferry lines.</p>
