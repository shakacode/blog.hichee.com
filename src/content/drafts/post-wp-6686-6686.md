<p>Dear hosts, it has been ohh so long since I was in your lovely ears. How long? Well, spring is in full force in NYC, I sold Eveland 1.0, I already bought 3 houses for Eveland 2.0 and I have lost my hair due to chemotherapy. If you have no clue what I’m talking about, go and listen to <a href="https://www.thehostingjourney.com/plan-c/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Episode 123: No Heat, a Leak, and Plan C</a> so you can catch up.</p>
<p>Before you leave, stop and listen to today&#8217;s episode, “5 Key Questions Everyone Should Answer.”</p>
<p>In this episode, I talk with Michelle from Cityami. Whether you’re just starting your short-term rental journey, you’re thinking of selling or you have been hosting for a while, this episode is for you.</p>
</div>

<p>If you’re starting out it’s a good thing to ponder the question of why you want to get into the short-term rental world. Believe me, it isn’t for the thin-skinned or faint of heart person.</p>
<p>Thinking of selling? Do you want to invest in another property? These questions can guide you in that decision.</p>
<p>Have you been hosting for a while and jumped in feet first without much thought? (My hand is going up here.) Sometimes it’s good to stop and create a plan, even if you’re in the middle of your journey. </p>
<p>In 5 Key Questions, Everyone Should Answer, Michelle urged me to answer these questions as I was preparing to invest up to $1.2M in cash. Yes, at the time of this recording I hadn’t sold Eveland 1.0 yet. We were starting the 1031 adventure. The answers dictated the journey we were embarking on. So listen up to 5 Key Questions Everyone Should Answer.</p>
