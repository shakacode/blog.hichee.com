<p>Like its name suggests, this place is home to incredible baobabs that reach up into the sky at 100-foot heights. Each of these ancient trees appears surreal and almost alien-like in aesthetic, as if they leaped right out of the pages of The Little Prince, where they are so whimsically featured. This place sits on a rural island in Madagascar, which also hosts unique wildlife, including lemurs that stare at you with wide, big eyes and some of the world&#8217;s largest moth species.</p>
