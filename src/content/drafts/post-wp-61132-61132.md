<p>Step into a world of pure opulence at Villa Roca, crowned by Conde Nast Traveller as the most luxurious villa in Ibiza and hailed by Harper&#8217;s Bazaar as one of the four most fabulous villas worldwide. This sanctuary, just a quick hop from the best of Ibiza, delivers an unmatched experience with a concierge, personal chef, butlers, and housekeepers. </p>



<p>Each of its 8 suites and 10 bathrooms boasts grandeur, treating guests to breathtaking views and deluxe amenities. Indulge in the rooftop hot tub or unwind in the hydro-massage spa baths. Keep fit in the gym or detox in the infra-red sauna, all while enjoying full spa services. It also features a 32m swimming pool with a floating cabana and a mist system that sprays cooling mineral water, perfect for sunny days. </p>



<p>Guests can dine al fresco in the rooftop dining area or relax in the Bedouin chill-out tent. With 9 terraces, 4 fireplaces, a dance floor, a sky lounge, and unmatched views, Villa Roca sets the gold standard in luxury.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading">From the owner:</h4>



<p>Villa Roca is considered the most Luxurious and exclusive villa in Ibiza (Conde Nast Traveller) and has been voted one of the 4th most fabulous villas in the World (Harper’s Bazaar). This is the ultimate vacation destination in the Balearic, offering the services of a luxury hotel within the privacy of a private villa. Built on its own mountain with no near neighbors, there are seven personal staff to cater to your every desire. The chef can prepare a selection of local organic ingredients and local seafood (food costs extra), and butlers provide a round-the-clock service.</p>
