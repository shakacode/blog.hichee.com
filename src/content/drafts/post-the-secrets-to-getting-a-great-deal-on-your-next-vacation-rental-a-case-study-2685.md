<h1 class="wp-block-heading">Getting a great deal on your next vacation rental</h1>



<p>When it comes to booking a vacation rental, nearly all travelers do the same thing.</p>



<p>They simply visit their Online Travel Agency (OTA) of choice. This may be Airbnb, Booking (.com), or VRBO. <br>They then search for their destination, enter dates and number of guests and shortlist a number of properties that suit their taste and needs.<br>They then select one from that list and book.<br><br>They make their final decision based on a number of criteria ranging from price, location, amenities, and so on.</p>



<p>Price is almost always a factor. We all have a budget in mind. <br>If we see two similar properties in the same location and one is substantially more expensive, most of us will select the lower-priced property. <strong>Why would we pay more than we need to?</strong></p>



<p>What most travelers don&#8217;t realize is that they can often get a better price for the property that they have selected &#8211; Just by doing a quick price comparison.</p>



<p>In this article, I&#8217;ll show you an example where a friend saved thousands of dollars in just a few minutes. I&#8217;ll also show you exactly what to look out for so that you are assured of getting the best deal, every time.</p>

<div class="wp-block-getwid-advanced-heading has-custom-font-size" style="font-size:22px"><span class="wp-block-getwid-advanced-heading__content"><strong>Not looking for deals</strong></span></div>



<p>The strange thing is that people spend time looking for the best deals on the (relatively) small ticket items like flights and car hire but very few look for deals on the big ticket item of the accommodation itself.<br><br>Most travelers don&#8217;t realize that many hosts/vacation rental owners market their properties on multiple websites and that prices for the same property, for the same dates, and for the same number of guests can be wildly different. <br><br>In fact, looking at the property that Charles is looking at we can see that it&#8217;s available via four different sites, Airbnb, VRBO, Marriott, and CB Island Vacations (the management company). </p>



<p>Prices vary between $10,333 and $18,421. That&#8217;s a range of $8,088</p>
