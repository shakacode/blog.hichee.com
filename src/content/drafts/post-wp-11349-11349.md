<p>Naturally if you Google a topic, you’re bound to find something to match your search. Well, I not only found a website that – <em>💵 Displays Real Time Gas Prices</em>, but it’s an 🏆 award winning website as well!</p>
<p>It’s called {GasBuddy} and it definitely should be used if you’re looking for the best gas prices around. We own a 🚌 Class C Motor Home and with an average of – 12 to 14 miles per gallon, I’m always on the lookout for best gas prices.</p>

<p>What I find remarkable coming from 🍁 Canada, is that the price of gas in the United States can vary greatly from – one station to next. </p>
<p>You can find different 🏆 prices even at the same intersection of a town or city in the U.S!</p>
<p>So using this app when traveling, I have worked out in our travels, that I can save up to 10 cents a gallon on average. </p>
<p>I can do this by spending a few moments on this website, searching for a station with the best price on our chosen route.</p>

<p>All you have to do is register, sign-in whenever you can and list the gas prices for the stations in your area.</p>
<p>What I really like, is you can get – {real time and up to date gas prices,} wherever you may be traveling. This is how you can take advantage of the best prices offered, in that particular area.</p>
<p><strong>A handy resource helping everyone save money at the ⛽ pumps!</strong></p>
<p>GasBuddy is loaded with great stuff! By registering for free, you can have your own member’s page. If so inclined, you can submit pictures, add a profile and much more!</p>

<p>They also have a handy – {trip cost calculator,} on their website. It helps you in planning your journey and what stations along the way, offering the best fuel prices at that time.</p>
<p>This is where you can plan out your trip, select your vehicle from a drop down list and get driving directions to your destination.  </p>
<p>With a click of the mouse, the trip calculator will guide you to the cheapest gas prices available at that time.</p>
<p>If you’re planning a long road trip, the savings could be huge!</p>
<p>The calculator will even show your gas savings, by using the stations suggested along the way! </p>
<p>All of this, because of the little time registered members take to inform the website of gas prices for that day and time in their community.</p>
