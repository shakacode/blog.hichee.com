<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-airbnb-alternatives-nbsp-hichee-com">Airbnb Alternatives:&nbsp;Hichee.com</h3>
</p>
<p>As most people know, there are many alternative websites to Airbnb.  Booking.com. Vrbo, Tripadvisor, Expedia, and Ownersdirect are just a handful. There are hundreds of other sites that compete with Airbnb.</p>
<p>What makes HiChee different is that it compares prices across the big three online travel agencies (OTAs) &#8211; Airbnb, Booking.com (BDC), and Vrbo, and shows you the best deal for any property. </p>
<p><strong>Think Skyscanner for short-term rentals.</strong></p>
<p>It also helps you find direct booking options that offer even lower prices.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-key-takeaways">Key takeaways</h3>
</p>
<p>Numerous Airbnb listings are also featured on alternative platforms, often without maintaining consistent pricing. You may be overpaying if you don&#8217;t check.</p>
<p>The average price difference in these cases is 13%, so the average saving on HiChee is $130 per booking.</p>
<p>The prices shown on HiChee include taxes and extra fees (like cleaning fees), so you see the price you pay.</p>
<p>HiChee empowers you with powerful tools to unlock direct booking options, allowing you to engage directly with the host or property manager. In 99% of cases, these represent the lowest-priced online deals, offering unmatched value.</p>
<p>HiChee offers a free Chrome extension that helps you find these deals while browsing Airbnb or Vrbo.</p>
<p>Leverage the power of HiChee&#8217;s Triplist feature (currently in beta) to craft your personalized property wishlist. Stay ahead with real-time price alerts, ensuring you never miss out on the best booking deals—whether prices soar or drop.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-the-basics">The basics</h2>
</p>
<p>As of today (March 2024), there are 8,327,731 listings on Airbnb, 5,587,720 on Booking,com, and 1,982,281 on Vrbo.</p>
<p>However, well over a million of these listings appear on two or more of these websites. And when these cross-listings happen, they are rarely listed at the same price.</p>
<p>HiChee has collated all of the listings from these sites, and when you add your dates and the number of guests, HiChee compares prices and shows you the best deal. </p>

<h4 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-company-backgrounds">Company Backgrounds</h4>
</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Airbnb</strong></li>
</p>
<li>Founded in 2008 in San Francisco, Airbnb evolved from renting an air mattress to becoming a global travel marketplace. It disrupted the traditional lodging industry by connecting travelers with unique local stays. </li>
</p>
<li>Today, Airbnb operates in over 220 countries, offering everything from rooms to castles, symbolizing its rapid growth from a simple idea to a major player in the global travel economy. It offers just under 8 million short-term rentals.</li>
</ul>
</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Hichee.com</strong>
<ul>
<li>HiChee takes a different approach. It was founded in 2017 by Justin Gordon. He had the idea of collating all of Airbnb&#8217;s, Booking.com&#8217;s, and Vrbo&#8217;s inventory and building a matching engine so users could see the best price for properties listed on two or more sites.</li>
</p>
<li>This was groundbreaking as there is virtually always a price difference between platforms, and these differentials can be huge.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
