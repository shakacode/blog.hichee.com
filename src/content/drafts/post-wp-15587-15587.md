<p>This place is a world-famous black-sand beach on the South Coast of Iceland, just beside Vik í Myrdal, the southernmost village on the mainland of Iceland. With its enormous basalt stacks, roaring Atlantic waves, and stunning panoramas, This place is widely considered to be the most beautiful example of Iceland’s black sand beaches. In 1991, National Geographic voted it one of the Top 10 non-tropical beaches to visit on the planet.</p>
