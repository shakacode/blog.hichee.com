<p>The first step is to research what’s going on in your local area and find out which events might attract potential guests. Types of events to look out for include: music festivals and gigs, sporting fixtures, national holidays or business conferences. Many cities have websites dedicated specifically for this purpose. You can also research any major organisations located in the area, such as music venues, universities, sports teams, etc., and check their websites for upcoming events. It’s also helpful to join local Facebook groups or follow hashtags related to your city on Twitter and Instagram in order to stay up-to-date on any event announcements. </p>
