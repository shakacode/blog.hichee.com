<p><strong>More bookings!</strong></p>
<p>With a new channel, we automatically increase the likelihood of generating you more bookings! Unlike <a href="https://www.booking.com/">Booking.com</a> or <a href="https://www.expedia.co.uk/">Expedia</a>, <a href="https://www.holidu.co.uk/">Holidu</a> is exclusively a <b>vacation rental </b>platform, not catering to hotels, hostels or B&#038;Bs. This means that there is less competition from alternative accommodation options, and you will reach only those looking to rent a whole property. With over 30 million users on the website each month, and 100k+ in monthly bookings, <a href="https://www.holidu.co.uk/">Holidu</a> represents a great opportunity for hosts to acquire more customers.</p>
<p><strong>Extended reach</strong></p>
<p><a href="https://www.holidu.co.uk/">Holidu</a> has a huge reach in terms of potential guests. Their target audience is mainland Europe which we know is also a key market for our hosts. With a presence in Germany, France, Spain and the UK, <a href="https://www.holidu.co.uk/">Holidu</a> is the ideal partner for expanding your reach globally.</p>
