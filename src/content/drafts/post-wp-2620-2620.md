<h2 class="wp-block-heading">What is NEXT?</h2>
</p>
<p>NEXT is TravelNet Solutions’ annual customer get-together. It’s an energizing forum for growth-oriented hospitality leaders to learn, collaborate, and push boundaries by sharing best practices with one another.</p>
<p>This year’s theme is “For the Love of Hospitality.” Love drives us to transform how hospitality works. It helped us get through the ups and downs of the past couple years, and it will keep pushing us to grow. This conference will celebrate and honor the fact that we love what we do.</p>
<p>NEXT brings together industry experts and power users from around North America to share their secrets for driving results. Join us and hundreds of other vacation rental and hotel professionals in Minneapolis on September 18-20, 2022 to show your love of the hospitality industry. We can’t wait to partner with you!</p>
