<p><a href="https://www.nomadicmatt.com/travel-guides/spain-travel-tips/barcelona/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Barcelona</a> is a vibrant, energetic city known for amazing food, beautiful architecture, and wild nightlife. It’s also a place that appeals to everyone: foodies, partiers, history buffs, night owls, and everyone in between will find <a href="https://www.nomadicmatt.com/travel-blogs/things-to-see-and-do-in-barcelona/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">something to see and do here</a>. </p>
<p>From introductory free tours to in-depth history and food tours, I never get tired of exploring and learning about Barcelona. It’s a city with layers and layers of history and culture that most visitors barely scratch the surface of.</p>
<p>To help you plan your trip, here’s my list of the best walking tours in Barcelona. </p>
<div class="top-choice">
<h5>Best Overall Tour</h5>
<div class="top-wrap" readability="9.246062992126">
<div class="top-right" readability="14.571428571429">
<h4>Take Walks</h4>
<p>Take Walks is my go-to tour company in Barcelona, offering access to places that no other company gets and incredibly knowledgeable guides. I always have fun and learn a ton on these tours.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.takewalks.com/barcelona-tours/complete-gaudi-tour-casa-batllo?tap_a=29895-287a1f&amp;tap_s=37909-476f1e" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">The Gaudí tour</a> is the most in-depth and comprehensive one available, but there’s also <a href="https://www.takewalks.com/barcelona-tours/sagrada-familia-tour?tap_a=29892-fbcc32&amp;tap_s=37909-476f1e" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">an educational skip-the-line tour of La Sagrada Família</a>, the city’s biggest tourist draw. If you want to visit this cathedral (and you should, it’s gorgeous), this tour is a must. Tours start at 53 EUR. </p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="top-choice">
<h5>Best Free Tour</h5>
<div class="top-wrap" readability="7.1073298429319">
<div class="top-right" readability="9.7304582210243">
<h4>New Europe</h4>
<p><a href="https://www.neweuropetours.eu/barcelona-walking-tours/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">New Europe</a> is one of the most popular free walking tour companies in all of Europe. Its main offering is a solid introduction lasting around 2.5 hours and covering all the highlights. It’s a great way to kick-start your trip if you’re on a budget. There’s also a free tour that focuses on Gaudi and modernism. Just make sure to tip your guide at the end!</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<p> <br />While those may be the best free and paid tours in town, Barcelona has a lot more to offer. Here are six other insightful and educational tours: </p>
<h3>1. Devour Food Tours</h3>
<p><a href="https://devourtours.com/destinations/spain/barcelona/?tap_a=107745-9dfcf2&amp;tap_s=37909-476f1e" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">Devour</a> is my favorite food tour company. Its offerings are always super insightful and delicious. In Barcelona, Devour runs a few different tours, so there’s something for everyone. </p>
<p>If you’re a history buff like me, the <a href="https://devourtours.com/tours/barcelona-tapas-taverns-history-tour/?tap_a=107735-85ed38&amp;tap_s=37909-476f1e" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">Tapas, Taverns &#038; History Tour</a> is the best choice for you. It lasts over three hours and includes some mouthwatering tapas as you learn about Barcelona’s expansive history.</p>
<p>There’s also an <a href="https://devourtours.com/tours/barcelona-gourmet-tapas-wine-tour/?tap_a=107733-15b20f&amp;tap_s=37909-476f1e" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">evening wine and tapas tour</a>, as well as a <a href="https://devourtours.com/tours/barcelona-day-trip-to-montserrat-and-winery/?tap_a=128912-457d87&amp;tap_s=37909-476f1e" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">day trip to Monserrat</a> that includes visiting a winery.</p>
<p><em>Tours start at 87 EUR.</em></p>
<p><center><a class="bookhostel" href="https://devourtours.com/destinations/spain/barcelona/?tap_a=107745-9dfcf2&amp;tap_s=37909-476f1e" rel="nofollow">Book here!</a></center><br /> </p>
<h3>2. Golden Tour Guide </h3>
<p>This tour company offers skip-the-line, in-depth tours of Barcelona’s major attractions. If you want to learn about Gaudí, <a href="https://www.getyourguide.com/barcelona-l45/barcelona-park-guell-guided-tour-with-skip-the-line-access-t419045/?partner_id=LLKQJ38&amp;utm_medium=online_publisher" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">this 75-minute Park Güell tour</a> (with skip-the-line access) is the best way to do so. You’ll get to wander the beautiful (and popular) park, admiring all the incredible mosaics and nature-inspired creations, while learning about the master artisan from an expert local guide. This is one of the most popular sights in town, so learning about it from an expert (and skipping the line) is key! </p>
<p><em>Tours start at 22 EUR.</em></p>
<p><center><a class="bookhostel" href="https://www.getyourguide.com/barcelona-l45/barcelona-park-guell-guided-tour-with-skip-the-line-access-t419045/?partner_id=LLKQJ38&amp;utm_medium=online_publisher" rel="nofollow">Book here!</a></center><br /> </p>
<h3>3. Travel Bound</h3>
<p>You can’t visit Barcelona without doing two things: eating tapas and watching a flamenco show. To make things easy, Travel Bound runs a cool tour in the Gothic Quarter where you can <a href="https://www.getyourguide.com/barcelona-l45/tapas-and-flamenco-experience-t66363/?partner_id=LLKQJ38&amp;utm_medium=online_publisher" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">watch an amazing flamenco show and snack on delicious tapas</a>. It’s the perfect tour for travelers who love learning about culture and want to soak up a more traditional slice of life.</p>
<p>Travel Bound also organizes a <a href="https://www.getyourguide.com/gothic-quarter-barcelona-l3028/barcelona-ghosts-and-legends-tour-t312833/?partner_id=LLKQJ38&amp;utm_medium=online_publisher" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">spooky ghost tour</a> on which you’ll hear about hauntings, gruesome crimes, and other eerie tales from Barcelona’s dark past. It’s just 90 minutes long and a great way to learn about the city after you’ve visited the main sights.</p>
<p><em>Tours start at 17 EUR.</em></p>
<p><center><a class="bookhostel" href="https://www.getyourguide.com/barcelona-l45/tapas-and-flamenco-experience-t66363/?partner_id=LLKQJ38&amp;utm_medium=online_publisher" rel="nofollow">Book here!</a></center><br /> </p>
<h3>4. Turisme de Barcelona</h3>
<p>Barcelona is an arty place, with lots of great museums, cool galleries, and stunning architecture. If you’re an art fan and want to explore more of this side of the city, Turisme de Barcelona runs a <a href="https://www.getyourguide.com/barcelona-l45/2-hour-picasso-walking-tour-museum-entrance-in-barcelona-t22736/?partner_id=LLKQJ38&amp;utm_medium=online_publisher" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">two-hour Picasso walking tour</a> that takes you around his old neighborhood and favorite haunts. From there, you’ll then visit the popular Picasso Museum, which is home to a vast collection of his works (there are over 4,000 pieces here). You’ll get skip-the-line entry too. If you’re a history buff or art fan, this super informative tour is a fun way to explore one of Barcelona’s many niches.</p>
<p><em>Tours start at 35 EUR.</em></p>
<p><center><a class="bookhostel" href="https://www.getyourguide.com/barcelona-l45/2-hour-picasso-walking-tour-museum-entrance-in-barcelona-t22736/?partner_id=LLKQJ38&amp;utm_medium=online_publisher" rel="nofollow">Book here!</a></center><br /> </p>
<h3>5. Original Europe Tours</h3>
<p>If you want to experience Barcelona’s energetic nightlife, head out on this <a href="https://www.getyourguide.com/barcelona-l45/barcelona-pub-crawl-t467460/?partner_id=LLKQJ38&amp;utm_medium=online_publisher" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">pub crawl and club tour</a>. You’ll visit at least four spots (including skip-the-line entry) and get a few complimentary shots as you bounce from bar to bar. The tour lasts five hours, ensuring that you’ll get to soak up as much of the nightlife as possible. There’s even the option to add an open bar to your tour for just 20 EUR! Naturally, this tour is filled with mostly young backpackers, but if you want to meet people and have fun, it’s worth checking out.</p>
<p><em>Tours start at 27 EUR.</em> </p>
<p><center><a class="bookhostel" href="https://www.getyourguide.com/barcelona-l45/barcelona-pub-crawl-t467460/?partner_id=LLKQJ38&amp;utm_medium=online_publisher" rel="nofollow">Book here!</a></center><br /> <br /><center>***</center></p>
<p><a href="https://www.nomadicmatt.com/travel-guides/spain-travel-tips/barcelona/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Barcelona</a> is one of the most popular cities in <a href="https://www.nomadicmatt.com/travel-guides/europe-travel-tips/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Europe</a>. And for good reason: it’s a gorgeous, energetic place that is bursting at the seams with amazing food, cool sights, and tons of history. By taking one of the walking tours above, you’ll be sure to have a fun, informative, and authentic visit to Spain’s most popular metropolis.<br /> </p>
