<p>TravelCon is back! In 2022, after five years (through a pandemic), I ended TravelCon. However, my friends at <a href="https://finconexpo.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">FinCon</a> offered to run it (thus reducing my workload) so we brought it back! If you’re media or PR in the travel industry, this is the best industry event for you to attend!</p>
<p><a href="https://travelcon.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">TravelCon</a> is a three-day travel media event that focuses on helping you improve your craft, learn new business skills, network with your peers, and connect you with all the awesome brands and tourism boards that attend!</p>
<p>This year, TravelCon will be May 15-17th in Portland, Oregon and will feature pre- and post-event trips; meet-ups; dinner parties; and in-depth, actionable talks. The goal is to help travel creators go pro by turning their passion into a profession and then scale their online business.</p>
<p>At TravelCon, you can:</p>
<ul>
<li>Improve your craft in the four major areas of travel: video, photography, writing, and blogging</li>
<li>Learn what’s hot, what works, and what doesn’t in the industry</li>
<li>Keep current on the best practices in digital travel publishing</li>
<li>Learn about new products and services in the industry</li>
<li>Meet destinations and travel brands</li>
<li>Learn from experts outside the travel industry</li>
<li>Make connections with other travel lovers</li>
<li>Have a ton of fun!!!</li>
</ul>
<h3>What can you expect this year?</h3>
<p>TravelCon is <em>the</em> business event for travel creators. The TravelCon sessions this year are going to focus on two things: going pro and scaling.</p>
<p>“Go Pro” sessions will empower part-time creators to go full-time with topics such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>Expanding your reach using advanced SEO, ads, &#038; email marketing.</li>
<li>Discovering creator tools and services to streamline your workflow.</li>
<li>Diversifying how you monetize your content, including affiliate marketing and collaborations.</li>
<li>Building effective partnerships with CVBs, DMOs, travel boards, travel brands, and local agencies.</li>
</ul>
<p>“Scale” sessions are for full-time creators who are ready to scale their brand, reach, content, and profit, with topics such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>Equipping your brand with the right team members, tools, and services.</li>
<li>Diversifying your revenue streams, including product development, courses, or membership communities.</li>
<li>Maintaining balance to avoiding burnout as a full-time creator and digital nomad.</li>
<li>Leveraging AI &#038; other emerging tech for content creation &#038; business growth.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Networking Opportunities</h3>
<p>Connecting brands and creators is at the core of our event. We provide lots of opportunities to “find your people” and do business in a way that feels natural and organic.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Expo</strong> – Featuring Travel Boards, Brands, CVBs, DMOs, creator service providers, and more — many with specially-curated offers, partnerships, and products to discuss with attendees. Over 40 brands and DMOs are attending this year!</li>
<li><strong>1:1 Meetings</strong> – Schedule one-on-one meetings with brands, sponsors, and travel boards using our event software. All attendees can participate!</li>
<li><strong>Meetups</strong> – Find your people in meetups tailored to your platform or niches. Our goal is to connect you with as many creators as possible!</li>
<li><strong>Creator Conversations</strong> – These are a hybrid between a session and mini masterminds led by creators and focused on topics that are more niche than general. They provide an opportunity to connect with other like-minded travel creators and to learn from each other.</li>
<li><strong>Evening Events</strong> – Who doesn’t love letting loose with your travel friends? Have fun at the Welcome Reception, Dinner Clubs, Late Night Meetups, and more.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Speakers</h3>
<p>Like in previous years, we’re going to have a lot of incredible speakers. You can see a <a href="https://travelcon.org/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">full list on the website</a>, but some confirmed speakers are:</p>
<ul>
<li>Me!</li>
<li>Jeff Jenkins, <em>Chubby Diaries</em></li>
<li>Raime Iacofano, <em>Raimee’s Travel</em></li>
<li>Rand Fishkin, <em>SEO guru</em></li>
<li>Preethi Chandrasekhar, <em>The Eager Traveler</em></li>
<li>Jessica Serna, <em>My Curly Adventures</em></li>
<li>Katherine Fan, <em>Badass freelance writer</em></li>
<li>Kemoy Martin, <em>Travel &#038; lifestyle creator</em></li>
<li>Kristin and Siya, <em>Hopscotch the Globe</em></li>
<li>Rachel Rudwell, <em>Rachel Roams</em></li>
</ul>
<p>Come join me for the best travel show out there. Of course, I’m biased because I started it, but I made this the kind of event that I’d want to attend. One that focuses on business, networking, and improving your skills while having a good time doing it.</p>
<p>Tickets are $499 and you can <a href="https://travelcon.org/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">purchase them here</a>.</p>
<p> It’s going to be a blast! I hope to see you there!</p>
