<p><span>As the world of short-term rentals evolves, maintaining positive community relationships and safeguarding your property become paramount. We’re excited to introduce our integration with Alertify,</span><a href="https://www.alertify.io/hosts-and-home-owners/"> <span>a cutting-edge room monitoring solution</span></a><span>. Alertify focuses on detecting excessive noise, indoor smoking, and elevated occupancy levels, ensuring the harmony of your rental space.</span></p>
<p><b>Alertify in a Nutshell</b></p>
<p><a href="https://www.alertify.io/product-features/"><span>Alertify</span></a><span> is a revolutionary plug-in room monitoring device designed to safeguard your short-term rental. By detecting indoor smoking, noise disturbances, and occupancy breaches, Alertify empowers you to take proactive action. Real-time alerts enable swift intervention to prevent noise complaints or property damages. Within the Alertify dashboard, you can easily access live and historical data on noise and smoking levels, further enhancing your property management insights. Additionally, after-incident reports can be downloaded and submitted as evidence in disputes, providing a robust layer of accountability.</span><span><br /></span><span><br /></span><b>Ensuring Guest Privacy and Security</b></p>
<p><span>Incorporating Alertify into your short-term rental operations doesn’t compromise guest privacy. The device offers peace of mind, even if guests attempt to interfere with it. In case of unplugging, a real-time notification ensures you stay informed. Additionally, Alertify’s backup battery guarantees continuous monitoring of indoor smoking and excessive noise.</span></p>
<h2><b>iGMS and Alertify: Seamless Integration for Efficient Management</b></h2>
<p><b>Unlocking the Power of GuestAlert</b></p>
<p><span>Our partnership with iGMS takes Alertify’s capabilities to the next level. With the integration, you gain access to Alertify’s GuestAlert feature, which enables automated messaging to guests via SMS and email if violations are detected. This intelligent system keeps you in control without constant intervention. Stay informed about each step, and receive notifications if the situation escalates.</span></p>
<p><b>Guest Screening Portal: A Glimpse of the Future</b></p>
<p><span>Excitingly, Alertify will soon introduce a guest screening portal for your short-term rental company. This customizable tool empowers you to learn more about incoming guests, verify their IDs and payments, and communicate house rules effectively.</span></p>
<p><b>Counting the Costs of Guest Violations</b></p>
<p><span>Excessive noise and indoor smoking can lead to costly consequences for your short-term rental business. Unaddressed noise complaints can damage your relationships with neighbors and community members. Indoor smoking incidents result in around $1,100 in damages on average, affecting walls, furniture, and linens. Preventive measures like Alertify can save you from future revenue loss and uphold the reputation of your rental property.</span></p>
<p><b>Protecting Your Investment with Alertify</b></p>
<p><span>Alertify equips you with the tools to prevent damages and, if necessary, provide the evidence you need. By maintaining a positive rapport with building managers and community members, you secure your property’s future. You can hold guests accountable for any damages that may occur during their stay.</span></p>
<p><b>Integrating iGMS with Alertify: A Step-by-Step Guide</b></p>
<ol>
<li aria-level="1"><b>Access Your Alertify Dashboard</b><span>: Log in and head to the “Integrations” page.</span></li>
<li aria-level="1"><b>Connect with iGMS</b><span>: Click “Connect” in the top right corner and log into iGMS when prompted.</span></li>
<li aria-level="1"><b>Sync Your Properties</b><span>: Allow Alertify to import and map your properties seamlessly.</span></li>
<li aria-level="1"><b>Enable GuestAlert</b><span>: Within the Alertify dashboard, activate the GuestAlert feature. Automatically communicate with your guests through iGMS in case of violations.</span></li>
</ol>
