<p>This state park in California is located 12 miles south of Pfeiffer Big Sur State Park on California&#8217;s Pacific coast. A main feature of the park is McWay Falls, which drops over a cliff of 80 feet into the Pacific Ocean. The park is also home to 300-foot redwoods over 2,500 years old.</p>
