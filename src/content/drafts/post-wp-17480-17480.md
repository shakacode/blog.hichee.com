<p><strong>1. Create a memorable welcome package for guests that includes local snacks, a guide book with favourite spots and activities, and any other amenities you think your guests will appreciate</strong></p>
<p><strong>2. Offer discounts or freebies for repeat visits – think free breakfast one day, a 20% discount on their next stay, or anything that makes them feel special. </strong></p>
<p><em>Using TravelNest’s direct booking link will cut out the middle-man when offering your guests the option to rebook. You’ll only pay 5% commission on any bookings processed via your link. Find out <a href="https://travelnest.com/direct/">more.</a></em></p>
<p><strong>3. Make sure to keep your reviews fresh by responding to each review promptly and positively</strong></p>
<p><strong>4. Reach out to previous guests to ask if they had any feedback and adjust your rental accordingly</strong></p>
<p><strong>5. Stay engaged with past renters through social media and newsletters</strong></p>
<p><strong>6. Send thank you notes after their stays reminding them of the great time they had and why they should come back soon</strong></p>
<p><strong>7. Regularly review rates to make sure they are competitive in the market. </strong></p>
