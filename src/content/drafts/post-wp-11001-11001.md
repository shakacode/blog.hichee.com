<p>This forest is a tropical rainforest in northeastern Puerto Rico. From El Portal Visitor Center, a walkway winds through the surrounding treetops. Trails run to La Mina waterfall and Mount Britton, then up to the high-altitude dwarf forest. In the south are ancient petroglyphs made by the indigenous Taíno people. The forest comes alive with the nightly calls of coquí tree frogs.</p>
