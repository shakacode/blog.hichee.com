<p><span>Work from homers and digital nomads can take to the sea and “work from helm” all while traveling to nine different destinations across the Mediterranean with Virgin Voyages latest offering: the <a href="https://www.virginvoyages.com/l/one-month-summer-mediterranean-cruise">Summer Season Pass</a>.</span></p>
<p><span>With this pass, Virgin Voyages is putting into motion Richard Branson’s belief that brilliant work is best paired with a hearty dose of fun. So bring your latest assignment, project, or hop on a meeting from your private balcony overlooking clear blue waters.</span></p>

<p><span>This one month cruise through the Mediterranean will take place on a Virgin Voyages ship, and for those planning on working while onboard they are guaranteed the fastest at-sea internet in the biz, making it easy to perfect your work-life balance at sea. The deal starts at $9990 for two Sailors.</span></p>
<p><span>Amenities while on board include: centrally located cabins with private balconies and hammocks for lounging, premium wifi, laundry service, early access booking, pre-voyage and onboard support, access to a members only sundeck with panoramic ocean views, and champagne to start your journey off with bubbles.</span></p>
<p><span>Stop at nine unique ports in the Mediterranean where you can work by day, explore the Tuscan wine country by night, laze on beaches in Spain in the morning, and still make your afternoon meeting. </span></p>
<p><span>Destination cities include Barcelona, Marseille, Cannes, Ibiza, Rome, and more. There are seven sailings to choose from between June and September, each spanning about 30 days.</span></p>
