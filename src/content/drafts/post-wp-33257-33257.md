<h2>Firstly why promote your vacation rental on social media? </h2>
<p>As Paul Anderson says, <strong><em>“Social media platforms are not just that, but are also advertising agencies…” </em></strong></p>
<p><strong><em><br /></em></strong>Instagram is a visually captivating platform that not only provides entertainment but also serves as a source of inspiration. This makes it an ideal platform for travel brands, including those in the vacation rental industry.</p>
<p>Currently, a staggering 200 million Instagram users visit at least one business profile each day. They actively utilise Instagram to search for products, services, and brands, relying on the recommendations and opinions of fellow Instagram users. </p>
<p>Social media marketing has revolutionised business growth opportunities, and it’s all at your fingertips. As a property manager, vacation rental owner, host or short-term rental hotelier, you can now elevate your brand and advertise to your audience quicker and more widely than ever before. </p>

Instagram is a great way to reach your target audience and build a connection with them.</p>

<h2>The secret sauce to building your Instagram vacation rental marketing strategy</h2>
<p>What is an algorithm? Understanding algorithms is a complex task. Especially when they often change and are different for each platform. </p>
<p>The secret to building your business on Instagram is working with the algorithm rather than against it. So, what’s the algorithm, and how do you work with it? </p>
<p>Paul Anderson provided us with 3 key takeaways:</p>
<ol>
<li>Understand your ideal client and the exact marketing content they will like</li>
</ol>
<ol start="2">
<li>Create only the content they will be interested in</li>
</ol>
<ol start="3">
<li>Teach the algorithm what you’re about through consistency</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>“If you consistently post about similar subjects, you’re showing the algorithm who you are and what you’re about.” </strong></p>
<p>Instagram measures the success of a campaign through awareness, reach and recall. </p>
<p>Remember, guests can’t book a short-term rental directly on Instagram. You can’t use direct links on your posts or videos. Instead, Instagram offers the opportunity for you to build large-scale brand awareness and create a solid inbound marketing strategy. </p>
