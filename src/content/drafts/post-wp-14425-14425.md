<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Purchasing_Travel_Insurance"></span>Purchasing Travel Insurance<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Cheap-Travel-Insurance.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-1" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Cheap-Travel-Insurance-600x200-1.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-30167" alt="Cheap Travel Insurance" width="600" height="200" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Cheap-Travel-Insurance-600x200-1.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Cheap-Travel-Insurance-300x100-1.jpg 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Cheap-Travel-Insurance-768x256-1.jpg 768w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Cheap-Travel-Insurance-1536x512-1.jpg 1536w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Cheap-Travel-Insurance-1200x400-1.jpg 1200w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Cheap-Travel-Insurance.jpg 1800w" /></a></p>
<p>First of all, purchasing travel insurance is without a doubt the – <strong><em>Most Important Thing</em></strong> you can do for you and your family. </p>
<p>As we all know, 🤕 accidents happen and they happen quickly!</p>
<p>There are a number of things to consider when looking at travel insurance. You want to be sure that you choose the insurance coverage that suits your travel plans best. </p>
<p>Your vacation planning should always include the {appropriate travel insurance}, for the destination and activities included in your holiday.</p>
<p>There are countless incidents that could cause you to; cancel your trip, return home early, or force you to seek 🏥 emergency medical treatment while away.</p>
<p><strong>Let’s have a look at a few obvious reasons that could cause {trip disruptions} and some important things to take note of.</strong></p>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/cheapest-family-travel-insurance" rel="attachment wp-att-7628"><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/travel-accessories.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-7628 size-full" title="cheapest family travel insurance" alt="cheapest family travel insurance" width="330" height="235" data-sizes="(max-width: 330px) 100vw, 330px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/travel-accessories.jpg 330w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/travel-accessories-300x214-1.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p><center><b><i><span>You or a member of your family requires emergency ⚕ medical and/or 🏥 hospital treatment prior to or while away on vacation.</span></i></b></center><center></center></p>
<p>This is without a doubt, the most important reason for purchasing travel insurance. </p>
<p>Travel medical insurance is vital and should always be included in your travel budget for the entire family!</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Compare_That_Cheap_Travel_Insurance_Coverage"></span>Compare That Cheap Travel Insurance Coverage<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/110610_2138_thecheapest2-jpg/"><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/110610_2138_Thecheapest2.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-440 size-full" title="travel medical cartoon" alt="travel medical cartoon" width="350" height="243" data-sizes="(max-width: 350px) 100vw, 350px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/110610_2138_Thecheapest2.jpg 350w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/110610_2138_Thecheapest2-300x208-1.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p>It’s very important that you know what you are purchasing, what is covered, and what is not.</p>
<p><center><b><i>📖  Be Informed and Read The Fine Print!</i></b></center></p>
<p>If you are choosing to purchase insurance online or with any insurance agency for that matter, be sure to <b>Question the Coverage ❓</b> and again, read and/or have the policy explained to you in full detail!</p>
<p>You want to absolutely be sure you and every member of your family is <em><span>Completely Covered,</span></em> in the event of a mishap where coverage is required. </p>
<p>This is where a {qualified travel insurance agent}, can assist you in making the right decisions for you and your family.</p>
<p>If not and as an example, a simple fall resulting in a – broken hip, – could cost you dearly in a foreign country!</p>
<p><b>Cheap Travel Insurance Policies Note:</b></p>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/cheapest-family-travel-insurance" rel="attachment wp-att-7632"><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/travel-insurance.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-7632 " title="Family Travel Insurance" alt="Family Travel Insurance" width="572" height="221" data-sizes="(max-width: 572px) 100vw, 572px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/travel-insurance.jpg 590w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/travel-insurance-300x116-1.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p>Here is a prime example that happened on one of our trips into the United States from Canada. </p>
<p>While traveling in our 🚌 Recreational Vehicle down south, my wife came down with an unexpected minor illness, that required medical attention.</p>
<p>After 2 visits to the doctor, lab tests and prescription medication, the bill came to almost 💵 $1000 in U.S. funds.  If not for our travel medical insurance, this would have taken quite a bite out of our travel expenses.</p>
<p><b>This was just a minor illness and I cannot imagine what the cost would be, for something much more serious in nature!</b></p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Low_Cost_Travel_Insurance"></span>Low Cost Travel Insurance<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/cheapest-family-travel-insurance" rel="attachment wp-att-7630"><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/credit-cards.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-7630 " title="credit card travel insurance" alt="credit card travel insurance" width="350" height="233" data-sizes="(max-width: 350px) 100vw, 350px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/credit-cards.jpg 425w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/credit-cards-300x200-1.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p>Today, many people like ourselves, have travel and medical coverage supplied with their chosen 💳 credit cards.</p>
<p>This is often a great option, but you <em><strong>Must Be Aware</strong></em> of what exactly is included and what is exempt, in your credit card insurance coverage.<em><br /></em></p>
<p>If you have trouble understanding or reading the coverage, which is the case most times for many of us, get a bank representative where your card is issued from, to explain the policy to you.</p>
<p>I know our card has many exclusions, that one may just take for granted. </p>
<p>For example;</p>
<p><em>✔Do you know what the total amount of coverage in dollars is?</em></p>
<p><em>✔How many members of your family are included?</em></p>
<p><em>✔ How many travel days are included in the policy?</em></p>
<p><em>✔What the coverage is for any pre-existing conditions?</em></p>
<p><em>✔What previous medical conditions are not covered?</em></p>
<p><em>✔What sports and activities are allowed?</em></p>
<p>So as you can see, the travel insurance provided by your credit card company, may not be really what you think! </p>
<p>Again, you must read the fine print in the policy, or have it explained to you in detail.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Trip_Cancellation_Interruption_Insurance_Policies"></span>Trip Cancellation &#038; Interruption Insurance Policies<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/avoid-hangovers-on-vacation/all-inclusive-holidays/" rel="attachment wp-att-9124"><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/all-inclusive-holidays.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-9124 size-full" title="all inclusive holidays" alt="all inclusive holidays" width="293" height="172" /></a></p>
<p>Trip cancellation or trip interruption is another reason you may want to consider travel insurance. </p>
<p>For example, let’s say you have previously paid a great deal of money up front for one of those terrific  <em>🛳  <a title="All Inclusive Vacations Are A Great Choice" href="https://gr8traveltips.com/cheapest-all-inclusive-vacations/"><span>All Inclusive Vacations</span> </a></em>.</p>
<p>
At the last moment, you may have to cancel that trip for one reason or another.</p>
<p><b>Without this insurance, everything you have paid to that point is lost and for a typical family, it could mean thousands of dollars paid for; airfare, hotel accommodation, deposits and so forth – all gone!</b></p>
<p>With the proper {trip cancellation insurance} in place, when you purchased your vacation package in most cases, you would be fully reimbursed for what you paid.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="With_The_Right_Insurance_Policy_You_Are_Covered"></span>With The Right Insurance Policy You Are Covered<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/travel-suitcase.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-2" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/travel-suitcase.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-3419 size-full" title alt="travel suitcase" width="237" height="213" /></a></p>
<p>The same goes for – trip interruption! </p>
<p>Let’s say you have a connecting flight and it’s cancelled while you are in transit for a reason that the airline would not be responsible for.  Now what? 🙁</p>
<p>You and your family are forced to stay overnight, get a hotel, pay for extra meals, transportation and so on.  With trip interruption insurance, you are taken care of, with limits of course.</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/lost-bags.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-3" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/lost-bags.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9927" alt="Lost Travel Baggage" width="272" height="164" /></a></p>
<p>The same goes for your 🧳  baggage. </p>
<p>Have you ever been the victim of <em>🧳  <a title="A Few Important Lost Travel Luggage Tips" href="https://gr8traveltips.com/lost-travel-luggage-tips/">Lost Travel Luggage</a></em>? ❓ </p>
<p>If so, then you know how disrupting that can be for travel of any kind. </p>
<p>Having <em>Loss of baggage insurance</em> included, will take care of replacing those items you need now!</p>
<p><strong>Government health plans may not cover all emergency medical expenses once you leave your home. In most cases and generally, coverage is extremely limited once you leave the country.</strong></p>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/Professional-Travel-Agent-1-scaled.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-4" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Professional-Travel-Agent-1-600x400-1.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-29545" alt="Professional Travel Agent" width="600" height="400" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Professional-Travel-Agent-1-600x400-1.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Professional-Travel-Agent-1-300x200-1.jpg 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Professional-Travel-Agent-1-768x512-1.jpg 768w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Professional-Travel-Agent-1-1536x1024-1.jpg 1536w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Professional-Travel-Agent-1-1200x800-1.jpg 1200w" /></a></p>
<p>You can purchase your travel insurance easily from any local insurance office, or online with any one of the <em>major insurance providers</em>.  They all offer competitive rates and excellent family all inclusive insurance packages, to suit your travel needs.</p>
<p>You can also choose individual; medical, trip interruption, trip cancellation and/or loss of baggage insurance.</p>
