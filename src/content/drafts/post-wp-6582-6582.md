<span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">It’s that time of year where over 50 million people travel different parts of the world to enjoy a Thanksgiving feast connecting with friends and family.  Over 3 million are expected to be in New York for the Macy&#8217;s Thanksgiving Day Parade.</span><br />
<span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span><br />
<span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">A quick bit of trivia. Thanksgiving is celebrated on the 4th Thursday of November. It originated as a harvest festival. In 1621, the Plymouth colonists and Wampanoag Indians shared a Fall harvest feast.  Today this is known as one of the first Thanksgiving. Nearing 400 years later we still give special Thanks on this day.</span><br />
<span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span><br />
<span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">One of the most celebrated Thanksgiving travel destination is New York City for the Macy’s Day Parade. Check out our New York link to see a few Manhattan properties to rent for the this Holiday or any visit to the Big Apple.</span><br />
<span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span><br />
<span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">The Parade&#8217;s 92nd year does not disappoint! With balloons of Charlie Brown, Paw Patrol, Dr. Seuss, Goku, Little Cloud and many others.</span></p>
