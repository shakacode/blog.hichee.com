<p>Use high quality images of your property, behind-the-scenes photos, and guest testimonials. Mix it up with local attractions to keep the content fresh, engaging and consistent. Ensure you have a bank of content ready to use, but if you are running out of imagery, you can also consider use text posts. Content calendars make it easy to anticipate, create, and schedule posts ahead of time; as well as plan the content you’re going to be using. Following the rise of TikTok, video content has exploded in popularity and engagement on social media. As more platforms are supporting short and long-form videos, businesses are finding it easier to create and perfect eye-catching content quickly and easily. Create an immersive experience for followers that will inspire and motivate them to want to go on a vacation and explore!</p>
