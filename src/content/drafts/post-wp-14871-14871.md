<p>Travel is beginning to resume safely once again for all of us. It’s been a long awaited return, especially for those of us who just love to travel!</p>
<p>I’ve just updated my post here on {🏨 Hotel Room Safety Tips For Traveling} as a reminder, to be aware of your belongings and valuables when staying in unfamiliar places.</p>
<p>Unfortunately, 🕵 crooks work year round and do prey on hotel and resorts around the 🌎 world.</p>
<p>Here are just a few 🏨 Hotel Room Safety Tips to think about, to help keep you and your valuables safe at all times. 🙂</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Hotel_Room_Safety_Hacks"></span>Hotel Room Safety Hacks<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><strong>Check Your Room Carefully</strong></p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Hotel-Room-Safety.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-1" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Hotel-Room-Safety.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-23035 size-full" title alt="Hotel Room Safety" width="600" height="400" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Hotel-Room-Safety.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Hotel-Room-Safety-300x200-1.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p>When checking into your room, take a good look around and ask yourself;</p>
<p>✔ Is the door secure and does it have good security locks?</p>
<p>✔ If there is a patio door, does it lock securely? This is especially important if your room is on the ground floor.</p>
<p>✔ Is there an in room safe, and is it securely fastened? Does it have a programmable security code you can enter?</p>
<p>✔ If the locks in your room are insufficient, then ask for a new room right away. If a new room is unavailable, ask for a maintenance person to come and fix the problem asap.</p>
<p>I know this is something that most of us rarely think of, especially when we are excited and having just arrived and particularly at one of those 🏖 all-inclusive resorts, to start your long awaited vacation.</p>
<p>One would assume that everything will be {safe and secure}, but unfortunately, that’s not always the case. 🙁</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/hotel-maid-stealing.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-2" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/hotel-maid-stealing.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-23039 size-full" title alt="hotel maid stealing" width="600" height="338" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/hotel-maid-stealing.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/hotel-maid-stealing-300x169-1.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p>Sticky fingers can be found pretty much everywhere around the world these days!</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Hotel_Security_Tips_-_Good_To_Know"></span>Hotel Security Tips – Good To Know<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><strong>Leaving Your Room For The Day</strong></p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/hotel-room-theft.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-3" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/hotel-room-theft.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-23036 size-full" title alt="hotel room theft" width="600" height="400" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/hotel-room-theft.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/hotel-room-theft-300x200-1.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p>I always say…</p>
<blockquote><p><center><b><i>“Trust Your Instincts!”</i></b></center></p></blockquote>
<p>Depending on where you are traveling to and the type of hotel or resort you are staying at, it’s always better to be safe than sorry.</p>
<p>Here’s a few things we always do in our room, when going out for the day or for the evening;</p>
<p>✔ Make sure your valuables are hidden well or locked away somewhere. If there’s no room safe, we always lock our valuables in a suitcase with a secure lock.</p>
<p>Not one of those cheap little luggage locks!</p>
<p>Then we place a few items on top of that suitcase that we’ve locked.</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/hotel-room-safe.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-4" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/hotel-room-safe.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-23037 size-full" title alt="hotel room safe" width="600" height="356" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/hotel-room-safe.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/hotel-room-safe-300x178-1.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p>✔ Always leave the television on low and if at night, keep a light on in the room.</p>
<p>✔ Never leave a door tag outside to have the Maid Clean The Room!</p>
<p>They will always get around to cleaning your room while making their rounds during the day. We even put the <em>Do Not Disturb Sign</em> on the door handle on occasions</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Do-Not-Disturb-Door-Sign.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-5" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Do-Not-Disturb-Door-Sign.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-23038 size-full" title alt="Do Not Disturb Door Sign" width="600" height="338" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Do-Not-Disturb-Door-Sign.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Do-Not-Disturb-Door-Sign-300x169-1.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p>✔ Never leave items hanging around the room that may lead a staff member into temptation, and to do something they normally would not.</p>
<p>There have been times when traveling to remote parts of the world, that we have actually had to hide valuables behind drawers and other hidden areas of our hotel rooms, just to keep them out of site.</p>
<p>I’ve even recall hiding money in the hotel fridge at one time. 🙂</p>
<p>Just have to use your imagination when all else fails!</p>
<p>As well, many times when leaving our hotel rooms, we have had to take our passports and cash along with us, as there were no other safe alternatives available at that time.</p>
<p>In many cases and depending on where you are staying, the front desk may have a safe to put your valuables in.</p>

<p>Unfortunately, hotel theft and break ins occur more often than we think, even in the safest of hotel and resorts around the world.</p>
<p>Are you bringing valuables with you that you really need to have with you? ❓</p>
