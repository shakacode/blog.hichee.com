<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Always_Be_Aware_Of_Your_Surroundings"></span>Always Be Aware Of Your Surroundings<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<blockquote class="instagram-media" data-instgrm-permalink="https://www.instagram.com/p/CA-4PWEpkDO/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;utm_campaign=loading" data-instgrm-version="12" readability="-10.25">
</blockquote>
<p>Take a moment and look around your surroundings and always – trust your instincts. If you feel unsafe in a particular area, then you probably are.  </p>
<p>Leave as quickly and safely as you can, especially when traveling solo!</p>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/travel-safely"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4001" title="Travel Safely" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/travel-safely.jpg" alt="Travel Safely" width="300" height="93" /></a></p>
<p>Many people simply forget to look around them, and see what’s going on. Always take a minute and look around you, to be sure your surroundings are safe and you feel secure.</p>
<p>This especially the case, when you’re traveling in a new and unfamiliar foreign destination.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Traveling_Safely_Abroad_Includes_Common_Sense_Good_Judgement_When_Traveling"></span>Traveling Safely Abroad Includes Common Sense &#038; Good Judgement When Traveling<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<h4><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Travel_in_Groups"></span><strong>Travel in Groups</strong><span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/travel-safely/traveling-in-a-group/" rel="attachment wp-att-18757"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-18757 size-large" title="Traveling in a Group" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Traveling-in-a-Group-600x286-1.png" alt="Traveling in a Group" width="600" height="286" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Traveling-in-a-Group-600x286-1.png 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Traveling-in-a-Group-300x143-1.png 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Traveling-in-a-Group.png 714w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a></p>
<p>Try not to be caught alone in unfamiliar areas where you really shouldn’t be, especially at night. If you’re on vacation, this is when you’re usually most vulnerable. </p>
<p>Having a good time while on vacation, is normally when we tend to let loose and let our guard down at certain times.</p>
<p>This is usually when we least think of our own personal safety, because we’re on vacation! 🙁</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Travel_Safety_Includes_Watching_Your_Liquor_Consumption"></span>Travel Safety Includes Watching Your Liquor Consumption<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/cruise-planning-alcohol/tropical-drinks/" rel="attachment wp-att-7579"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-7579 size-medium" title="Tropical Drinks" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Tropical-Drinks-300x275-1.jpg" alt="Tropical Drinks" width="300" height="275" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Tropical-Drinks-300x275-1.jpg 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Tropical-Drinks.jpg 640w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a></p>
<p>We also may tend to over indulge in 🍹 alcoholic beverages while enjoying our vacation and unfortunately, this is when many tourists are at most risk with 🕵 criminals.  </p>
<p>Criminals especially prey on visitors at bars and nightclubs.</p>
<p>Of course, it’s all part of enjoying ones holiday, but try never to let your guard down or make decisions you may regret later.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Be_Aware_Of_Your_Wallet_Handbag_Always"></span>Be Aware Of Your Wallet &#038; Handbag Always<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/london-travel-scams/pickpocket-theives/" rel="attachment wp-att-14333"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-14333 size-full" title="Pickpocket Theives" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Pickpocket-Theives.jpg" alt="Pickpocket Theives" width="600" height="314" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Pickpocket-Theives.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Pickpocket-Theives-300x157-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a></p>
<p>Men should never carry their wallets in their back pockets. It still amazes me to this day, to see men with their wallets carried in this manner when traveling. </p>
<p>They’re just asking to be a victim of pick pocketing!</p>
<p>Women should keep 👜 purses tight against them at all times. This is especially true in crowds such as at; busy ✈ airports, 🚉 train stations and other high traffic areas. </p>
<p>It’s always good to make sure you travel with a purse that zips tightly, and has no open pockets.</p>
<p>🕵 Criminals always prey on the unprepared!</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Try_To_Leave_That_Expensive_Jewelry_At_Home"></span>Try To Leave That Expensive Jewelry At Home<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/travel-safely/expensive-jewellery/" rel="attachment wp-att-18759"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-18759 size-full" title="Expensive Jewellery" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Expensive-Jewellery.jpg" alt="Expensive Jewellery" width="571" height="210" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Expensive-Jewellery.jpg 571w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Expensive-Jewellery-300x110-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 571px) 100vw, 571px" /></a></p>
<p>Expensive 💍 jewelry definitely draws attention to undesirables out there. It definitely makes tourists a target. </p>
<p>In fact, in one country we were traveling to recently, a local guide told us about thieves actually pulling gold earrings right off of women’s ears!</p>
<p>Yikes!! 🙁</p>
<p>It’s also a good idea to go through your wallets, before leaving home. Empty out extra 💳 credit cards, identification and other unnecessary items, that you really don’t need when away from home. </p>
<p>No sense in taking the chance of losing items that you really didn’t need anyway.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Watch_What_You_Eat_What_You_Drink"></span>Watch What You Eat &#038; What You Drink<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p> <br /><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Food-Truck.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-3" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-12358" title src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Food-Truck.jpg" alt="Food Truck" width="600" height="404" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Food-Truck.jpg 803w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Food-Truck-300x202-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a><br /> </p>
<p>To avoid 🤢 sickness and health related ailments while away, it’s important to be aware of what you’re eating and drinking at all times!</p>
<p>Never drink 🚱 tap water when visiting a new foreign destination! You’re just asking for trouble and unnecessary illness. </p>
<p>Also, be careful of 🍤 food stands and street vendors. As tempting as they may be, many travelers become ill from eating at – questionable food stands.</p>
<p>You certainly don’t want your holiday ruined from eating or drinking something, you probably knew you should have avoided in the first place. </p>
<p>The last thing we all don’t want when traveling, is to find ourselves constantly running to the …<br /> </p>

<p>For those who wear 👓 prescription eyeglasses like myself, always take an extra pair in case your original is lost or broken. </p>
<p>I was caught many years ago without an extra pair of glasses, while traveling abroad. I somehow misplaced them and being near sighted, I was left struggling to see in the distance, for the remainder of the trip.  </p>
<p>A hard lesson learned, but never to be forgotten again. 😎</p>
