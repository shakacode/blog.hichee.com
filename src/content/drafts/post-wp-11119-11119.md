<p>I would imagine most of you are like me, always trying to keep up with the ever changing – 🧳  Air Travel Carry On Baggage Restrictions. </p>
<p>Just what is allowed to be carried on these days and what is not. ❓</p>
<p>Have you been one of the unfortunate victims of <em><b>Carry On Item Confiscation</b> </em>at the 🛂 airport security line? </p>
<p>Is it perhaps you were not quite aware of what you were allowed to carry in that 👜 bag in the first place?</p>
<p>Well relax, you are definitely not alone!</p>
<p>I am going to try to clear things up on just what is and what is not acceptable to pack in your 💼 carry on baggage. There are strict rules for carry on, that you should be aware of before travel and these rules keep changing!</p>
<p><strong>You certainly do not want necessary items confiscated at the airport!</strong></p>
<p>First, have a quick look at this short 📹 video from – <em>Airfarewatchdog,</em> on carry on restrictions. Some changes to {Carry On Rules} that just keep on changing.</p>

<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Check-In_Baggage_Restrictions_Traveling_By_Air"></span>Check-In Baggage Restrictions Traveling By Air<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>

<p>What can I take on board my flight that is acceptable? Have you had that expensive can of body spray, perfume or your nail clippers taken from you? </p>
<p>We have and it is frustrating to watch that pricey can of body spray for example, taken from your bag and thrown into a big barrel by airport security. 🤨</p>
<p>Still today, as you saw in the video above, barrels of items are confiscated out of bags every day at 🧭 airports worldwide.</p>
<p>A big reason is because people are still unsure of what they can or cannot take on board an ✈ airplane. </p>
<p>Well, I am going to try and shed a little light on the subject and hopefully you will have a better idea of what is acceptable and what is not.</p>
<p>Here goes!</p>

<p>Most major airlines do allow each passenger to take – two pieces of carry on luggage. These are size restricted pieces of 🧳 baggage!  </p>
<p>See my recent update note below (Maximum Hand Luggage Size,) for some new size limitation restrictions now in force on most major airlines.</p>
<p>Usually the second item is a; laptop computer case, large camera case,  large purse of some sort or duty–free purchases.</p>

<p>You should always put your wallets, cash, traveler’s checks, personal identification and travel documents into your carry on. You should never have these items leave your possession while in transit. </p>
<p>I strongly suggest never putting any of the above listed items in your checked baggage!</p>

<p>For those passengers with infants or small children, diaper bags, strollers with limitations and special needs items are normally allowed in addition to your normal allowance.</p>
<p>Again, be sure to check with each individual airline prior to departure.</p>
<p><a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/carry-on-luggage/carry-on-liquid-rules/" rel="attachment wp-att-16608"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-16608 size-full" title="Carry On Liquid Rules" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Carry-On-Liquid-Rules.jpg" alt="Carry On Liquid Rules" width="582" height="231" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Carry-On-Liquid-Rules.jpg 582w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Carry-On-Liquid-Rules-300x119-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 582px) 100vw, 582px" /></a></p>
<p>100 ml (3.4 oz) is the maximum allowable container size for liquids or gels in your carry on luggage. As well, these containers must be displayed in clear plastic bags  for easy viewing at airport security checkpoints.</p>

<p><b>Air Travel Carry On Baggage Regulations – Update:</b></p>
<p>It was announced few seasons ago, that sharp tipped ✂ scissors and small pocket knives are now allowed in your carry-on luggage. </p>
<p>Really? ❓</p>
<p>Again, it is best to check before your flight to confirm if this strange rule is still in effect.  Here is the general restriction allowance for this item:</p>
<p><em>Sharp-tipped scissors and small pocket knives with blades measuring 6 cm (2.4 in) or less, and all round-tipped scissors</em></p>
<p>There are also medical exceptions for syringes and needles that are accompanied with printed prescriptions and so forth.  </p>
<p>Again, it is always best to check before departure with the airline you are traveling on.</p>

<p>Now that most airlines are charging for meals in 💺 Economy Class, more and more people are choosing to bring their own meals or snacks on board. I know we do, but haven’t always known just was is acceptable and what is not. </p>
<p>Below, is a short list of the more common carry on food items that are permitted to be taken onto the aircraft. You can bring solid food in both carry-on and checked baggage. </p>
<p>Examples of solid food products include;</p>
<p><em> meat, bread, fruits, vegetables, sandwiches, chips, cookies, cakes, muffins, granola bars, hard candies, cheese, nuts, crackers, chocolate bars and other similar food items.</em></p>
<p>With a little pre-travel planning and knowing what you can put into your carry on, will save you time, money and frustration at airport security.</p>
