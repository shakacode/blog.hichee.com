<h2 id="2-chekin-self-check-in-app">2. <a href="https://chekin.com/en/"><strong>Chekin</strong></a><strong>: Self-check-in app</strong></h2>
<p>You can make the check-in process easier and faster for you and your guests using Chekin. This simple-to-use app helps you automate check-in, so your guests can check themselves in when they arrive.</p>
<p>In addition to online check-in, the tool can help with identity verification, customisation and signing rental contacts. You can charge your guests for tourist taxes, accept deposits or purchase insurance during the check-in process, and even send relevant documents to local authorities to make sure you comply with legal requirements.</p>
<p>The app also allows you to upsell personalised experiences and services, like early or late check-out, services, transportation and activities. </p>
<p><strong>Price: Basic plan starts at €4 a month per property.</strong></p>
<h2 id="3-yourwelcome-digital-welcome-book">3. <a href="https://www.yourwelcome.com/"><strong>YourWelcome</strong></a><strong>: Digital welcome book</strong></h2>
<p>YourWelcome is a digital welcome book tablet you place in your property to give guests easy access to all the information they need about your property and the local area. </p>
<p>The digital guidebook can provide contactless check-in, property guides, and guest surveys where you can collect feedback and details. You can also upsell services, advertise local businesses, and collect guest reviews during their stay.</p>
<p>A digital welcome book helps promote your brand and elevate the guest experience to encourage repeat bookings.</p>
<p><strong>Price: $240 per property per year.</strong></p>

<p>Ecobee also provides home security systems that integrate with your smart thermostat. You can protect your home around the clock with professional monitoring that could even reduce your home insurance costs.</p>
