<p>These cliffs are one of the most outstanding coastal features of Ireland. Rising slowly from Doolin village, they ascend to over 700 feet (213 meters), boasting some of the most breathtaking scenery in Ireland. They have become one of Ireland&#8217;s premier tourist attractions and a must-see for many people visiting Ireland. They are a hotspot for a wide range of flora and fauna, with as many as 20 different species of birds to be seen. The cliffs have been featured in many movies, including Harry Potter, The Princess Bride, Leap Year, and many more.</p>
