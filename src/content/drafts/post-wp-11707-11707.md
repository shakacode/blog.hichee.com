<p>It’s that time of year again when many of us are getting ready for that long awaited vacation. Many of you I’m sure, are perhaps making plans for a long 🛣 road trip adventure.</p>
<p>As a previous long haul 🚛 trucker and driving instructor, I thought I would pass on a few important abd helpful 😴 Tips to Avoid Driver Fatigue.  </p>
<p>We all want to get to our planned destination as quick as we can, but we must be aware of the 🛑 signals of fatigue when behind the 🚙 wheel.</p>
<p><strong>Did you know that driver fatigue is a major contributing factor in at least 20 percent of all automobile crashes worldwide? ❓ </strong></p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Getting_Proper_Sleep_Is_Priority_One"></span>Getting Proper Sleep Is Priority One<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Driver-Fatique.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-1" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-13927 size-full" title src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Driver-Fatique.jpg" alt="Tips to Avoid Driver Fatigue" width="600" height="400" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Driver-Fatique.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Driver-Fatique-300x200-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a></p>
<p>So, you’re all packed and ready to head off on that long awaited road trip vacation. But, are you really prepared and ready for those long hours of driving ahead, especially if you are traveling with loved ones? ❓</p>
<p>Taking a road trip holiday is exciting, no matter what mode of 🚌 transportation you choose. Discovering new sites and attractions along the way is always half the fun, if you take the time to enjoy your drive.</p>
<p>Whether you travel by 🚗 car, 🏍 motorcycle, 🚍 recreational vehicle or any other mode of transportation, you must take the necessary steps to ensure you and your family will be safe on the road.</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Mt-Shasta-640x459-1.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-2" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10071" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Mt-Shasta-640x459-1.jpg" alt="RV Camping Class C Motorhome" width="640" height="459" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Mt-Shasta-640x459-1.jpg 640w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Mt-Shasta-640x459-300x215-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" /></a></p>
<p>Sleep experts suggest that most people do require between {💤 seven and nine hours of sleep} each day. This amount, is needed in order to be able to function fully during the course of the day, and to carry out everyday tasks. </p>
<p>Such as work, driving, exercise and any other daily activities that we perform on a daily basis.</p>
<p>The quality of our sleep that we get is equally important as well.</p>
<p>Try to schedule and plan your trip ahead, which will allow the driver to get a good night’s sleep before heading off. Ideally, having another person along who can share driving responsibilities is always great. </p>
<p>If this is not possible, then be aware of the <strong>⚠ Warning Signs</strong> of driver fatigue.</p>
<p>These signs include…</p>
<p><em>✔ Frequent Yawning;</em><br /><em>✔ Blinking Your Eyes More Often;</em><br /><em>✔ Forgetting the Last Stretch of Road Just Driven;</em><br /><em>✔ Slowing for No Reason;</em><br /><em>✔ Drifting the Vehicle in Your Lane.</em></p>
<p>These are just a few of the more common ⚠ warning signs of driver fatigue to be aware of. Should you do experience any such signs, pull the vehicle over immediately when safe to do so and – 🛑 Stop Driving!</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Be_Sure_To_Take_Frequent_Rest_Stops"></span>Be Sure To Take Frequent Rest Stops<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Power-Nap-Sign-Post.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-3" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-13928 size-full" title src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Power-Nap-Sign-Post.jpg" alt="Power Nap Sign Post" width="600" height="400" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Power-Nap-Sign-Post.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Power-Nap-Sign-Post-300x200-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a></p>
<p>When driving, do try to take regular rest intervals, at least every couple of hours. Step from the vehicle, relax and get some 🚶 exercise if possible. </p>
<p>This will definitely help refresh and revitalize you,so you can carry on more alert, for the next part of your journey!</p>
<p>If you do need to rest during your trip, 🐈 cat naps or short ⏰ twenty to forty minutes of sleep, can do wonders for reducing sleepiness and making you feel more refreshed.</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/P4241843-640x480-1.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-4" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9272" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/P4241843-640x480-1.jpg" alt="Semi Transport Truck" width="640" height="480" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/P4241843-640x480-1.jpg 640w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/P4241843-640x480-300x225-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" /></a> </p>
<p>When I was driving long-haul commercial transport 🚛 truck, I always made sure to have regular short pit stops along the way. I always tried to get out and 🚶 stretch, and revitalize myself as often as I could.</p>
<p>Many times I would take short 💤 30 minute cat naps, which I found extremely beneficial and recharged my batteries to continue on. 🙂</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Try_Not_To_Eat_Unhealthy_Fatty_Foods"></span>Try Not To Eat Unhealthy Fatty Foods<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/You-Snooze-You-Lose-Banner.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-5" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-13929 size-full" title src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/You-Snooze-You-Lose-Banner.jpg" alt="You Snooze You Lose Banner" width="600" height="275" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/You-Snooze-You-Lose-Banner.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/You-Snooze-You-Lose-Banner-300x138-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a></p>
<p>Try not to eat heavy 🍔 fatty meals when driving, as we all know how tired we feel after eating a full meal. Try to keep meals light and nutritious, with lots of 🍎 fruit if possible. </p>
<p>Lighter meals and good hydration will all help keep you alert and feeling better as well. Drinking lots of water is also important and definitely helps reduce – overall fatigue.</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/drinking-water.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-6" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10707" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/drinking-water.jpg" alt="drinking water" width="200" height="283" /></a></p>
<p>Taking these few precautions, will help to ensure you and your family have a safe and enjoyable road trip.</p>

<p>Remember, getting there is half the fun! 😎</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Final-Thoughts-Banner.png" data-rel="lightbox-image-7" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Final-Thoughts-Banner.png" alt="Final Thoughts Banner" width="500" height="155" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-33185" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Final-Thoughts-Banner.png 500w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Final-Thoughts-Banner-300x93-1.png 300w" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" /></a></p>
<p>Be sure to get out and enjoy the sites along the way at specific viewpoints, attractions and so many more places. </p>
<p>Do a little research, stop at tourist information centers and see what may be of interest, as you make your to your final destination.</p>
<p>We are always discovering new and exciting places on our road trips and so can you!<br /> </p>
