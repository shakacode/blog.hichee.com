<h4>Decorate Your Airbnb: <strong>Eclectic Mid Century Modern</strong></h4>
<p>Okay, let’s dive in and break this down. Eclectic interiors and Mid Century Interiors are two different things, but together they make up a very popular aesthetic you no doubt have seen a lot of on Pinterest. It’s like a style mash up, if you will. </p>
<p><strong>Eclectic Interiors</strong> allow you to play around with stylishly mixing antiques and ultra modern, on-trend pieces in one space. The combination of styles within a space and use of unique one-of-a-kind finds is what sums up the Eclectic style. This principal adds layers and character to the home that will not go unnoticed as guests are scrolling through their Airbnb search feed of properties looking for a place to stay. </p>
<p><strong>Mid Century Modern Interiors</strong> describes an iconic design style that originated in the 1950’s and 60’s. This style has seen a resurgence in popularity in the last 10 years, and many popular stores, like West Elm, focus on creating affordable furniture representing this style’s modern, clean lines, interesting shapes, and bold pops of color with an emphasis on bringing nature indoors.</p>

<h3>Decorate Your Airbnb: <strong>Want to Achieve an Eclectic Mid Century Style in your Airbnb? </strong></h3>
<p>Here are some easy things you can do to create the gorgeous mash-up of Eclectic and Mid Century Modern styles in your rental! </p>
<h3><strong>Easy Action Items:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Go Vintage.</strong> You must layer some vintage or antique flare into your space to pull off this style. Try taking a trip through your local flea market or vintage store to score some deals on one-of-a-kind items. </li>
<li><strong>Have fun with bold color play.</strong> Try a velvet sofa in a bold color, or a large bold piece of art. But do stick with <a href="https://www.quilldecor.com/blog/Picking-the-Right-Paint-Colors-for-your-Airbnb" target="_blank" aria-label="undefined (opens in a new tab)" rel="noreferrer noopener"><strong>a consistent and thought through color palette</strong></a> to keep things cohesive and grounded.   </li>
<li><strong>Fold MCM details in seamlessly.</strong> Mid Century Modern lamps and end tables easily pull an eclectic space towards this iconic era. Check out <a href="https://www.westelm.com/" target="_blank" aria-label="undefined (opens in a new tab)" rel="noreferrer noopener"><strong>West Elm</strong></a><strong> </strong>and <a href="https://www.worldmarket.com/" target="_blank" aria-label="undefined (opens in a new tab)" rel="noreferrer noopener"><strong>World Market</strong></a> for affordable finds.</li>
</ul>
