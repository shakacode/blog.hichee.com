<div align="center" class="MsoTitle" style="text-align: center;">
<span lang="EN" style="font-size: 18.0pt; line-height: 115%;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span lang="EN">There’s a lot to do when moving into a new<br />
apartment: turn on utilities, change your address, meet the neighbors. The list<br />
goes on. But the one thing you shouldn’t leave off your to-do list is a move-in<br />
cleaning. While any good landlord cleans between tenants, your landlord’s idea<br />
of “move-in ready” might not match yours. Doing your own cleaning ensures your<br />
new place is exactly how you want it.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<h2>
<span lang="EN">Why clean before moving in?<o:p></o:p></span></h2>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span lang="EN">A move-in cleaning is more than a chance to<br />
bust odors and dust bunnies left behind by the previous tenant. It’s also an<br />
opportunity to get a closer look at your new apartment. If you notice damage<br />
you didn’t spot during the walk-through, you can notify the landlord and get it<br />
fixed without impacting your security deposit. Plus, your new place will never<br />
be easier to clean than when it’s completely empty!<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
</div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span lang="EN">A deep cleaning is especially important if you<br />
have allergies. If the previous tenant had pets or smoked indoors, you can<br />
eliminate <u><span style="color: #1155cc;"><a href="https://www.merrymaids.com/blog/quick-tips/cleaning-tips-for-allergy-sufferers/"><span style="color: #1155cc;">lingering allergens</span></a></span></u> before they<br />
trigger symptoms.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<h2>
<span lang="EN">DIY deep cleaning vs.<br />
professional cleaning<o:p></o:p></span></h2>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span lang="EN">A DIY deep cleaning is the most cost-effective<br />
choice. If you have access to the new place before your old lease is up, you<br />
can spend a few days cleaning before moving your stuff in. Unfortunately, not<br />
everyone can delay moving in. If you need to move in ASAP, hire a cleaning<br />
service instead of trying to work around piles of boxes and furniture. A<br />
one-time apartment cleaning will get your place move-in ready without taking a<br />
big bite out of your budget. It’s always a little worrying letting strangers<br />
into your home, but you can use <u><span style="color: #1155cc;"><a href="https://www.homeadvisor.com/near-me/apartment-cleaners/"><span style="color: #1155cc;">this helpful tool</span></a></span></u> to find<br />
highly-rated, trusted apartment cleaners in your area.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
