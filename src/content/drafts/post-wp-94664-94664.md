<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Airbnb: pros and cons of </strong>the <strong>guest screening process</strong></h2>
<p>What are the pros and cons of the Airbnb background check process?</p>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Pros:</h3>
<ul>
<li>When signing up for Airbnb, guests must provide basic information such as their legal name, address, and a copy of an identification document. </li>
<li>The channel may require guests to submit a copy of their passport in countries where this is mandatory, such as Spain, Italy, and Portugal.</li>
<li>If you’re a verified host, you can require guests to verify their accounts through the Reservation Requirements page before booking your property. </li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Cons:</h3>
<ul>
<li>Airbnb occasionally submits guest details to background check providers, but they don’t run screening checks on every guest. </li>
<li>The Airbnb screening process doesn’t include a criminal record background check or checking against sex offenders registers.</li>
</ul>
<p>Learn more about Airbnb’s screening process in our article, “<a href="https://www.superhog.com/does-airbnb-do-background-checks/">Does Airbnb do background checks on guests?</a>” </p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Vrbo: pros and cons of the guest screening process</strong></h2>
<p>Vrbo performs only minimal checks on most guests. Here are the pros and cons of Vrbo background checks.  </p>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Pros:</h3>
<ul>
<li>To create an account and book a property, guests must provide their full name, email address, physical address, mobile phone number, payment details, and date of birth.</li>
<li>Vrbo awards a Verification Badge to guests who complete a verification process. To complete the process, Vrbo will verify guest details and identity documents.</li>
<li>Hosts can perform any extra screening checks they require.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Cons:</h3>
<ul>
<li>The verification process is optional. Unverified guests can still make bookings on the channel. </li>
<li>Vrbo’s verification process cannot:
<ul>
<li>Run criminal record background checks.</li>
<li>Check names against sex offenders registers.</li>
<li>Reveal credit history or chargebacks.</li>
<li>Detect fake IDs.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Read our article, “<a href="https://superhog.com/does-vrbo-do-background-checks-on-guests/">Does Vrbo do background checks on guests?</a>” for more information on Vrbo’s policies.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Booking.com: pros and cons of the guest screening process</strong></h2>
<p><a href="https://superhog.com/blog/does-booking-com-verify-guests/">Does Booking.com do background checks</a>? Yes, Booking.com’s background check process is more thorough than Airbnb’s or Vrbo’s. </p>
<p>The channel recently developed AI and machine learning solutions to increase security for hosts. </p>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Pros:</h3>
<ul>
<li>Guests are screened against global sanctions watchlists.</li>
<li>Booking.com’s machine-learning models can detect potential fraud and perform risk assessments after a booking is made. </li>
<li>Guests who have reports against them or have been banned from other properties won’t be able to make new bookings. </li>
<li>Hosts are encouraged to report guest misconduct, and you can block guests from booking your properties.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Cons:</h3>
<ul>
<li>Despite these screening measures, hosts can still be at risk of property damage from guests who pass these checks. </li>
<li>Booking.com doesn’t do sex offender background checks.  </li>
</ul>
