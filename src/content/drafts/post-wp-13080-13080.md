<h2><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Preparing_Travel_Documents_For_Minors_-_What_Is_Needed"></span>Preparing Travel Documents For Minors – What Is Needed<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>

<p>For those with children, it’s quite easy to forget about checking all the necessary 📇 Travel Documents for Minors, when planning a vacation.  </p>
<p>Speaking from past experience, there’s a lot to do when making 🏖 vacation plans especially with your 👨 kids. So, it’s quite easy to forget some simple things like; checking expiry dates on passports, travel visas and so on.</p>
<p>You are responsible for your child’s documents and believe it or not, many people completely forget about them. The result is, ending up in a mad panic and rush just before leaving! 😲</p>
<p><strong>Take a moment well before leaving on your vacation and make sure everyone’s travel documents are valid and up to date.</strong></p>
<p>If you miss anything, it could cause – trip disruptions and/or cancellations all together. So be prepared and make sure everything is in order prior to travel. </p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Travel_Documents_You_Should_Carry"></span>Travel Documents You Should Carry <span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Documents-For-Minors.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-0" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Documents-For-Minors.jpg" alt="Travel Documents For Minors" width="640" height="427" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-35615" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Documents-For-Minors.jpg 640w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Documents-For-Minors-300x200-1.jpg 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Documents-For-Minors-600x400-1.jpg 600w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" /></a></p>
<p>Family travel experts highlight three simple, but very important tips for travel most anywhere.  </p>
<p>I especially agree that your personal safety and <em>🧳 <a title="Learn To Travel Safely With Your Family" href="https://gr8traveltips.com/traveling-safely-abroad-travel-advice/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Traveling Safely Abroad </a></em>at all times, is 🥇 Number One!</p>
<p>When we traveled with our children, this was always – <i>Priority One,</i>when taking our family holidays!</p>
<p>Experts list three main priorities;</p>
<p><em>✔ Personal Safety</em><br /><em>✔ Documentation</em><br /><em>✔ Vaccinations</em></p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Keeping_Your_Childrens_Travel_Documents_In_Order"></span>Keeping Your Children’s Travel Documents In Order<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-262" title="Passport Travel Stamps" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/102810_1844_Whattraveld2.jpg" alt="Passport Travel Stamps" width="231" height="240" /></p>
<p>It is vital, that you are aware of the <em>required documents</em> necessary for each travel destination you are going to, especially when traveling to a foreign country. </p>
<p>Included in your documentation should be;</p>
<p><em>✔ Passport</em><br /><em>✔ Visa (if required)</em><br /><em>✔ Travel Insurance</em><br /><em>✔ Complete Itinerary</em></p>
<p>What we have done for many years now, is to take photos and 📧 email copies of our passports and other important identification to us, for safekeeping.  </p>
<p>Having copies at the ready, will make things so much easier should you lose one and need an emergency replacement while away from home.<br /> </p>
<blockquote class="instagram-media" data-instgrm-permalink="https://www.instagram.com/p/BvmqloUnfms/?utm_source=ig_embed&amp;utm_campaign=loading" data-instgrm-version="12" readability="-5.3333333333333">
</blockquote>
<p> <br />If you are stopping in a different country on the way to your final destination, you must have the documents necessary to enter that country as well.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Photocopy_Or_Scan_All_Your_Travel_Documents"></span>Photocopy Or Scan All Your Travel Documents<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-261 size-full" title="Passports For Travel" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/102810_1844_Whattraveld1.jpg" alt="Passports For Travel" width="280" height="297" /></p>
<p>As mentioned, in case you do lose the originals, replacing them is much quicker and easier if you have copies of the originals with you. It goes without saying, valid passports are required for travel to any country in the world.</p>
<p><strong>Passports</strong> are by far the …</p>
<p><center><b><i>Preferred and internationally accepted evidence of identity and citizenship</i></b></center> </p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Passports_Must_Be_Valid_Six_Months_Past_Date_Of_Entry"></span>Passports Must Be Valid Six Months Past Date Of Entry<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Passports.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-1" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-29515" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Passports-600x416-1.jpg" alt="Passports" width="600" height="416" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Passports-600x416-1.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Passports-300x208-1.jpg 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Passports-768x533-1.jpg 768w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Passports.jpg 960w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a></p>
<p>Always make sure your passports are valid and up to date.<strong> </strong> Take them out and have a look months before your travel date.</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Of-Special-Note-e1626881250268.png" data-rel="lightbox-image-2" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Of-Special-Note-e1626881250268.png" alt="Of Special Note" width="150" height="150" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-33912" /></a></p>
<p>Your passports need to be valid for at least 6 months beyond your return date home and for any form of travel whether by; land, sea and air.</p>
<p>In the event of loss of a passport while traveling, that passport can be replaced at a Consulate Embassy. Most other forms of travel documents cannot. 🙁</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Travel_Documents_For_Children_Include_Necessary_Visas"></span>Travel Documents For Children Include Necessary Visas<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p> <em>Sample of a Canadian Visitors Visa</em></p>
<p><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-7810 " title="Travel Visa" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Visa-1.jpg" alt="Travel Visa" width="426" height="297" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Visa-1.jpg 500w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Visa-300x209-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 426px) 100vw, 426px" /><br /> </p>
<p><b>Visa Definition:</b></p>
<blockquote><p><center readability="2.5"><i readability="5">“Certificate issued or a stamp marked (on the applicant’s passport) by the immigration authorities of a country to indicate that the applicant’s credentials have been verified and he or she has been granted permission to enter the country for a temporary stay within a specified period. </p>
<p>This permission, however, is provisional and subject to the approval of the 🛂 immigration officer at the entry point.”</p>
<p></i></center>   BusinessDictionary.com</p></blockquote>
<p>In many cases, a {Tourist Visa} may also be necessary for entering the country you are visiting.  </p>
<p>Even if you’re heading away on one of those fantastic – all inclusive family vacations, you may still need a Tourist Visa in order to enter your chosen country.</p>
<p><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-7571 " title="Travel Agent Cartoon" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/travel_agent.gif" alt="Travel Agent Cartoon" width="325" height="296" /></p>
<p>Be sure to double check with your travel agent, or with an official tourism website for the country you are visiting. </p>
<p>This is just one of the many 👩 <a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/benefits-of-booking-with-a-travel-agent/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><em>Benefits of Booking With A Travel Agent</em></a>.</p>
<p>Obtaining a tourist visa is one of the documents most often overlooked, especially because so many people are booking travel on their own these days, 💻 online.</p>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Visa.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-3" data-rl_title data-rl_caption title><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-29516" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Visa-600x300-1.jpg" alt="Travel Visa" width="600" height="300" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Visa-600x300-1.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Visa-300x150-1.jpg 300w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Visa-768x384-1.jpg 768w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Visa-1536x768-1.jpg 1536w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Travel-Visa-1200x600-1.jpg 1200w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a></p>
<p>A qualified and reputable travel agent will often pass this vital travel information on to their clients, when making travel arrangements for them.</p>
<p><strong>Ensure all necessary travel documents are in order, to avoid last minute panic and/or possible travel disruptions.</strong></p>
