<h2><b>Why Sure?</b></h2>
<p><span>Sure isn’t just another home warranty provider. They offer a modern, comprehensive digital solution tailored to the needs of today’s property owners and managers. And now, this solution is available directly within your iGMS portal!</span></p>
<h2><b>Benefits of the iGMS and Sure Integration:</b></h2>
<p><b>1. Predictable Repair Expenses:</b></p>
<ul>
<li aria-level="1"><span>Traditional maintenance costs can be unpredictable. Most property owners typically reserve about 1% of the property’s value or $1 per square foot annually for these expenses.</span></li>
<li aria-level="1"><span>With Sure Home Warranty, starting at just $44.99/month, you can cover all major appliances, HVAC units, and water heaters. This means you can accurately forecast and budget for future maintenance costs without any surprises.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><b>2. Trusted Service Providers at Your Fingertips:</b></p>
<ul>
<li aria-level="1"><span>Whether you need a quick repair or routine maintenance, partnering with Sure gives you access to a vast network of service providers spanning all 50 states. </span></li>
<li aria-level="1"><span>Have a trusted service contractor? Great! You can add them to the approved list of service providers.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><b>3. Zero Deductibles, Zero Hassles:</b></p>
<ul>
<li aria-level="1"><span>Forget about those hefty service fees that other warranty providers charge. With Sure Home Warranty, there’s no deductible or service fee. As long as the repair costs stay within the coverage limits for each appliance or the overall contract, you won’t have to pay a dime.</span></li>
</ul>
