<h2>My Collection Of Best Travel Tips From Experts Volume 7</h2>
<p>I’ve put together a detailed collection of some of the best 🧳 travel tips from experts. Offering some very useful tips and advice from myself and other seasoned travelers gathered over the years.</p>
<p>I thought it was time to share some of the more helpful tips I’ve learned from years of travel, and recommendations from others to pass on to you. </p>
<p>I hope these travel tips will help to make your travel experience safe, secure and as enjoyable as possible.</p>
<p>Below, is Volume 7 of my collection. Be sure to check out editions 1 to 6 as well! 🙂</p>
<h3>Some Great Travel Tips From Seasoned Travelers</h3>
<p><strong>🙂 Best Travel Tips Volume 7 – Collection 1 …</strong></p>
<p><em>Dominican Republic Beach Hut</em></p>
<p><a href="http://gr8traveltips.com/best-travel-tips-edition-7/olympus-digital-camera-50/" rel="attachment wp-att-15385"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15385" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/Dominican-Republic-Beach-Hut-1.jpg" alt="Dominican Republic Beach Hut" width="450" height="600" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/Dominican-Republic-Beach-Hut.jpg 450w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/Dominican-Republic-Beach-Hut-225x300-1.jpg 225w" sizes="(max-width: 450px) 100vw, 450px" /></a></p>
<p><em>➡   Taking a Beach Vacation? Do not leave valuable unattended on the beach when swimming;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Purchase Small Key Locks for Your Carry On Luggage to Prevent ✈ <a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/beware-of-in-flight-theft">Air Travel In Flight Theft ✈</a>;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Catching a Connecting Flight? Try to travel with Carry On Only to Prevent 🧳 <a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/lost-travel-luggage-tips/">Lost Luggage 🧳</a>;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Do Not Put Your Home Address on Your Luggage Tags. Your email and/or phone number is sufficient;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Always Wear Your Seatbelt whenever possible. Never take chances, especially when traveling to foreign destinations!</em></p>
<p> <br /><strong>🙂 Best Travel Tips Volume 7 – Collection 2 …</strong></p>
<p>Caribbean Art Work</p>
<p><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-14866" title="Tropical Colorful Painting" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/Tropical-Colorful-Painting-1.jpg" alt="Tropical Colorful Painting" width="600" height="449" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/Tropical-Colorful-Painting.jpg 675w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/Tropical-Colorful-Painting-300x224-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></p>
<p><em>➡   Need a Quiet Hotel Room?  Then request a quiet room and away from any elevators;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Store Your Liquids in Zip Lock Bags to Prevent Spillage;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Back Packers Should Consider a New Bag Protector to Prevent Theft, especially if staying in Hostels.;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Be Courteous of other Hotel Guests, try to Avoid Slamming Your Room Door and Being Loud in the Hallways;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Be Aware of the Very Popular 🕵 <a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/hotel-scam/">Hotel Room Credit Card Scam 🕵</a></em></p>

<p><em>St Maarten Tourism Office</em></p>
<p><a href="http://gr8traveltips.com/best-travel-tips-edition-7/olympus-digital-camera-51/" rel="attachment wp-att-15386"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-15386 size-full" title="St Maarten Tourism Office" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/St-Maarten-Tourism-Office-1.jpg" alt="St Maarten Tourism Office" width="600" height="449" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/St-Maarten-Tourism-Office.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/St-Maarten-Tourism-Office-300x225-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a></p>
<p><em>➡   Always Carry a Pair of Soft Earplugs When Traveling;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Keep the Television Volume at a Respectable Level in Motel/Hotel Rooms;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Carry a Compact Umbrella for Unexpected Rain;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Wash Your Hands Frequently with Soap and Hot Water to Prevent Illness;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Be sure to Carry a Pen for Completing Customs Forms and Making Notes.</em></p>
<p> <br /><strong>🙂 Best Travel Tips Volume 7 – Collection 4 …</strong></p>
<p><em>The Cosmopolitan Las Vegas Nevada</em></p>
<p><a href="http://gr8traveltips.com/best-travel-tips-edition-7/the-cosmopolitan-las-vegas-nevada/" rel="attachment wp-att-15387"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-15387 size-full" title="The Cosmopolitan Las Vegas Nevada" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/The-Cosmopolitan-Las-Vegas-Nevada-1.jpg" alt="The Cosmopolitan Las Vegas Nevada" width="600" height="450" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/The-Cosmopolitan-Las-Vegas-Nevada.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/The-Cosmopolitan-Las-Vegas-Nevada-300x225-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a></p>
<p><em>➡   Visiting New York on a Budget? Get a Seven Day Unlimited Metro Subway Pass and Save;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Keep Your Belongings Visible and Double Check When Leaving Your Room. I recently left a pair of Sunglasses in my Hotel Room in a Drawer;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Your iphone will Charger Faster in Airplane Mode Setting if in a hurry;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Do Not Leave Any Valuable Visible in Your Rental Car!  Lock it, Hide it, Keep it;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Canadians Using Credit Cards at Fuel Pumps at Service Stations.  Enter the three numerals from your postal code followed by three zeros to authorize the gas purchase.</em></p>
<p> <br /><strong>🙂 Best Travel Tips Volume 7 – Collection 5 …</strong></p>
<p><em>Okanagan Lake British Columbia</em></p>
<p><a href="http://gr8traveltips.com/best-travel-tips-edition-7/okanagan-lake-british-columbia/" rel="attachment wp-att-15388"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-15388 size-full" title="Okanagan Lake British Columbia" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/Okanagan-Lake-British-Columbia-1.jpg" alt="Okanagan Lake British Columbia" width="600" height="450" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/Okanagan-Lake-British-Columbia.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/Okanagan-Lake-British-Columbia-300x225-1.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a></p>
<p><em>➡   Visit the Local Toursim Office for your Travel Destination. They often provide free wifi and other services for visitors;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Taking a Taxi? Don’t get taxi scammed! Always ask for a price before entering the cab, especially in foreign countries;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Pedi-Cabs in New York.  Again, always ask for a price for your destination. These guys charge $5 a minute;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Never Ever Give Your Credit Card Information to Strangers on the Phone;</em></p>
<p><em>➡   Flavored Vitamin C Tablets are Great for Improving Water Taste and Your Immune System.</em></p>
