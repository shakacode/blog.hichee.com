<h2><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Beware_In_Flight_Theft_Is_Definitely_On_The_Rise"></span>Beware In Flight Theft Is Definitely On The Rise<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>
<div id="ez-toc-container" class="ez-toc-v2_0_45_1 counter-hierarchy ez-toc-counter ez-toc-light-blue ez-toc-container-direction">
<div class="ez-toc-title-container">

<nav></nav>
</div>
<p>This is something that most of us really don’t think much about when ✈ flying. Having recently researched this subject, it’s important to beware of in flight theft, that’s occurring more frequently during air travel.</p>
<p>This especially the case, on those long–haul flights on larger aircraft.</p>
<p>With the dramatic increase in 🧳 carry on luggage and people not opting to check their bags to save money, theft is on the rise as a result.</p>
<p>In all the years we have been traveling, it really wasn’t something I ever thought about.</p>
<p>It was on our last 🛫 flight, when my carry-on luggage had to be placed 7 rows back from where I was seated. The bins above my seats were already full, when I boarded the aircraft for our – 8 hour flight home.</p>
<p>It was at that time that it occurred to me, that anyone could open the bin and check out what may be inside some of my zipper compartments.</p>
<p><strong>So when I got home, I decided to start researching this topic. What I discovered was surprising!</strong></p>
<p>Theft committed on-board airplanes is definitely on the rise!</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="How_Safe_Is_Your_Carry_On_Baggage"></span>How Safe Is Your Carry On Baggage?<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airline-Crew.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-1" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airline-Crew.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-13858 size-full" title alt="Airline Crew" width="600" height="399" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airline-Crew.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airline-Crew-300x200-1.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p>Well, not very safe these days it seems!</p>
<p>As commercial airliners get larger and larger, holding more and more people, it’s very easy for your <em>🧳   <a href="https://gr8traveltips.com/carry-on-baggage-restrictions/">Carry On Luggage,</a> </em>to get away from you.</p>
<p>Just as mine did on our latest 🛫 international flight.</p>
<p>With these larger planes filled with many more people these days, it ultimately leaves the door open for most anyone, to open up your bag and have 👀 look.  </p>
<p>In most cases, without others even giving it a second thought!</p>
<p>So the moral of the story here is, if you have valuables in your 👝 bags that you do not want to lose, then 🛅 lock them up or carry them on your person.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Five_Tips_To_Prevent_Cabin_Theft"></span>Five Tips To Prevent Cabin Theft<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airplane-Theft.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-2" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airplane-Theft.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-13860 size-full" title alt="Airplane Theft" width="600" height="380" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airplane-Theft.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Airplane-Theft-300x190-1.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p><em><em>✔ Lock those bags if you have valuables you do not want to lose;</em></em></p>
<p><em>✔ Never let your Passport, Important Documents and Cash out of your site;</em></p>
<p><em>✔ Keep zippers or other baggage openings facing inside the wall of the overhead bins to prevent easy access;</em></p>
<p><em>✔ Do not leave anything valuable unattended in the seat pocket in front of you;</em></p>
<p><em>✔ If placing items on the floor make sure they are in front of you and visible.</em></p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="In_Flight_Thieves_Are_Everywhere"></span>In Flight Thieves Are Everywhere<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Large-Airplane-Cabin.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-3" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Large-Airplane-Cabin.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-13859 size-full" title alt="Beware of In Flight Theft" width="580" height="280" data-sizes="(max-width: 580px) 100vw, 580px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Large-Airplane-Cabin.jpg 580w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Large-Airplane-Cabin-300x145-1.jpg 300w" /></a><br />Here are just a few examples of recent thefts that people have been victims of on their flights.</p>
<blockquote readability="22">
<p><em>“ Traveling alone, I was seated next to another lady and had my bag with a money purse on the floor next to me. As soon as we reached cruising altitude, I went to use the washroom. When I returned and ordered a drink with dinner, my money was all gone from my change purse.”</em></p>
<p><em>“ On a recent flight, I stuffed my wallet and a few belongings in the seat pouch in front of me to have a nap. I did not know the 2 others seated beside me. After the flight, I discovered my money and credit cards missing from my wallet.”</em></p>
<p><em>“ I travel with carry on only and on this overnight flight, I had my bag and expensive jacket stowed in the overhead bin a few rows back. After the flight, it was discovered that my jacket was gone! No one had seen anything as most were sleeping during the flight.”</em></p>
</blockquote>
<p>How many times have you taken flights without even thinking that this could ever happen? ❓</p>
<p>I know we certainly have, and we’ve taken several long haul flights over the years. The next time we travel by air, we’ll certainly be more diligent regarding how we manage our 🎒 carry on bags.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Beware_of_In_Flight_Theft_-_A_Few_Things_to_Remember"></span>Beware of In Flight Theft – A Few Things to Remember<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<h3><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Flight-Attendant-Closing-Overhead-Bin.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-4" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Flight-Attendant-Closing-Overhead-Bin.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-13861 size-full" title alt="Flight Attendant Closing Overhead Bin" width="600" height="353" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Flight-Attendant-Closing-Overhead-Bin.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Flight-Attendant-Closing-Overhead-Bin-300x177-1.jpg 300w" /></a></h3>
<p>Some people mistakenly think crew members are responsible for the safe keeping of your baggage in overhead bins, or elsewhere.  </p>
<p><center><b>Wrong!!</b></center></p>
<p>It’s not the flight attendants responsibility to look after your bags anywhere, anytime!</p>
<p>Even though you are in a confined space when flying, there are amateur and professional 🕵 thieves out there, just waiting for the opportunity to strike!</p>
<p>If the opportunity presents itself, they will steal from you and it doesn’t matter how little the theft may be.  </p>
<p>I was a 👮 police officer for over 30 years and I know that people will befriend you and at the right moment, will steal from you in heart beat!</p>
<p>Always remember that person seated next to you is a stranger. Would you leave your wallet or purse unattended in a restaurant or bar, and go use the washroom for example? ❓</p>
<p>It really is no different when you’re on an ✈ airplane.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Never_Leave_Your_Valuables_Unattended"></span>Never Leave Your Valuables Unattended<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p><a href="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/air-travel-theft.jpg" data-rel="lightbox-image-5" data-rl_title data-rl_caption><img data-lazyloaded="1" src="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/air-travel-theft-600x530-1.jpg" decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-18824 size-large" title alt="air travel theft" width="600" height="530" data-sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" srcset="https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/air-travel-theft.jpg 600w, https://blog.hichee.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/air-travel-theft-300x265-1.jpg 300w" /></a></p>
<p>Never let your guard down with your valuables on an airplane, or anywhere else for that matter. Be diligent and aware of your surroundings and those around you, especially when traveling.</p>
<p>Professionals often work in pairs, even on airplanes I’ve discovered. So again, do not leave your precious valuables or items unattended or in 🔐 unlocked baggage.</p>
<p>This is especially the case when taking overnight long haul flights, when many passengers are 😴 sleeping.</p>
