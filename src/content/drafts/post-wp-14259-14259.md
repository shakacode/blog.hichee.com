<p>Sharing your Direct link via message is the quickest and easiest way to get it out to family, friends, trusted guests, and more. Messaging platforms such as WhatsApp, SMS, or any other communication platform are great ways to share your direct bookings link with ease. Not only can you quickly get your message across, but you can also streamline communication with guests by providing them with an instant response to any enquiries they may have. This helps make the booking process smoother and less complicated.</p>
