<p>This is a village in western Norway at the head of Geirangerfjord. The Norwegian Fjord Center has multimedia on the region&#8217;s history and its inhabitants. Part of the steep Trollstigen mountain road weaves through the village, connecting to Flydalsjuvet lookout, which has views over the fjord. The fjord’s waterfalls, including the Seven Sisters, the Suitor, and the Bridal Veil, are visible by boat.</p>
