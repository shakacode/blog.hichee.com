<p>What do you take on your trip? What do you need? As long-time readers know, I’m a fan of packing light. I don’t think you really need much when you travel. As a backpacker, I want to make sure everything I own fits into one bag. I often think people bring too much stuff when they travel. </p>
<p>I went to <a href="https://www.nomadicmatt.com/travel-guides/costa-rica-travel-tips/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Costa Rica</a> on my first trip overseas and I took with me the entire suggested packing list my tour company gave customers. I carried around so much stuff I never used. Years later, when I did my first backpacking trip around the world, I still carried so much, I ended up leaving stuff in hostels as I went.</p>
<p>But I also recognize everyone has different travel styles and needs. No two travelers are alike.</p>
<p>As you prepare for future trips and are wondering, “what do I really need to bring?”, I wanted to give you a list of what I view as practical and must-have items. These items won’t take up too much space, are incredibly useful, and will make your trip better. </p>
